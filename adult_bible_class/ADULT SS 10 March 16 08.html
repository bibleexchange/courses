The Earth is the lord’s

 

Text: Psalm 24:1 (Psalm 104)

 

INTRODUCTION

  1. The Supreme Being; Jehovah; the eternal and infinite Spirit, the Creator, and the Sovereign of the universe—Webster’s

 

  1. The devil cannot displace God.

The devil has not hijacked earth away from God. God is still WELL in control!

Matt. 4:8, 9; Daniel 2:21

 

  1. Man cannot remove God.

Not by: perverting His laws, blaspheming His name, usurping authority, denying Him.

Gen. 11:4, 8; Ps. 2:4; Phil. 2:11

 

  1. God’s Wisdom in its Design, 104:24

 

  1. All creation establishes the fact of design and disproves the suggestion of chance.
  • Snowflakes Job 38:22
  • Grains of Maize/Indian Corn
  • Perfect Order in Plant Leaves
  • Scientific Discovery

 

  1. God is to be worshipped.

Ps. 19:1; 104:31-35

  1. God’s Authority in its Laws, 104:5-9

 

  1. All human achievement is limited and bounded by the laws God has set; Man cannot violate these laws without consequence.
  • Gravity, Prov. 26:27
  • Seven Day Principle, Ex. 20:8-11
  • Seed Produces after its Kind, Gen. 1:11
  • Law of Thermodynamics, John 19:11

 

  1. God is to be obeyed and followed.

Deut. 11:27, 28; I Peter 3:22

 

  1. God’s Power in its Continuance, 104:29-30

 

  1. All life has its source and sustenance in God. Heb. 1:3
  • Creatures of prey are dependent upon God’s providence. Ps. 104:21
  • Whether fullness or Famine, Peace or Trouble. Amos 3:6; Matt. 11:28

 

  1. God is essential to life. Without God is nothing and by Him are all things! John 1:3

 

CONCLUSION

Devil…‘Kings’ of the Earth…Troubled Soul…Brothers and Sisters…

The earth is the LORD'S, and the fulness thereof; the world, and they that dwell therein.
