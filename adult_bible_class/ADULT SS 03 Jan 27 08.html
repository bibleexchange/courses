  1. Six reasons why God’s people need help.
  1. A lack of zeal and strength from God to do His will. v. 15
  2. God is our Father, Redeemer, and Potter and without His help we can’t do anything. 63:16; 64:8
  3. They have erred from His ways and hardened their hearts without fear. v. 17
  4. So few remain faithful servants.  v.17-18
  5. Adversaries trod down the sanctuary. v.18
  6. We are in covenant relationship; we are a people that is highly favored. v.19; Rom. 9:23-30
