Thoughts and Their Fruit

Psalm 139:1-6; Matthew 12:37

 

TEXT: Jeremiah 6:19

Hear, O earth: behold, I will bring evil upon this people, even the fruit of their thoughts, because they have not hearkened unto my words, nor to my law, but rejected it.

 

  1. Thoughts have good fruit when sown by good words or by good actions (for actions speak louder than words).

 

THOUGHTS OF:

  1. Humility

Think right about yourself. Maintain a right estimation of self.

 

  1. Forgiveness

 

  1. God’s Goodness & Greatness

 

  1. Thankfulness

 

  1. Abundance

 

  1. Submission

 

  1. Longing for Jesus

 

Cultivate thoughts like you would flowers

-- and prepare for the harvest. --

  1. Thoughts have evil fruit when sown in wickedness.

 

THOUGHTS OF:

  1. Opposition to God’s Word

 

  1. Opposition to God’s Laws

 

  1. Pride

 

  1. Self-righteousness

 

  1. Murmuring

 

  1. Unbelief

 

  1. Procrastinating, Wandering
