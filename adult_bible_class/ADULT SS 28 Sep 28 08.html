True Love is Triumphant with Service

Text: I Cor. 13:7

 

True Love Always Obeys, Thanks God and Serves.

Love conquers to triumph.

 Scriptural Ex: Rom. 8:35-39; I Cor. 15:57-58; II Cor. 2:14

 

  1. “Beareth all things”

Eph. 4:2,3; Col. 3:13; Prov. 10:12; I Peter 4:8

 

  1. To cover all things properly with silence.

To not give out, but to take on, to deal with in love and covering until conquered unto triumph.

 

  1. To endure all things patiently with suffering.

To go through to put up with, to standing against to win over.

 

  1. To forbear all things perfectly with strength.

Refrain – Abstain – Restrain - Retain the Word of God* - Obtain**

*Prov. 4:4; II Cor. 2:4; I Cor. 15:57-58

**I Cor. 9:24-25; I Thes. 1:9; II Tim. 2:10

 

  1. “Believeth all things”

Luke 17:4; Matt. 18:21,22; Eph. 4:32

 

  1. Completely Trusting God Rom. 8:28

 

  1. Always eager to believe God for the worst/lowest to have life.

 

  1. Love sees and understands the circumstances and accepts, forgives and believes the very best for a person.

 

  1. Believes to See

Ps. 27:13; I John 4:20; II Cor. 5:7; I Peter. 1:8; John 20:29; Heb. 11:1,27; Rom. 15:13

 

  1. “Hopeth all things”

Ro. 8:24; 15:4; II Thess. 2:16-17; I Jn. 3:3

 

  1. Never ceases to hope for triumph.

 

  1. Refuses to accept failure to be final.

 

  1. No matter how tragic – difficult – faulty we always hope for the ultimate triumph.

 

  1. True love always comes with obedience, thanks and faithfulness to serve.

 

  1. “Endureth all things”

Matt. 10:22; I Cor. 15:58; Gal. 6:9; Jam. 1:12; 5:11; Heb. 13:1; II Pt. 1:7

 

  1. Actively fights and stands against all attacks of the enemy without corrupting submission to God, His ways and His messengers.

 

  1. The Person (flesh and blood) is not our adversary but the object of our love. So, we will endure every attack without buckling under pressure and continue to love.

  1. Continues…Continues…Continues…

  3. Faith to Faith Rom. 1:17
  4. Grace for Grace John 1:16
  5. Light to Light Prov. 4:18
  6. Glory to Glory I Cor. 3:18
  7. Mercy to Mercy Matt. 5:7
  8. Learning Correction to Chastening to Enduring
