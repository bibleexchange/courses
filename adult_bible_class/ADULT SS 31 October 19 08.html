II Thessalonians 3:5

And the Lord direct your hearts into the love of God, and into the patient waiting for Christ.”

 

The Heart – directed into the LOVE OF GOD.

The Mind – directed into the PATIENCE OF CHRIST.

 

  1. Only the Lord can keep our hearts and minds.

 

Philippians 4:7

And the peace of God, which passeth all understanding, shall keep your hearts and minds through Christ Jesus.

 

  1. God prepares the heart and the mind

 

Psalm 10:17

  • Lord thou hast heard the desire of the Humble
  • Thou wilt prepare their heart
  • Thou will cause thine ear to hear.

Ps. 34:19; 37:23-24; 40:2; Pr. 24:16; Micah 6:8; Jude 24

  1. God hears the heart and mind that He prepares.

 

Psalm 10:17

LORD, thou hast heard the desire of the humble: thou wilt prepare their heart, thou wilt cause thine ear to hear:-ß A cry in the Heart  by Divine Preparation

 

A Cry for:

 

  • Salvation
  • Healing
  • Deliverance
  • Infilling
  • Revival
  • A Perfect Heart

 

--II Kings 20:3; Isaiah 38:3

 

  • When you think of “direction”, what is the first thing you think of?

 

  • If you allow your heart to go in a wrong direction, you have no control over the circumstances.

 

  • You have total control over a choice, but absolutely no control over the circumstances after that choice is acted on.

 

Isaiah 26:3; II Thess 3:16

 

  • Lord, direct our hearts into your love and our minds into your patience lest we make a wrong choice.

 

  • Every choice made without Christ is a WRONG choice.

 

  • Worldly affections and vain thoughts lead to wrong choices.
