Stop, Look & Listen

 

TEXT: Revelation 3:2-3; Luke 12:35-40

 

  • Preparation is necessary for every Believer.
  • Not all will prepare themselves perfectly.
  • Caution is necessary for every Believer.
  • Not all will be cautious unto perfection.
  • Faith is necessary for every Believer.
  • Not all will have perfect faith.

 

The message is to STOP, LOOK, and LISTEN.

  • Not to look and listen to see if you are going to stop or not.
  • Not to stop and go without looking and listening.

 

  1. Stop – a mind to stop what we are doing

And do only the will of God.

 

  1. Repent

  2. 2:10 Smyrna fear none of these things
  3. 2:16 Pergamos repent or else
  4. 2:22 Thyatira except they repent
  5. 3:1,3 Sardis hast a name…but dead
  6. 3:11 Philadelphia Hold fast that thou hast
  7. 3:19 Laodicea be zealous and repent

 

All need to repent. All must STOP. Be watchful and strengthen the things which remain that are ready to die.
