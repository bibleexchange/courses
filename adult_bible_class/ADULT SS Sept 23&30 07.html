SPIRIT OF HUMILITY

I Peter 5:1-11

 

TEXT:  I Peter 5:9

 

I. It is the spirit that recognizes and resists the devourer by faith.

 

    1. The adversary devours through the intellect and the soulish realm.

 

      1. A humble spirit keeps the mind operating by faith.
        1. The mind is the devil’s workshop.
        2. The mind is the devil’s battleground. Eph. 6:10-13
        3. The mind must be renewed. Rom. 12:2

 

      1. A proud spirit takes counsel from the mind and acts upon it.

 

      1. A humble spirit resists the devourer.
        1. The devil
        2. The Flesh
        3. The World
        4. Contrary Winds
          1. False Doctrine
          2. Opposition
          3. Affliction
          4. Tests or Temptations

 

IN THE TEST—Humble yourself under the mighty hand of God.

IN THE TEMPTATION—Resist the devil steadfast in the faith.

 

The Problem is never with the Word of God but in the mind of the individual.
