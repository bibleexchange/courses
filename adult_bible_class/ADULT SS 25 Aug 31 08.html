Love Champions the Cause

 

Text: I Cor. 8:13

 

What Caused it?

 

  • The cause is what makes something happen.

 

  • The phrase: ‘Love will champion the cause’ is equal to the phrase: ‘Love will make it happen.’

 

  • If love is not the cause of something happening, then the results will always prove to be disastrous.
