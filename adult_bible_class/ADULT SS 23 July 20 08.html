Precepts of the Christian Life

Romans 12

 

  1. The First Precept v.1

 

  1. Every man must consecrate himself wholly to the service of God

 

  1. It is our reasonable service.

 

  1. present our bodies a living sacrifice

 

  1. Holy – holy lives given for service

 

  1. Acceptable unto God –

 

  1.  Must have life in it (alive) a LIVING sacrifice

 

  1. Good/Right/Excellent Spirit

Prov. 17:27; Dan. 5:12, 14; 6:3;

Rom. 12:11

 

– A sacrifice without a right spirit is NOT a reasonable service –

 

  1. It is only by the mercies of God

 

  1. That we can have an excellent spirit.

 

  1. That we can sacrifice and do service with an excellent spirit.

 

  1. The Second Precept v.2

 

  1. Every man must follow only the will of God.

 

  1. It is God’s will that we be not conformed to this world.

 

  1. No room left for man’s reasoning.

 

  1. No room left for worldly counsel.

 

  1. No room left for man’s free will.

Eph. 1:18; 2:3; 4:17; Col.1:21

 

  1. It is the will of God that we be transformed by the renewing of our mind.

 

  1. Having a will to do only His will.

Rom. 12:2; John 7:17

 

  1. Having a mind only for the will of God.

 

  1. The Word of God

 

  1. The Glory of God

 

  1. The Spirit of God

 

  1. It is the will of God that we overcome.

Rom. 12:21

 

  1. Overcome by Loving –NOT an option

Rom. 12:9; John 13:34,35; 15:12,17; I Thess. 4:9; I Pt. 1:22; I John 3:1

 

  1. Overcome by Forgiving

Matt. 6:12, 14-15; 18:21, 35; Mark 11:25-26; Luke 6:37; 11:4; 17:3-4; 23:34; I John 1:9; Ps. 25:18; 86:5

 

  1. Operate by Grace through Faith

 

  1. The Third Precept v.3

 

  1. Think with a sound mind only what God is saying.

 

  1. Remain where God calls you.

  1. The Fourth Precept vs. 4-21

 

We should be working together:

 

  1. In the office that God designs.

 

  1. In the gifts that God delivers.

 

  1. In the grace that God dispenses.

 

  1. In the Spirit that God distributes.
