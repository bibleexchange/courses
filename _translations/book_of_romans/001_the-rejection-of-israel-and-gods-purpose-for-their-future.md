### Upofu ulikuwa kwa kusudi na sio kitu cha kudumu mstr11-21The Blinding is Purposeful and Does not Have to be Permanent, vs. 11-21

 11 Nasema tena,wamejikwaa hata wakaanguka? God forbid: but rather through their fall salvation is come unto the Gentiles, for to provoke them to jealousy.
 12 Sasa ikiwa kuanguka kwao imekuwa utajiri kwa ulimwengu,na upungufu wao ni utajiri wa mataifa;Je itakuwaje zaidi udanganyifu waoNow if the fall of them be the riches of the world, and the diminishing of them the riches of the Gentiles; how much more their fulness?
 13 Kwa maana naongea na nyinyi wa mataifa,kwa kadri nilivyo mtume wa mataifa nimetambua ofisi yangu:For I speak to you Gentiles, inasmuch as I am the apostle of the Gentiles, I magnify mine office:

 15 Kwa maana kutupwa kwao kumekuwa kupatanishwa kwa Ulimwengu,ni kitu gani watakipokea,kama sio uzima kutoka kifo?For if the casting away of them be the reconciling of the world, what shall the receiving of them be, but life from the dead?
 16 Kwa maana kama limbuko limekuwa takatifu,na bunda pia limekuwa takatifu: na kama shina limekuwa takatifu basi na matawi.For if the firstfruit be holy, the lump is also holy: and if the root be holy, so are the branches.
 17 Na kama baadhi ya matawi yameondolewa,na nyinyi kuwa mzabibu mwitu,ule wa pili umechorwa miongoni mwenu na pamoja nao tumeshiriki shina na ukuu wa mzeituni;And if some of the branches be broken off, and thou, being a wild olive tree, wert graffed in among them, and with them partakest of the root and fatness of the olive tree;
 18 Msijisifie matawi.Bali kama mtajisifia msirishikishe na shina,bali ninyi ni shina.Boast not against the branches. But if thou boast, thou bearest not the root, but the root thee.
 19 Na mtasema baadaye,matawi yameshaondolewa,iliniweze kuingizwa ndani yake.Thou wilt say then, The branches were broken off, that I might be graffed in.
