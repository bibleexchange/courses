# Matthew

# Mathayo

## INTRODUCTION TO THE GOSPEL ACCORDING TO MATTHEW

## UTANGULIZI WA GOSPEL KATIKA MATTHEW

### Description of this Book

### Maelezo ya Kitabu hiki

Matthew links the Old and New Testament. In the Old Testament we find Two great covenants:

Mathayo inaunganisha Agano la Kale na Jipya. Katika Agano la Kale tunapata maagano mawili makubwa: 

1. Covenant with David - II Sam. 7:8-13, 16 (A KING)
2. Covenant with Abraham - Gen. 22:8 (A SACRIFICE)

1. Agano na Daudi - II Sam. 7:8-13, 16 (Mfalme)
2. Agano na Ibrahimu - Mwanzo 22:8 (Dhabihu)

Matthew opens with the birth of a king, and closes with the offering of a sacrifice.

Mathayo huanza na kuzaliwa kwa mfalme, na kuishia na sadaka ya dhabihu.

In the book of Matthew we find The King's:

Katika kitabu cha Mathayo tunapata mambo kuhusu Mfalme:

- Geneology (From Abraham to Josep)
- Birthplace (Bethlehem)
- Forerunner (John the Baptist)
- Ministry
- Rejection by Israel
- Promise of Coming Again in Power and Glory

- Historia ya familia (Kutoka kwa Ibrahimu hadi Yosefu)
- Mahali pa kuzaliwa (Bethlehem)
- Mtangulizi (Yohana Mbatizaji)
- Huduma
- Kukataliwa na Israeli
- Ahadi ya Kuja tena kwa Nguvu na Utukufu

Matthew was wise in not alienating the Jews. He showed them that this Jesus fulfilled every prophecy spoken concerning their Messiah. Matthew uses over 25 quotes from the Old Testament. Many times he talks about "that it might be fulfilled which was spoken by the prophet". 16 times Matthew uses the word "fulfilled."

Mathayo alitumia hekima kwa kuhusisha Wayahudi. Aliwaonyesha kwamba Yesu huyu alitimiza unabii wote uliosema kuhusu Masihi wao. Mathayo hutumia nukuu zaidi ya 25 kutoka Agano la Kale. Mara nyingi anazungumzia juu ya "ili iweze kukamilika ambayo yalisemwa na nabii." Mara kumi na sita Mathayo anatumia neno "kutimiza."

A Jew tends to feel that he must give up his tradition and orthodox and accept another creed. Matthew in this book and Paul in Galatians, show the Jewish Christians that they were not giving up their old faith, but rather were only giving up types and shadows for the real substance.

Myahudi hujisikia kwamba lazima aache utamaduni wake na kukubali imani nyingine. Mathayo katika kitabu hiki na Paulo katika Wagalatia, inaonyesha Wakristo Wayahudi kwamba hawakuacha imani yao ya zamani, lakini badala yake walikuwa wakitoa tu aina na vivuli kwa dutu halisi.

Matthew is the book of fulfillment.

Mathayo ni kitabu cha kutimiza.

Matthew used his knowledge of Jewish history and customs. He speaks of farming, fishing, and the housekeeping of his people to touch their hearts.

Mathayo alitumia ujuzi wake juu ya historia ya Kiyahudi na desturi. Anasema kuhusu kilimo, uvuvi, na kuhifadhi nyumba ya watu wake kugusa mioyo yao.

Matthew presents our Lord in a distinctly Jewish relationship.

Mathayo inaonyesha Bwana wetu kwa njia ya Kiyahudi.

Only in Matthew do we find a record of the Messiah saying "I am not sent but unto the lost sheep of Israel" (Matt. 15:24).

Tu katika Mathayo tunapata rekodi ya Masihi akisema "Sikutumwa ila kwa kondoo waliopotea wa nyumba ya Israeli" (Mat. 15:24).

What did his own people do with Him? John 1:11

Watu wake walifanya nini naye? (Yoh. 1:11)

### Author of this Book

### Mwandishi wa Kitabu hiki

The Apostle Matthew is the author of this book. The name "Matthew" means "gift of God". The Disciple Matthew had been a tax collector before Jesus called him. This disciple was also known by the name "Levi". Matthew calls himself a publican, which was a reproach (perhaps something like calling someone a politician today).

Mtume Mathayo ndiye mwandishi wa kitabu hiki. Jina "Mathayo" linamaanisha "zawadi ya Mungu." Mathayo mwanafunzi alikuwa mtoza ushuru kabla ya Yesu kumwita. Mwanafunzi huyo pia alijulikana kwa jina la "Lawi." Mathayo anajiita kuwa mtoza, ambayo ilikuwa ni aibu.

Other evangelists tell about the great feast he gave Jesus. They also record the significant fact that he left all and followed Jesus. No doubt he was a man of means.

Wainjilisti wengine wanasema juu ya karamu kubwa Mathayo alimpa Yesu. Pia wanaandika ukweli muhimu kwamba yeye alitoka wote na akamfuata Yesu. Hapakuwa na shaka alikuwa mtu wa utajiri.

### Position of This Book

### Nafasi ya Kitabu hiki

#### Breaks the 400 Years of silence between Malachi's prophecy and the announcement of the birth of Jesus.

#### Inavunja miaka 400 ya kimya kati ya unabii wa Malaki na kutangazwa kwa kuzaliwa kwa Yesu.

1. Israel was under the domination of the Roman Empire.
2. No man of the house of David had been allowed to sit upon the throne for over 600 years.

1. Israeli ilikuwa chini ya utawala wa Dola ya Kirumi.
2. Hakuna mtu wa nyumba ya Daudi aliyeruhusiwa kukaa juu ya kiti cha enzi kwa zaidi ya miaka 600.

#### Matthew follows after the Old Testament and is the beginning of the New Testament.

#### Mathayo hufuata baada ya Agano la Kale na ni mwanzo wa Agano Jipya.

1. It is the connecting link between the old covenant and the new covenant.
2. It is written for the Jew.
3. The Old Testament closed with the chosen nation looking for their long-promised king (their Messiah).
4. The New Testament begins with the appearance of this king.

1. Ni kiungo cha kuunganisha kati ya agano la kale na agano jipya.
2. Imeandikwa kwa Myahudi.
3. Agano la Kale lilimaliza na taifa lililochaguliwa likitafuta mfalme wao aliyepangwa kwa muda mrefu (Masihi wao).
4. Agano Jipya huanza na kuonekana kwa mfalme huyu.

#### Numerically

#### Mahali kwa namba

1. Matthew is the 40th book of the Bible. (39 books in the OT before Matthew.)
2. The number 40 means testing or probation
3. Jesus fasted 40 days and then was tempted of the devil
4. Israel was in the wilderness 40 years
5. Moses was in a palace for 40 years and then on the backside of the desert 40 years
6. In this 40th book, Israel is in the place of testing

1. Mathayo ni kitabu cha 40 cha Biblia. (Kuna vitabu 39 katika Agano la Kale kabla ya Mathayo.)
2. Nambari 40 ina maana ya kupima au kutathmini
3. Jesus fasted 40 days and then was tempted of the devil
4. Israeli alikuwa jangwani miaka 40
5. Musa alikuwa katika nyumba ya mfalme kwa miaka 40 na kisha jangwani miaka 40
6. Katika kitabu hiki cha 40, Israeli iko mahali pa kupima

To the Jews a stumbling block (I Cor. 1:23).

Kwa Wayahudi Yesu alikuwa kizuizi (I Kor. 1:23).

Christ is presented as King to the Jews, and they reject Him not only as their Messiah, but as their Savior (Mat. 16:21).

Kristo anaonyeshwa kama Mfalme kwa Wayahudi, na wanamkataa sio tu kama Masihi wao, bali kama Mwokozi wao (Mat. 16:21).
