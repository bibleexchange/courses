Psalm 34:3 O magnify the LORD with me, and let us exalt his name together.

As the Lord towers over all our problems and troubles we can look up and praise Him while we are in awe of Him rather than troubled.

 

  1. This is a humble, fearful request; for the humble and the fearful to worship the Lord together and not be separated from each other or from His greatness.

 

  1. The humble, hear this praise, not a complaint or a correction and be glad.

v.2 “the humble shall hear thereof, and be glad”

 

 

 

  1. None can make God great but we can tell of His greatness as we see Him.

 

  1. None can show or reveal His greatness, but we can talk of it and He will use our testimony and worship for His honor and His Glory.
