INTRODUCTION: Psalm 65—àA Hymn of Harvest, A Psalm of Thanksgiving

Inspired for the Ocassion for Prayer, Commitment to vows, thanksgiving and rejoicing.

Prayer for God to break the boundries of the Gentiles to be called and chosen to the Gospel and the benefits which flow from it. The Psalmist prays particularly that God would continue his former kindness to the Jewish people in Sion.
