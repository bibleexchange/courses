 2 Yea, in heart ye work wickedness; ye weigh the violence of your hands in the earth.

Law was given that sin might appear-might be set forth in its own colours; when we saw it subjected us to death by a law perfectly holy, just, and good; that sin, by the law, might be represented what it really is:

 an EXCEEDING GREAT and deadly evil.
