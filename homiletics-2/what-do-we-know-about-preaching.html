What do we know about preaching? Tunajua nini kuhusu kuhubiri?

In the Bible, we find the pattern for Christian preaching. Following is a list of some of what we know about preaching: Katika Biblia, tunaona mfano kwa mahubiri ya Kikristo. Ifuatayo ni orodha ya mambo ambayo tunajua kuhusu kuhubiri:

  1. Teaching and Preaching are distinct but go hand in hand. Matt. 11:1 Kufundisha na kuhubiri ni tofauti lakini nenda nayo vyote. Mat. 11:1
  2. John the Baptist preached the baptism of repentance for the remission of sins. Mark 1:4 Yohana mpatizaji alihubiri ubatizo wa toba kwa ajili ya ondoleo la dhambi. Mark 1:4
  3. A major reason Christ came was to preach. Mark 1:38 Sababu kubwa kristo alikuja kuhubiri Mar 1:38
  4. The preacher must be SENT. Mark 3:14; Romans 10:15 Mhubiri lazima awe AMETUMWA. Mariko 3:14; Warumi 10:15
  5. The Gospel must be preached EVERYWHERE to EVERYONE without exception. Mr 16:15; Rom. 1:15; 2 Cor. 10:16; Gal. 1:16; Re14:6; Matt. 10:7 Injili lazima ihubiriwe KILAMAHALI kwa KILA MTU pasipo udhuru. Mar 16:15; Rom. 1:15; 2 Cor. 10:16; Gal. 1:16; Uf14:6; Mat. 10:7
  6. Where and what we preached must be inspired by the Holy Ghost. Acts 16:6,10; I Cor. 1:17; 2Co 2:12; Gal. 2:2; Jonah 3:2; Isa. 61:1 Popote na chochote tunacho hubiri lazima tuvuviwe na Roho Mtakatifu. Mdo 16:6-10; I kor. 1:17; 2kor 2:12; Gal. 2:2; Yona 3:2; Isa. 61:1
  7. Christ must be preached. Acts 17:3; I Cor. 1:23; 2 Cor. 4:5; Phil. 1:15,16 Kristo lazima ahubiriwe . Mdo 17:3; I kor. 1:23; 2 kor. 4:5; Waflp. 1:15-16
  8. We must preach the Word. Romans 10:8; 2 Tim. 4:2 Lazima tuhubiri neno. Warumi 10:8; 2 Tim. 4:2
  9. They that preach the Gospel should live of the Gospel. I Cor. 9:14 Wale wahubirio Injili waishi kwa Injli. I kor. 9:14
  10. The preacher's duty and burden is to preach. I Cor. 9:16; Eph. 3:8 Kazi ya mhubiri na mzigo ni kuhubiri. I kor. 9:16; Waef. 3:8
  11. The Gospel is to be free. I Cor. 9:18 Injili ni ya kutolewa bure. 1 kor.9:18
  12. The preacher preaches and leaves the results and glory to God. I Cor. 15:11 Mhubiri huhubiri na kuacha matokeo na utukufu kwa Mungu. I kor. 15:11
  13. The preacher must preach TRUTH. Gal. 1:8,9 Mhubiri lazima ahubiri UKWELI. Gal. 1:8-9
  14. The goal of preaching is EDIFICATION and CHANGED LIVES. Col. 1:28; Luke 4:18; 9:60; Ac 5:42; 10:42; 14:15; 15:21; Romans 15:20 lengo la kuhubiri ni MALEZI na maisha ya mabadiliko. Wakol. 1:28; Luka 4:18; 9:60; Mdo 5:42; 10:42; 14:15; 15:21; Warumi 15:20
