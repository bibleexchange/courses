The Unity of Biblical Sermons (Having one theme) Umoja wa hotuba za kiBiblia (kuwa na dhamira moja)

Effective Communication of the Gospel demands a single theme. Mawasiliano ya Injili yenye mafanikio yanahitaji dhamira moja.

"What is the "theme"? " " "Dhamira"ni nini? "

The theme refers to the main subject of the sermon. It is the main thought or idea that is to be communicated to the people. Dhamira inahusika na somo kuu la hotuba. Niwazo kuu au dhana imewasilishwa kwa watu.

The subject will answer the question:"What am I talking about?" Somo litajibu swali:"Nina ongea juu ya nini?"

The theme or subject is simply what the sermon is going to be about. What is the message that God has for the people? This one message or subject is what is called the theme. Dhamira au jambo ni mfano wa kile ambacho hotuba inaenda kuwa Ujumbe gani ambao Mungu anayo kwa ajili ya watu? ujumbe huu moja au jambo moja ndio huitwa dhamira.

The subject of a sermon should never be only one word. Somo la hotuba lisinge kuwa neno moja tu.

Single words are too vague to form a complete and concise subject. Single words like worship, faith and love are too broad for a sermon subject. The subject needs to be narrowed down to form a complete thought. For example, there are so many aspects of love that could be preached in a sermon. You need to know what part of love you intend to preach for one sermon. The theme should narrowly define the subject to be preached. Maeneno matupu ya mazoea hayafai kwa muundo wa ukamilifu na somo fupi.Maneno pekee kama kuabudu, imani na upendo yanafaa sana kwa ajili yasomo la hotuba.Somo linahitaji kuelekezwa chini kuunda wazo kamili.Kwa mfano, kuna tabia nyigi za upendo ambazo zinaweza kuhubiriwa katika hotuba.Unayopaswa kujua aina gani ya upendo unategemea kuhubiri kwa ajili ya hotuba moja.Dhamira ingefafanua kwa bidii somo litakalo hubiriwa.

Remember a rule: the text naturally suggests the theme. The theme should be drawn from the text that is to be preached. The Word should always be the foundation for the sermon. Kumbuka kanuni:kifungu kwa asili kipendekeze dhamira. Dhamira lazima iletwe kutoka kwenye kifungu ambacho kitaenda kuhubiriwa.Neno sikuzote liwe msingi kwa ajili ya hotuba.

"What is the "thesis statement"? " "maelezo ya maneno" ni nini?

This term is used to refer to the one complete sentence that will define the subject to be preached. It is the entire sermon described in one complete sentence. Mhula huu unatumika kurejelea sentesi moja kamili ambayo ita fafanua somo litakalo hubiriwa.nihotuba ya kuingilia ilioandikwa katika sentesi moj kamili.

"The thesis statement completes the subject. " "Maelezo ya maneno hukamilisha somo. "

A subject can not stand alone. A subject needs a complement to be complete. A complete sentence needs both a subject and a predicate. The thesis statement will add a predicate to the subject. You need to know what you are going to say about your subject. Somo haliwezi kusimama lenyewe.somo linahitaji ukamilifu wa kukamilisha.sentesi kamili huhitaji yote mawili somo na habari.maelezo ya maneno yata ongeza habari fulani kwenye somo. Unapaswa kujua kile unacho enda kusema juu ya somo lako.

The subject answers the question: What am I going to say? Somo hujibu swali:Nini ninachoenda kusema?

The thesis statement answers the question: "What am I going to say, about what I am going to say?" Maelezo ya maneno hujibu swali: "Nini ninachoenda kuhubiri?"
