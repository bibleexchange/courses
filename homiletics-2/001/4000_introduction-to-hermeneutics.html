Meaning of Hermeneutics Maana ya vizibuo

Webster's dictionary defines hermeneutics as: noun. The art of finding the meaning of an author's words, and of explaining it to others. Dikishenari ya Webster hufafanua vizibuo kama:jina.sanaa ya kupata maana ya maneno ya mwandishi,ya kuielezea kwa wengine.

Biblical Hermeneutics is the study of Biblical interpretation. Biblical hermeneutics is simply the processof correctly interpreting the Scriptures. Vizibuo vya kibiblia ni kujifunza ufafanuzi wa kiBiblia. Vizibuo vya kibiblia ni kufanya harakati za kufafanua maandiko kwa usahihi.

Hermeneutics deals with issues such as: Vizibuo hushugulika na maswala kama vile:

What is the 'formal' interpretation of this text? Ni nini ufafanuzi wa kawaida wa kifungu hiki?

What is the 'official' interpretation of this text? Ni nini ufafanuzi" rasmi" wa kifungu hiki?

What did the author intend to say? Ni nini ambacho mwandishi anakusudia kusema?

What message did the author intend to convey? Ujumbe gani ambao mwandishi anakusudia kuuwasilisha?

Is the use of a particular word, grammatical construction, verb tense, et, significant in this instance? Je ni matumizi ya neno maalum,Uundaji wa kisarufi,wakati wa tendo,herufi, umuhimu katika mfano huu?

Who were the author's readers or listeners, culturally, et? Nani waliokuwa wasomaji au wasikilizaji wa mwandishi,kwakupitia herufi?

How was the text interpreted by the author's contemporaries? Jinsi gani kifungu kimefasiriwa na watu wa siku za mwandishi?

Source of Hermeneutics chanzo cha vizibuo

In interpreting the Bible a number of Principles of Textual Interpretation apply. Katika kufasiri Biblia namba ya kanuni za ufasiri wa kifungu huhitajika.

Most of these principles were not developed by biblical scholars. Hizi kanuni hazikuendelezwa na wasomi wa kiBiblia.

Most of these principles were not developed specifically for Bible study or interpretation. Hizi kanuni hazikuendelezwa mahususi kwa ajili ya kujifunza Biblia au kufasiri.

Most of these same principles are used by courts, historians, literary scholars, editors, news reporters and academics. Kanuni zilezile hutumiwa na wanasheria, wanahisitoria,wasomi wa simlizi,wanamahesabu, waandishi wa habari,na wanataaluma.

The same principles are used when examining texts that have nothing to do with religion, ethics, morality, etc. Kanuni zilezile hutumika wakati wa kupima vifungu hiyo haina cha kufanya pamoja na dini,mifumo ya maadili,unyoofu,nakadhalika.

The rules are based on logic, experience, and common sense, not religious beliefs. Kanuni zilijengwa juu ya habari,uzoefu,na akili ya kawaida,sio imani za kidini.

This is not a formal set of rules adopted by some Society or Association. It is an informal list of the various principles generally use Huu sio mpango wa muundo wa kanuni zilizo pangwa na baadhi ya jamii.Ni orodha isio rasmi ya matumizi kwa ujumla ya kanuni mbali mbali
