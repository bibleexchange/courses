Organization of Biblical Sermons Mpangilio wa hotuba za kiBiblia

Peter's Sermon on the Day of Pentecost, Acts 2:14–41 Hotuba ya Petro siku ya Petecost, Mdo 2:14-41

Note that Peter's sermon had a textual foundation. Nukuu kile ambacho kilikuwa msingi wa kifungu cha hotuba ya Petro.

TEXT: Vs. 17–21 (Quotation from Joel) Joel 2:28–32 KIFUNGU: Mstr. 17–21 (Nukuu toka Yoeli) Yoel 2:28–32

TITLE:Jesus is the Messiah KICHWA CHA UJUMBE:Yesu ni Masiha

INTRODUCTION: UTANGULIZI:

"Vs. 14—16 He called the attention of the people ." Mstr. 14—16 Yeye aliita usikivu wa watu.

JESUS FULFILLED GOD'S PLAN (Vs.22–24) YESU ALITIMIZA MPANGO WA MUNGU (Mstr.22—24)

Jesus was approved of God Yesu alikuwa pendekezo la Mungu

By miracles, wonders and signs Kwa miujiza, maajabu na ishara

The work of God Kazi ya Mungu

The crucifixion was according to God's plan. Kusulubiwa ilikuwa sawa na mpango wa Mungu.

The resurrection was fulfilled according to prophecy. Ufufuo ulikuwa umetimizwa kulingana na unabii.

According to the foreknowledge of God Kulingana na maarifa ya Mungu

JESUS FULFILLED DAVID'S PROPHECY (Vs. 25–31) YESU ALITIZA UNABII WA DAUDI (Mstrs. 25—31)

"David was a prophet of God. " "Daudi alikua nabii wa Mungu. "

Christ to sit on David's throne. Kristo kukaa kwenye kiti cha enzi cha Daudi.

Resurrection of Christ was foretold by David. Ufufuo wa kristo ulitabiriwa na Daudi.

"JESUS FULFILLED THE PLAN OF GOD AS THE EXALTED CHRIST(Vs. 32–36) " "KRISTO ALITIMIZA MPANGO WA MUNGU KAMA KRISTO MTUKUKA(Mstr. 32–36) "

This Jesus hath God raised up. Huyu Yesu Mungu alimfufua.

Jesus is exalted on the Right hand of God. Yesu alitukuzwa kwenye mkono wa kuume wa Mungu.

Jesus is Lord and Christ. Yesu ni Bwana na kristo.

"CONCLUSION: vs. 37–40 " "HITIMISHO: Mstr. 37–40 "

"Results pricked in their heart (conviction) " "Matokeo ya lichoma ndani ya mioyo yao (thibitisho la hatia ya kosa) "

Application call to repent and be baptized Haja yakuita kutubu na kubatizwa

Many responded— 3000 souls saved Wengi waliitikia watu— 3000 waliokoka
