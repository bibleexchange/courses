PRINCIPLE ELEVEN: We must be taught by the Holy Spirit. He is the Master Teacher! KANUNI YA KUMI NA MOJA: Lazima tufundishwe na Roho mtakatifu.Yeye ni mwalimu mkuu!

I Cor. 2:13–14—Which things also we speak,not in the words which man's wisdom teacheth, but which the Holy Ghostteacheth; comparing spiritual things with spiritual. But the natural manreceiveth not the things of the Spirit of God: for they are foolishness untohim: neither can he know them, because they are spiritually discerned. I Wakr. 2:13—14—Nayo twayanena,sio yaliyo fundishwa katika hekima ya kibinadamu bali ni yale yanayo fundishwa na Roho mtakatifu; linganisha vitu vya kiroho kwa vitu vya kiroho.Basi mwanadamu wa tabia ya asili hawezi kupokea mambo ya Roho wa Mungu: maana kwao ni upuzi:wala hawezi kuyajua maana ya tambulikana kwa jinsi ya Roho.

Be careful of the words which men's wisdom teacheth. Jihadhari na maneno ambayo hufundishwa kwa hekima za watu.

Commentaries can be helpful especially to learn about culture and history, but they are nosubstitute for the teaching of the Holy Ghost. In fact many of the commentators that are held so dearly did not believe in the Baptism of the HolyGhost. Maoni ya weza kufaa hasahasa kwa kujifunza siasa na historia,bali sio mbadala wa mafundisho ya Roho mtakatifu.Kwahakika wengi wa washikilia maoni ambao ni wale hawaamini juu ya Ubatizo wa Roho mtakatifu.

Weigh everything against the Holy Ghost inspired Word of God. Pima kila kitu kilicho kinyume cha Roho mtakatifu na neno la Mungu lililo vuviwa.

Correct interpretation will always be in agreement with the rest of the Bible. Ufasiri sahihi siku zote utakuwa na mapatano na msimamo wa Biblia.

The Holy Ghost will teach you. Roho atakufundisha.

John 14:26—But the Comforter, which is the Holy Ghost, whom the Father willsend in my name, he shall teach you all things, and bring all things to your remembrance, whatsoever I have said unto you. Yohn 14:26—Lakini Mfariji,ambaye ni Roho Mtakatifu,ambaye Baba atamtuma kwa jina langu,atawafundisha yote,nakuwakumbusha yote,niliyo kwisha kuwaambia.
John 16:13—Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide you into all truth: for he shall not speak of himself; but whatsoever he shall hear, that shall he speak: and he will shew you things to come. Yohn 16:13—Lakini yeye,Roho wa kweli, atakapokuja ,atawaongoza awatie kwenye kweli yote:maana hatasema kwa shauri lake mwenyewe;bali chochote atakachosikia,hicho ndicho atakacho sema:na atawaonyesha mambo yajayo.

This is a promise of God. Hii ni ahadi ya Mungu.

Pray for understanding of the Word you are studying. Omba kwa ajili ya neno unalo jifunza.

God is faithful to reveal His Word to you. Mungu ni mwaminifu atakufunulia neno lake.

Stand on the promise of the Word to be taught by the Holy Spirit. Simama kwenye ahadi ya neno kwa kufundishwa na Roho Mtakatifu.

Meditate on the Word of God. Tafakari neno la Mungu.

Joshua 1:8 This book of the law shall not depart out of thy mouth; but thoushalt meditate therein day and night, that thou mayest observe to do accordingto all that is written therein: for then thou shalt make thy way prosperous,and then thou shalt have good success.
