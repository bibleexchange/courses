MEDITATING ON GOD'SWORD SWAHILI

WHAT DOES IT MEAN TO MEDITATE ON GOD'S WORD? SWAHILI

HEBREW: 07742. suwachto muse pensively:—meditate & 0189"hagah" to murmur (inpleasure or anger); by implication, to ponder:—imagine, meditate, mourn,mutter, roar, X sore, speak, study, talk, utter. SWAHILI

GREEK: 3191. "meletao" totake care of, (by implication) revolve in the mind:——imagine,(pre–)meditate. SWAHILI

DICTIONARY: to keep the mind fixed upon in a state of contemplation; to dwell on anything in thought;to think seriously; to muse; to reflect; SWAHILI

SYNONYMS: consider;ponder; weigh; revolve; study SWAHILI

SCRIPTURES: Gen. 24:63; Jos 1:8; Ps 1:2; 4:4; 19:14; 39:3; 49:3; 63:5,6; 73:12—22;77:10—12; 104:34; 119:11,15,16,23,48,55,59,78,97—99,148; 139:17,18; 143:5; 1Ti4:13—15 SWAHILI
