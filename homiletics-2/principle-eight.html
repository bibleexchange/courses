Scripture often has a near and far application. Relating to a soon coming event, also to be fulfilled at a latter time events in the End Time. Passages written to Israel, can have valueable meaning to the present day Church. Vifungu walioandikiwa Waisrael,vya weza kuwa na maana yenye thamani kuwakilisha kanisa la leo.

In exegesis we try to expose the 1)Original Meaning of the text; while in, 2)Personal Application we seek to find significance in the text for us today. Katika mukitadha wenyewe tunajaribu kufunua 1)Maana ya asili ya kifungu;wakati katika,2)Matumizi binafisi tunatafuta kupata umuhimu katika kifungu kwa ajili yetu leo.

These two endeavors together create a spiral of interpretation called the "Hermeneutical spiral" (Grant R. Osborne) Juhudi hizi mbili kwa pamoja hutengeneza mzunguko wa kufasiri unaoitwa vizibuo mzunguko"(Grant R. Osborne)

Although an event recorded in Scripture happened at a definite time in history passed, the principles of its passages are timeless and personally relevant. Ingawa tukio lililo andikwa katika maandiko lilitokea wakati wa mwanzo katika historia iliopita,kanuni za vifungu vyake havina muda na uhusiano binafsi.

"The relationship between meaning and significance summarizes the hermeneutical task. The preacher must ask how the biblical writer would have applied the theological truths of the passage if he were addressing them to the modern congregation."
