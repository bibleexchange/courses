What is the definition of Homiletics? Ufafanuzi wa elimu ya mahubiri ni nini?

Homiletics is The science or art of religious discourse or preaching. Elimu ya mahubiri ni sayansi au sanaa ya mazungumzo ya kidini au mahubiri.

What is the definition of Preaching? Ufafanuzi wa mahubiri ni nini?

The spoken communication of Divine truth with a view to persuasion. Nimawasiliano ya mazungumzo ya ukweli wa kiungu pamojo na mtazamo wa kushawishi.

What is the thesis statement? Maelezo ya maneno ni nini?

The thesis statement is the one complete sentence that will define the subject to be preached. Maelezo ya maneno ni sentesi ambayo ita fafanua jambo ambalo litaenda kuhubiriwa.
