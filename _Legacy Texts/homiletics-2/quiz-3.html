What is the source of Christian teaching? Ni nini chanzo cha mafundisho ya kikristo?

Remember that Scripture is the source of Christian teaching. Kumbuka maandiko matakatifu ndio chanzo cha mafundisho ya kikristo.

Outlining will be your primary means of what? Kutoa dondoo itakua ndio msingi wako kumaanisha nini?

Outlining will be your primary means of producing quality, Bible–centered lessons, talks, and sermons. Kutoa dondoo itakuwa msingi wako kumaanisha ubora wa uzalishaji,masomo ya katika ya Biblia, ni mazungumzo,na hotuba.

List the three kinds of sermons and give a brief description of each one. Orodhesha aina tatu za hotuba na utoe maelezo mafupi ya kila moja wapo.

Expository sermons are those in which the subject, maindivisions and most of the details are derived from the text. Textual sermons are those in which the subject and main divisions are derived from the text. Topical sermons are those in which the subject alone is derived from the text. What is a text without context? Ni nini kifungu pasipo mazingira?

A text without a context is just a pretext. Kifungu pasipo mazingira ni kama hoja tu.

What is the chief difference in textal sermons from topical sermons? Ni nini tofauti kubwa katika hotuba za kifungu kutoka hotuba za kimada?

The chief difference in textual sermon and topical sermons is the source of the divisions. Tofauti kubwa katika hotuba ya kifungu na hotuba za kimada ni chanzo cha migawanyo.

What is the source of the divisions in a textual sermon? Ni nini chanzo cha migawanyo katika hotuba ya kifungu?

The Bible supplies divisions from the text chosen. Biblia hutoa migawanyo katika kifungu kilicho chaguliwa.

What is better than a man who attempts to find texts? Ni nini ambayo ni bora kuliko mtu kujaribu kuvipata vifungu?

It is far better for one to put himself in the way of texts finding him, than for him to attempt to find texts. Nibora mtu kujiweka katika njia ya vifungu, kumpata kuliko yeye kujaribu kuvipata vifungu.

What should naturally suggest the theme of a topical sermon? Ni nini ambayo kiasili ingependekeza dhamira ya hotuba ya kimada?

The text should naturally suggest the theme. Kifungu kiasili lazima kipendekeze dhamira.
