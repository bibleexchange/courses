What is"Hermeneutics"? "Vizibuo" ni nini?

Biblical hermeneutics is simply the process of correctly interpreting the Scriptures. Vizibuo vya kiBiblia ni kufanya harakati ya kufasiri maandiko kwa usahihi.

Concisely list the ELEVEN principles of Hermeneutics we have studied. Kwa kumaliza orodhesha kanuni KUMI NA MOJA za vizibuo tulivyo jifunza.

  1. INTERPRETATION MUST BE CONSISTENT AND OBJECTIVE. FASIRI LAZIMA IWE THABITI NA YA KUHUSU JAMBO LINALO HUSIKA.
  2. THERE IS ONLY ONE CORRECT MEANING OF A BIBLE PASSAGE. KUNA MAANA MOJA TU YA KUSAHIHISHA KIFUNGU CHA BIBLIA.
  3. THE LITERAL INTERPRETATION IS MOST USUALLY THE BEST. UFAFANUZI WENYE MAANA ILIO WAZI KWA KAWAIDA HUWA BORA.
  4. READ FROM THE AUTHOR'S POINT OF VIEW. SOMA KUTOKA MTAZAMO WA POINTI YA MWANDISHI.
  5. CONTEXT IS THE GREATEST KEY TO INTERPRETATION. MUKITADHA NI UFUNGUO MKUU KWA UFAFANUZI.
  6. ALWAYS COMPARE SCRIPTURE WITH SCRIPTURE. SIKUZOTE LINGANISHA ANDIKO KWA ANDIKO.
  7. THERE ARE NO CONTRADICTIONS IN THE BIBLE. HAKUNA UBISHI KATIKA BIBLIA.
  8. PASSAGES CAN HAVE BOTH NEAR AND FAR APPLICATIONS. VIFUNGU VYAWEZA KUWA NA YOTE MAWILI MATUMIZI YA MBALI NA YA KARIBU.
  9. LANGUAGE RULES MUST NOT BE IGNORED. KANUNI ZA LUGHA LAZIMA ZISIDHARAULIWE.
  10. REFERENCE MATERIALS ARE POWERFULTOOLS BUT THEIR USE MUST BE GOVERNED BY PRINCIPLES OF LOGIC. VIFAA VYA REJEA NI VYOMBO VYENYE NGUVU BALI MATUMIZI YAKE LAZIMA YA TAWALIWE NA KANUNI ZA MAANTIKI.
  11. WE MUST BE TAUGHT BY THE HOLY SPIRIT. HE IS THE MASTER TEACHER! LAZIMA TUFUNDISHWE NA ROHO MTAKATIFU.YEYE NI MWALIMU MKUU!

Using the principles of Hermeneutics, what does Matthew 5:43 "hate thine enemy" mean? Kutumia kanuni za vizibuo,nini anachomaanisha Mat 5:43"umchukie adui yako"?

In Matthew 5:43, Jesus was quoting what has been said by someone else. He is not endorsing hating your enemy. As a matter of fact, Christ commanding us in the context to love our enemy. Katika Mat 5:43,Yesu alikuwa akinukuu kile kilichosemwa na mwingine zaidi.hakuwa akiunga mkono kumchukia adui.kama jambo la hakika,Kristo anatuamru katika mukitadha kupenda adui.

In the conclusion of this chapter, 5 final considerations were presented Please list them concisely. Katika hitimisho la sura hii ya, 5 fikira za mwisho zimewasilishwa Tafadhali ziorodheshe kwa kuhitimisha.

  1. Consider the historical setting of a passage to insure correct interpretation. Fikiria mpangilio wa kihistoria wa kifungu kuuhakikisha ufafanuzi sahihi.
  2. Consider archeological factors to help you understand the meaning of a passage. Fikiria kweli za akiolojia kukusaidia maana ya kifungu.
  3. Consider geographical factors that would contribute to the understanding of the passage. Fikiria kweli za kijeogirafia ambazo zina changia kufahamu maana ya kifungu.
  4. Consider cultural practices of the time setting of the passage. Fikiria matendo ya kisiasa ya mpangilio wa wakati wa kifungu.
  5. Consider the entire Bible when interpreting the meaning of a passage. Fikiria Biblia yote wakati wa kufasiri maana ya kifungu.
