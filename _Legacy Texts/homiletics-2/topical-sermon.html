Topical Outlines Dondoo za kimada

How is the Topical Outline Different from the Textual Outline? Jinsi gani dondoo za kimada ni tofauti na dondoo za kifungu?

Founded on the theme or topic of the text, rather than on the words of the text. Iliopatikana kwenye dhamira au mada ya kifungu,kidogo kuliko maneno ya kifungu.

The theme is most prominent in a topical sermon. Dhamira iliojitokeza katika hotuba ya kifungu.

The divisions of the sermon are formulated by the preacher. (Which should be based on the theme) Migawanyo ya hotuba ilioundwa na mhubiri(ambayo ingejengewa juu ya dhamira)

The text should naturally suggest the theme. Kifungu kwa asili kingependekeza dhamira.

Advantages of the Topical Sermon Faida za hotuba ya kimada

It allows for rhetorical perfection. (If the aim of preaching be to make the sermon a work of art, let it be treated topically. Inaruhusu kwa ajili ya ukamilifu wa usemaji. (Kama shabaha ya kuhubiri ni kufanya hotuba kuwa kazi ya sanaa,iache ifanywe kimada.

It allows for thorough examination of the theme. (A single text rarely does this.) Inaruhusu kipimo kamili cha dhamira. (Kifungu kimoja marachache hufanya hivi.)

It trains the mind to breadth of view. (The spirit of the words is what we shouldaim to bring into the light, and not just the bare words.) Inafundisha akili kupanua mtazamo. (Roho ya maneno ni kile ambacho tungelenga kuleta kwenye nuru,nasio kama maneno matupu.)

A theme can be chosen first, A text can then be selected, Which supports that theme. Kwanza, kuchagua mandhari na kisha kuchagua Nakala kwamba inasaidia kwamba mandhari.

Treatment of the Topical Sermon Utengenezaji wa hotuba ya kimada

Its style. Mtindo wake.

RHETORICAL (unity, coherence, emphasis, euphony [See English II notes on the "Principles of Rhetoric"]) YA USEMAJI (Umoja, ya uwiano, ya kusisitiza,ya utamu wa sauti [Tazama maandishi mawili ya kiingereza juu ya "kanuni za usemaji])

ILLUSTRATIVE (Opportunity for illustrations, "stories".) YENYE SIMULIZI (Furusa kwa ajili ya picha za maelezo, "na habari".)

CLIMACTIC (Rises to successive climaxes reaching to a well–defined conclusion.) YA KIMATENDO (Huinua kwa matendo yenye mafanikio kufikia hitimisho lililo fafanuliwa vizuri.)

PERSUASIVE (Ends with recapitulation, application, and earnestness.) WENYE KUSHAWISHI(huishia mhitasari mfupi,matendo, na yenye kusemwa kwa bidii.)

The element of argument should be found in every sermon. Action should be our aim. The proposition must be proved to get proper action. Chanzo cha hoja kingekuwa kinapatikana katika kila hotuba. Tendo lingekuwa ndio shabaha yetu. Kihusishi lazima kiandaliwe ili kuleta tendo maalum.
Plain, direct application should be found in every sermon. No sermon should lack application. No congregation should be deprived of application. Wazi kabisa, matendo elekezi yangepatikana katika kila hotuba. hotuba isingepungukiwa vitendo. mkutano wa ibada haupaswi kunyimwa vitendo.
Without plain, direct application the preacher does little more than fire blank cartridges; he invites us to a field day rather than challenges us to aconflict. T. Harwood Pattison Pasipo uwazi, vitendo elekezi mhubiri hufanya kidogo kuliko risasi tupu za moto; yeye hutualika kwenye sikukuu kidogo kuliko kutupa changa moto kwenye kupambana.T. Harwood Pattison

Its arrangement. Mpango wake.

PROGRESSIVE (The points are indicated, reached, and reviewed.) YENYE KUENDELEA (Pointi zimeonyeshwa,zimefikiwa,na zimechunguzwa.)

EXHAUSTIVE (Every facet of the "gem" is held to the light.) YENYE KURUHUSU (Kila sehemu ya "kito cha thamani" imeshikiliwa katika mwanga.)

Observation. Kuangalia.

"Carefully go over the text to see what is going on, the "who, what, where, when, how, and why."This will pull out facts through the nature of Scripture and help avoid presumptions and fallacies. Allow the Word of God to "speak for Itself."" "Kwa uangalifu nenda juu ya kifungu angalia kile kinachoendelea juu yake,kwamba"nani,nini,wapi,lini,jinsi gani,na kwanini,""Hii itavuta kweli kutoka inje kwa asili ya maandiko na itasaidia kuepuka kiburi na hoja za uongo.acha neno la Mungu lijizungumzie lenyewe.""

Read the Text in Its Context Several Times. For example, if you are studying the Book of John, begin with reading the whole book. Soma kifungu katika mazingira yake nyakati kadhaa.Kwa mfano,kama unajifunza kitabu cha Yohana,anza kwa kukisoma kitabu chote.

"Make sure you are going into your relationship with God's Word in ameaningful manner. If you start too hastily, thinking you already know thepassage, you will not gain the insights and depth because your haste will make waste." "Hakikisha unaenda katika uhusiano wako na neno la Mungu katika kiwango chenye maana.Kama unaanza kwa haraka mno,kufikiri unajua kifungu cha maneno tayari,hutapata utambuzi na undani kwasababu ya haraka yako itakufanya mbaya usiefaa."

Never let your pride clog your vision and keep you in the dark or from discovering all you can. Kamwe usiache kiburi chako kungandisha maono yako na kukuweka katika giza au kutoka kugundua yote unayoweza.

Write a Summary for the Passage in Your Own Words Andika muhtasari kwa ajili ya kifungu katika maneno yako mwenyewe

Then you may start to look for the principal theme and idea. Kisha unaweza kuanza kutafuta dhamira kuu na dhana.

Start with an introduction; it needs to state the main topic or idea of the outline . This is the general principle/idea that represents the passage you are studying. Anza na utangulizi;inahitaji kutawala mada kuu au dhana ya dondoo.hii ni kanuni ya jumla/wazo ambalo linawakilisha kifungu cha maneno unayo jifunza.

Then write a short description of that idea. Kisha andika maelezo mafupi juu ya hilo wazo.

Make sure it is accurate to the text. Hakikisha yako sahihi na kifungu.

Make sure you write down the verses next to each idea. Hakikisha unaandika mistari baadaye kwa kila wazo.

"The chapter and verses were added by the publisher and are not part of the original text nor are they inspired. So, donot constrain yourself to them. You will find that sometimes they cut off key ideas in mid–thought that are still in "thread," that is, still going on. " "Sura na mistari ilio ongezwa na mchapishaji na sio sehemu ya kifungu cha asili wala kuvuviwa.Kwahiyo usijilazimishe nayo.utaikuta hiyo mara kadhaa ikiondoa mawazo ya ufunguo katika wazo la katikati ambalo bado ni"ni uzi,"ambao bado unaendelea."

Then List All Of the Various Ideas/Points in Sequence. Kisha orodhesha mawzo yote mbalimbali/pointi katika utaratibu.

This is where you use "Interpretation". This is taking what is said and finding out what the text means, how to interpret literally in the correct context. Hii ndio pale unapo tumia "Fasihi."Hii inachukua kile kina cho semwa na kupata kile kifungu kinamaanisha,jinsi ya kufasiri kwa simlizi katika mazingira sahihi.

This is where the supporting information and details go. This is called your Sub–Topic; these are commonly listed under the idea/topic with each piece ofinformation listed separately. Hapa ndio pale habari saidizi na habari zinazoendelea.Hii inaitwa mada– yako ndogo;hizi kwa kawaida huorodheshwa chini ya wazo/mada na kila kipande cha habari vilivyo orodheshwa kwa kutenganishwa.

Always, always go in the sequence of the text. Do not jump around! Siku zote, siku zote nenda katika utaratibu wa kifungu.Usirukie!

Never take out of God's Word what is not there, or read in your will as His. Usiliwekee neno la Mungu kitu ambacho hakikuwepo,au kulisoma kwa mapenzi yako kama lako.

Each sub–topic describes the main ideas from the paragraph/passage. Kila maandishi ya mada ndogo mawazo makuu kutoka kifungu cha maneno/aya.

When supporting information is listed under a sub–topic, there is a sequence that follows a logical order, especially in the Epistles. Wakati habari saidizi imeorodheshwa chini ya mada ndogo, kuna utaratibu ambao hufuata habari inayotakiwa, hasa hasa katika nyaraka.

"In the narrative passages, you will also find a logical order of events. However, Hebrew literature does sometimes jump the points around. It is best just to stick to the text and in the opening, summary, or conclusion, to draw attention to the general context. " Katika vifungu simlizi,utapata pia habari za matukio zenye maantiki.kwa vyovyote ,simlizi za kiebrania hufanya wakati mwingine kurukia pointi.Nibora kunasa kwenye kifungu na katika kufungua,mhitasari,au hitimisho,kuvuta usikivu kwa mazingira ya ujumla."

There are normally two ideas of information that should be listed. If there is only one piece of information to support a sub–topic, traditionally that information is included in the sub–topic. Kuna mawazo mawili ya kawaida ya habari ambayo inapaswa kuorodheshwa.kama kuna kipande kimoja tu cha habari kusaidia mada ndogo ,kwa desturi ambayo habari imehitimishiwa katika mada ndogo.

"Do not be so concerned with your form that you neglect your principal duty, which is to present the text in a logical and systematic way. Being true to the Lord's Word and the Divine Author's intent is paramount." "Usijihusishe na mfumo wako ambao unahitaji kazi yako ya kanuni,ambayo ni kuwakilisha kifungu katika utaratibu unaotakiwa na njia ya utaratibu.Uwe mkweli kwa neno la Bwana na kusudi la mwandishi wa ki Mungu ni kuu."
