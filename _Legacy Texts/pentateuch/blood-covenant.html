—To the ancient Hebrews, a blood covenant was the most binding, sacred agreement that could be made.— —Kwa waebrania wa zamani agano la damu lilikuwa mara linafunga,makubaliano thati kwamba jambo lingeweza kufanyika.—

The making of a blood covenant seems to be a universal concept found among all peoples in even the most remote parts of the world. The custom of making blood covenants can be found among American Indians as well as in the deepest most remote parts of Africa. The origin of the blood covenant customs looms somewhere beyond the horizon of known history.
