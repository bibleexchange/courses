Syllabus

 

What is the Pentateuch?

  • The first five books of God’s Holy Word which contain the record of the beginning and origin of all things and shadows of all subsequent Biblical revelation.
  • One of the most diverse and powerful studies of the Bible.
  • Essentially, the Pentateuch is the foundation of all spiritual truth and social principle!

 

What does this course involve?

  • You will be reading the textbook and responding to the essays by answering questions.
  • Each section will conclude with Study Questions that are answered ‘open book’ which prepares you for the test that follows. The test is not ‘open book’ but is taken by memory.
  • You will be consecutively reading each of the five books of the Pentateuch at the same time producing a question and answer from each chapter.
  • You will be studying on your own various topics relating to the Pentateuch; such as: The Feasts of the Lord, and Murmurings in the Wilderness.
  • You will be memorizing Scripture.
  • You will complete an open book Final Exam at the conclusion of this course that will test your understanding of the Pentateuch.

 

How is this Course Divided?

  • This course is divided into five (5) main sections. Each section will conclude with: 1) a special assignment 2) study questions and 3) a test.
  1. Section 1: Genesis
  2. Section 2: Exodus
  3. Section 3: Leviticus
  4. Section 4: Numbers
  5. Section 5: Deuteronomy

 

All assignments in this course are either:

 

  1. Open Book

Resources may be used to answer questions; such as, this textbook, the Bible, commentaries, and etc. OR,

 

  1. Closed Book

No resources are to be used. Closed book assignments are by memory only and are designed to test your memory as well as comprehension.

NOAH’SARK QUESTIONAIRRE

 

  1. How long did it take to build the Ark?

It took Noah 120 years to build the Ark. In order to save faithful and his family, God waited until The ark was ready before he punished the wickedness of man. All the while Noah was building, he was preaching! Gen. 6:3; I Peter 3:20; 2 Pt. 2:5

 

  1. How old was Noah when the Flood began?

Noah was 600 years old at the beginning of the Flood. Noah lived a total of 950 years. Gen. 7:6; 9:28,29

 

  1. What were the dimensions of the Ark with their feet equivalents?

300 cubits long = 450 ft. 30 cubits high = 45 ft. 50 cubits wide = 75 ft.

1 cubit = 1.5 feet; Gen. 6:15

 

  1. Make a couple comparisons with some things to the Ark to illustrate its size?

The Ark was as long as 1.5 football fields and as high as a 5-story building.

 

  1. How many decks did the Ark have?

The ark was divided into three decks. Gen. 6:16

 

  1. Why did God instruct Noah to take seven of the clean animals?

The reason is that clean animals were used for sacrifice. Gen. 8:20

 

  1. How long did it rain?

It rained for forty days and forty nights. Gen. 7:12

 

  1. Why was God so angry with man that He would cause such a flood?

God saw the wickedness of man and repented that He had made him. Gen. 6:5-7

 

  1. How long was Noah and his family with the animals in the ark?

BEGAN TO RAIN: 17th day of the 2nd month of Noah’s 600th year. Gen. 7:4,11

EXITED: 27th day of the 2nd month of Noah’s 601st year. Gen. 8:13,14
