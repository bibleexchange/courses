The difference between the major and minor prophets -

 

The Minor Prophets are shorter in comparison with the Major Prophets, but their messages are important and form a definite and valuable part of God’s whole revelation to man.

The prophets of these works came from diverse arenas of life and each of their ministries was unique as well. In their day, some of the prophets enjoyed more notoriety and acceptance than others, but none of these writings are inferior to the next. The messages each prophet carried are equally necessary and important because they are all the Word of God. It is just as important that we hear from God  as it was for the prophets of old.
