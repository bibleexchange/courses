  1. Have you sinned?

The Romans Road

                 Romans 3:23 “For all have sinned, and come short of the

                  glory of God;”

 

 

  1. What is cost of your sin?

Romans 6:23a “For the wages of sin is death…”

 

Romans 1:18a “For the wrath of God is revealed from heaven against all ungodliness and unrighteousness of men”

 

  1. What can you do to save yourself?

Romans 3:20 “Therefore by the deeds of the law there shall no flesh be justified in his sight: for by the law is the knowledge of sin.”

 

Romans 3:17 ‘Where is boasting then? It is excluded. By what law? of works? Nay: but by the law of faith.”

 

  1. So, is there hope for you?

Romans 5:8-9 “But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us. Much more then, being now justified by his blood, we shall be saved from wrath through him.”

 

Romans 6:23 “For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord.”

 

  1. Now friend, this final question is the most important question you will ever answer. Will you receive Jesus Christ as your Lord and Saviour and finally get on the right road?

Romans 10:13 “For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved.”

It is as simple as ABC:

    1. Admit that you are a sinner. (Romans 3:23 “For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God”)
    2. Believe that Jesus died on the cross to take the punishment for your sins and believe in your heart that God raised Jesus from the dead. (Romans 3:22 “Even the righteousness of God which is by faith of Jesus Christ unto all and upon all them that believe: for there is no difference:”) (Romans 10:9 “That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved.”)
    3. Call on Jesus, asking Him to forgive you of your sins (I John 1:9 “If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness.”) and with faith in your heart, ask Jesus to save you by his grace.  (Romans 10:13 “For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved.”) (Romans 3:28 “Therefore we conclude that a man is justified by faith without the deeds of the law.”) (Ephesians 2:8 “For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God:”)

 

  1. So friend, if you have truly received Jesus as your Lord and Saviour, your life has changed.

Romans 9:33c “…whosoever believeth on him shall not be ashamed.”

Go and tell others what a great thing God has done for you, sharing the news of God’s gift of salvation both far and wide.
