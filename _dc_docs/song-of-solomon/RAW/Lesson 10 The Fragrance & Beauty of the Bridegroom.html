    1. A Bundle of Myrrh

 

The bridegroom is to the bride as a bundle of myrrh. He is saturating her with his own fragrance, person, and nature.

 

Deep in the little heart of the bride, there dwells the beloved. Her whole life becomes permeated with his beautiful fragrance. The sweetness, fragrance, and beauty of Christ in the lives of the saints is beautiful.
