Song 2:3 As the apple tree among the trees of the wood, so is my beloved among the sons. I sat down under his shadow with great delight, and his fruit was sweet to my taste.

“As the apple tree” tappuwach - (fruit bearing tree)

  1. As a Fruitful tree

 

“As the apple tree among the trees of the wood”

  1. One above all the others
    1. Fruitful – same as Jesus
    2. Producing fruit
    3. Represents the Truth of the Word
  2. “the trees of the wood”  ( like men)
    1. No fruit – barren,  carnal, babes, immature, undeveloped
    2. Producing leaves, spill, cones
    3. Represents man’s theories, philosophy, opinions, teachings, ideologies, doctrines, beliefs, thoughts.
