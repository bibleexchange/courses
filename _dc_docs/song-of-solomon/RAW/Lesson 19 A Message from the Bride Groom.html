A Message from the Bridegroom to the Bride

 

INTRODUCTION

There is a great message from God not only here but in the entire book.

It is for those who are willing to pay the price, to attain to all that God has provided for them. Some are not interested of getting any more than what they have as far as simple salvation.

Born again is not something we decide to do. It is not by the church, It is not acquired by works.

Born again is an experience by the Spirit.

  1. A Message of Grace

 

11 For, lo, the winter is past, the rain is over and gone;

12 The flowers appear on the earth; the time of the singing of birds is come, and the voice of the turtle is heard in our land;

 

  1. For, lo, the winter is past
  1. He is using symbolic language, and the winter is symbolic of the law.
    1. Winter is Hard, cold, barren and no life.
    2. Law is binding, hard, unbending, simply stating the facts, producing no life.
    3. God does not bend His Law for anyone.
