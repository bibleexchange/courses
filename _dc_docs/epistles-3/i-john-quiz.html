I John Quiz

What is the theme of I John?

Develop that practical godliness that produces perfect fellowship with God.

What are the 2 evidences that John gives of those who have divine sonship?

A righteous walk (2:29 to 3:10)

Love for the brethren (3:11-18)

Concerning trying the spirits, what is the test John gave to determine whether they are of God or not?

I John 4:2-3

Every spirit that confesseth that Jesus Christ is come in the flesh is of God: And every spirit that confesseth not that Jesus Christ is come in the flesh is not of God: and this is that spirit of antichrist.

What is the proof of God's love for us?

God sent his only begotten Son into the world, that we might live through him. 4:9

What does God's love require of us?

To love one another 4:11

The proof of our love for God is manifested in _______________ _____ ______________________.

keeping His commandments 5:3

What is the victory that overcometh the world?

Our faith 5:4

Who is he that overcometh the world, but he that believeth that _____________________________.

Jesus is the Son of God 5:5

For there are three that bear record in heaven, the __________, the __________, and the ___________________: and these three are __________.

Father / Word / Holy Ghost / one 5:7

God has given us eternal life. Where is this life?

In His Son 5:11
