The Ministry and Work of the Holy GhostSWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will always exalt Jesus.SWAHILI

John 16:13-14 -- Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide you into all truth: for he shall not speak of himself; but whatsoever he shall hear, that shall he speak: and he will shew you things to come. He shall glorify me: for he shall receive of mine, and shall shew it unto you.SWAHILI

He shall not speak of Himself.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost does not exalt Himself. He will always exalt Jesus. The error of the false charismatic churches is their exaltation of the Spirit. The spirit that they operate in is not the true Holy Ghost.SWAHILI

John 15:26 -- But when the Comforter is come, whom I will send unto you from the Father, even the Spirit of truth, which proceedeth from the Father, he shall testify of me:SWAHILI

He will not glorify man.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost does not exalt man. The Holy Ghost will use a man to glorify Jesus.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will show you things to come.SWAHILI

John 16:13 -- Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide you into all truth: for he shall not speak of himself; but whatsoever he shall hear, that shall he speak: and he will shew you things to come.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost speaks through prophecy.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will reveal future events.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will show what is to come through an understanding of the Word.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will give power to minister.SWAHILI

Acts 1:8 -- But ye shall receive power, after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you: and ye shall be witnesses unto me both in Jerusalem, and in all Judaea, and in Samaria, and unto the uttermost part of the earth.SWAHILI

He will anoint a man to preach the Gospel.SWAHILI

I Thessalonians 1:5 -- For our gospel came not unto you in word only, but also in power, and in the Holy Ghost, and in much assurance; as ye know what manner of men we were among you for your sake. SWAHILI
I Corinthians 2:4 -- And my speech and my preaching was not with enticing words of man's wisdom, but in demonstration of the Spirit and of power:SWAHILI

He will minister to the congregation during the preaching.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will lead men to the truth.SWAHILI

He will help you to understand the Word.SWAHILI

He will draw men to the truth.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost is the comforter.SWAHILI

John 14:26 -- But the Comforter, which is the Holy Ghost, whom the Father will send in my name, he shall teach you all things, and bring all things to your remembrance, whatsoever I have said unto you.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will help you to pray.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will empower and help you to pray. There is praying in tongues in the Holy Ghost, but there is also praying in one's own language in the power of the Holy Ghost.SWAHILI

Jude 20 -- But ye, beloved, building up yourselves on your most holy faith, praying in the Holy Ghost,SWAHILI

Praying through the saints.SWAHILI

Romans 8:26-27 -- Likewise the Spirit also helpeth our infirmities: for we know not what we should pray for as we ought: but the Spirit itself maketh intercession for us with groanings which cannot be uttered. And he that searcheth the hearts knoweth what is the mind of the Spirit, because he maketh intercession for the saints according to the will of God.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will lead you into the will of God.SWAHILI

Rom. 8:14 -- For as many as are led by the Spirit of God, they are the sons of God.SWAHILI
Matthew 4:1 -- Then was Jesus led up of the Spirit into the wilderness to be tempted of the devil.SWAHILI

He will give you direction for ministry. -- Acts 8:29, Acts 10:19-20SWAHILI

He will keep you from doing your own will. -- Acts 16:6-7SWAHILI

The conviction of the Holy Ghost.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will convict men of their sin.SWAHILI

John 16: 7-8 -- Nevertheless I tell you the truth; It is expedient for you that I go away: for if I go not away, the Comforter will not come unto you; but if I depart, I will send him unto you. And when he is come, he will reprove the world of sin, and of righteousness, and of judgment: SWAHILI

The word for reprove here has the meaning of convincing and convicting.SWAHILI

The Word alone without the power of the Holy Ghost will not produce conviction of sin.SWAHILI

Man cannot produce conviction of sin.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost is Active in SalvationSWAHILI

Born of the Spirit.SWAHILI

John 3:5-7 -- Jesus answered, Verily, verily, I say unto thee, Except a man be born of water and of the Spirit, he cannot enter into the kingdom of God.SWAHILI

That which is born of the flesh is flesh; and that which is born of the Spirit is spirit.SWAHILI

Marvel not that I said unto thee, Ye must be born again.SWAHILI

Salvation is a work of the Spirit of God.SWAHILI

There is a difference between being born of the Spirit and being filled with the Spirit.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost is at work in Sanctification.SWAHILI

Romans 15:16 -- That I should be the minister of Jesus Christ to the Gentiles, ministering the gospel of God, that the offering up of the Gentiles might be acceptable, being sanctified by the Holy Ghost.SWAHILI

Sanctification is a part of Salvation.SWAHILI

There is not true salvation without a work of sanctification.SWAHILI

The power of the Holy Ghost helps the saint to stay saved.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost gives the saint power over sin.SWAHILI

The Holy Ghost will keep us from the world.SWAHILI

1 Peter 1:5 -- Who are kept by the power of God through faith unto salvation ready to be revealed in the last time.SWAHILI
