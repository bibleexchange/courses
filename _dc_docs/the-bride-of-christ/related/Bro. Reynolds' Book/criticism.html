Suggestion: My first experience with receiving revelation from God was many years ago while reading and studying diagrams of a well-known writer; as I read the Spirit of the Lord spoke to me and said, "What you are reading is wrong."

NOTE: The sentence I've copied from the text begins a new paragraph, and it seems to me because of that the use of "experience" without any further definition is unclear.  My first  experience… with what?

NOTE2: I feel there are two different thoughts trying to be expressed here.  Yes, they are closely connected, but are a whole lot to be said in one breath.  Therefore, I have suggested the use of  “;” followed by the phrase "as I read."  This is merely to break up the thought into two parts so that the reader may understand what is being said better.
