Study Questions 3 -- Ch. 4-8:1

What did John hear when the door was opened in heaven?

A voice as it were a trumpet.

What did John see when the door was opened?

John saw a throne which was set in heaven and One that sat on that throne.

What was it that encircled the throne?

The throne was encircled by a rainbow,

What type of a throne was it?

The throne was a throne of Judgment.

Who are the Twenty-Four Elders?

The twenty-four elders are the Bride of Christ.

Who are the Four-Living Creatures?

The four-living creatures are the body of Christ, the Church.

What do the Four-Living Creatures give to Him that sat on the throne?

They gave Him glory and honor and thanks to Him that sat on the throne.

What do the Twenty-Four Elders do?

They fell down and worshiped Him, and cast their crowns before the throne.

Who was found worthy to take the book and to open the seals thereof?

The Lion of the tribe of Judea,The Lamb of God

What was the number of the angels that gave honor to the Lamb?

Ten thousand times ten thousand, and thousands and thousands.

What do the four horses represent?

The four horses represent the Judgments of God.

What were the colors of the four horses?

A white horse, A red horse, A black horse, and A pale horse

Tell what these colors signify?

white –Righteous Judgment, red –War, black –Famine, pale –Death

Tell what happened under the fifth seal.

Souls under the altar slain for the Word of God.

Tell what happened under the sixth seal.

A great earthquake The sun turns black Moon turns as blood The stars fall

Who are the 144,000 that were sealed?

They are Jews of all the tribes of the children of Israel.

How large was the multitude that was clothed with white robes?

It was an innumerable multitude that was clothed with white robes.

What did they have in their hands?

They had palms in their hands.

Where did they come out from?

They came out of great tribulation.

Tell what happened under the seventh seal.

Under the seventh seal there was a mysterious silence in Heaven and seven angels were given seven trumpets.
