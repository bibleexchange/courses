Do Chapter's 1 & 2 contradict each Other? Je Sura ya kwanza na ya pili zina pingana?

The sequential differences of the creative events recorded in chapters one and two have been the topic of much criticism. In chapter one, the purpose of the writer is to give a brief summary of the highlights of creation. In chapter two, the writer is establishing theplace of man in this creation while reiterating material already covered in chapter one. There is no contradiction found here. It is because the author's purpose changes when chapter two begins, that we see any differences.Again, these differences are still never contradictory, but only the details that achieve the specific goal of the Author are reiterated while unnecessary details are left out.
