The Preparation of the Priesthood (Ch. 8) Maandalizi ya Ukuhani (Sura 8)

This chapter gives us an account of the solemn consecration of Aaron and his sons to the priest's office.[1]Hii sura inatupa hesabu ya uwekaji wakfu waibada wa Haruni na wanawe kwenye Ofisi ya kuhani.[1]

It was done publicly, and the congregation was called together to be witnesses of it, Lev. 8:1-4. Ilikuwa inafanyika kwa wazi,kusanyiko lilikuwa limeitwa pamoja ilikushudia, Wal. 8:1-4.

It was done exactly according to God's appointment, Le 8:5. Iliukuwa ianfanywa kwahalisi kulingana na ahadi au maafikiano ya Mungu, Wal 8:5.

Verses 1-30 " Mistari ya 1-30 "

They were washed and dressed, 6-9, 13. Walioshwa na kuvikwa, 6-9, 13.

Thetabernacle, utensils and priests were anointed, 10-12. Hema ya kukutania, vyombo vya nyumbani na makuhani walipakwa mafuta, 10-12.

A sin-offering was offered for them, 14-17. Sadaka ya-dhambi ilikuwa inatolewa kwa ajili yao, 14-17.

Aburnt-offering, 18-21. Sadakaya kuteketeza, 18-21.

The ram of consecration, 22-30. Kondoodume wa kuwekwa wakfu, 22-30.

The continuance of this solemnity for seven days, 31. Mwendelezo wa sherehe hii kwa siku saba, 31.

Verses 31-36 Mistari ya 31-36

Moses, having done his part of the ceremony, now leaves Aaron and his sons to do theirs. Musa,ameshafanya sehemu yake ya sherehe,sasa anamwacha Haruni wanaye kufanya sehemu ya.

They must boil the flesh of their peace offering, and eat it in the court of the tabernacle, and the remains were burnt with fire, Lev 8:31-32. Lazima wachemshe nyama ya sadaka yao ya amani,na kuila katika baraza la hema ya kukutania,na mabaki kuteketezwa kwa moto, Wal 8:31-32.

This signified their thankful consent to the consecration: when God gave Ezekiel his commission, he told himto eat the roll,( Eze 3:1-2.) Hii ilionyesha makubaliano yao ya shukuranikwa kuweka wakfu: wakati Mungu alipompa Ezekieli agizo lake,alimwambia alile gombo,( Eze 3:1-2.)

They must not stir out of the court of the tabernacle for seven days, Lazima wasinge shinda inje ya baraza ya hema ya kukutania kwa siku saba,

Untangling themselves with the norm of earthly life that they might pursue spiritual and eternal purposes.(Lev 8:33) " kujiachilia wenyewe pamoja na kanuni ya maisha ya kidunia ambayo wangeweza shikilia kiroho na makusudi la milele.(Wal 8:33) "
