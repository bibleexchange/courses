The theme or subject of a sermon is the main though or idea to be preached. The theme of Romans 1:16 is the Gospel. Dhamira au somo la hotuba ni wazo kuu au dhana itakayo hubiriwa.dhamira ya Warumi 1:16 ni injili.

What is the thesis statement of a sermon? Formulate a thesis statement from Romans 1:16. Maelezo ya maneno ya hotuba ni nini?Elezea maelezo ya maneno toka Warumi 1:16.

The thesis statement of the sermon is the entire sermon described in one complete sentence. A thesis statement from Romans 1:16 could be "The Gospel is full of power."
