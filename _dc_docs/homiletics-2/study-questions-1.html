What is the definition of Homiletics? Ufafanuzi wa elimu ya mahubiri ni nini?

Homiletics is The science or art of religious discourse or preaching. Elimu ya mahubiri ni sayansi au sanaa yamazungumzo ya kidini au mahubiri.

What is the definition of Preaching? Ufafanuzi wa mahubiri ni nini?

The spoken communication of Divine truth with a view to persuasion. Mawasiliano ya mazungumzo ya ukweli wa kiungu pamoja na mtazamo wakushawishi.

What was Paul's instruction to Timothy in 2 Timothy 4:2? Maelekezo ya Paulo kwa Timotheo yalikuwa ni nini katika 2 Tim 4:2?

Preach the Word. Hubiri neno.

Since Christian preaching is not a theatrical importance, why is necessary to develop pulpit "skills"? Hata mahubiri ya kikristo sio umuhimu wakujifanya, kwanini ni muhimu kuendeleza ufundi wa 'madhabahuni'?

Preaching is not a performance but it is still public speaking requiring skill. Kuhubiri sio mchezo lakini bado ni mazungumzo ya wazi yanayo hitaji ufundi.

What is the thesis statement? Maelezo ya maneno ni nini?

The thesis statement is the one complete sentence that will define the subject to be preached. Maelezo ya maneno ni sentesi moja kamili ambayo itafafanua somo litakalo hubiriwa.

Specifically, what is the foundation upon which a sermon is built? Kwa uhalisi, nini ambayo ni msingi juu ya kile ambacho hotuba inaenda kujengwa?

The text is the foundational Scripture upon which the preacher preaches. Kifungu ni andiko la msingi juu ya kile mhubiri anacho hubiri.
