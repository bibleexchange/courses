Quiz Chapter 4: Hermeneutics Jaribio Sura ya 4: Vizibuo

What is "Biblical Hermeneutics"? "Vizibuo" vya kiBiblia ni nini?

Biblical hermeneutics is simply the process of correctly interpreting the Scriptures. Vizibuo vya kiBiblia ni kufanya harakati za kufasiri maandiko kwa usahihi.

Concisely list as many as you can of the ELEVEN principles of Hermeneutics that we have studied. Kwa kuhitimisha orodhesha kwa wingi kadiri uwezavyo Kanuni kumi na moja za vizibuo ambazo tumeshaajifunza.

  1. INTERPRETATION MUST BE CONSISTENT AND OBJECTIVE. UFAFANUZI LAZIMA UWE THABITI NA UNAOHUSIKA NA JAMBO MAHUSUSI.
  2. THERE IS ONLY ONE CORRECT MEANING OF A BIBLE PASSAGE. KUNA MAANA MOJA TU SAHIHI YA KIFUNGU CHA BIBLIA.
  3. THE LITERAL INTERPRETATION IS MOST USUALLY THE BEST. UFAFANUZI ULIOWAZI MARA NYINGI HUWA BORA.
  4. READ FROM THE AUTHOR'S POINT OF VIEW. SOMA KUTOKA MTAZAMO WA POINTI YA MWANDISHI.
  5. CONTEXT IS THE GREATEST KEY TO INTERPRETATION. MUKITADHA NI UFUNGUO MKUU KWENYE UFAFANUZI.
  6. ALWAYS COMPARE SCRIPTURE WITH SCRIPTURE. SIKU ZOTE LINGANISHA ANDIKO KWA ANDIKO.
  7. THERE ARE NO CONTRADICTIONS IN THE BIBLE. HAKUNA UBISHI KATIKA BIBLIA.
  8. PASSAGES CAN HAVE BOTH NEAR AND FAR APPLICATIONS. VIFUNGU VYA WEZA KUWA NA VYOTE VIWILI MATUMIZI YA KARIBU NA YA MBALI.
  9. LANGUAGE RULES MUST NOT BE IGNORED. KANUNI ZA LUGHA LAZIMA ZISIDHARAULIWE.
  10. REFERENCE MATERIALS ARE POWERFULTOOLS BUT THEIR USE MUST BE GOVERNED BY PRINCIPLES OF LOGIC. VIFAA VYA REJEA NI VYOMBO VYENYE NGUVU LAKINI MATUMIZI YAKE LAZIMA YATAWALIWE NA KANUNI ZA KIMAANTIKI.
  11. WE MUST BE TAUGHT BY THE HOLY SPIRIT. HE IS THE MASTER TEACHER! LAZIMA TUFUNDISHWE NA ROHO MTAKATIFU.YEYE NI MWALIMU MKUU!

In Principle Two, what must we make allowance for as an exception to the rule? Katika kanuni ya pili,kwa kitu gani lazima tutoe ruhusa kama udhuru kwenye kanuni?

We must make allowance for prophecy as it often has a two–fold fulfillment. But, still, only one interpretation of these fuilfillments. Lazima tutoe ruhusa kwa ajili ya unabii ikiwa mara nyingi una sehemu mbili za utimilizo.Lakini bado uwe ufafanuzi mmoja wa timilizo hizi.

In the conclusion of this chapter, 5 final considerations were presented. Please list at least two of them concisely. Katika hitimisho la sura hii ya ,5 fikira za mwisho zimewasilishwa.Tafadhali ziorodheshe angalau mbili kati ya hizo kwa kuhitimisha.

  1. Consider the historical setting of a passage to insure correct interpretation. Fikiria mpangilio wa kihistoria wa kifungu kwa kuhakikisha ufafanuzi sahihi.
  2. Consider archeological factors to help you understand the meaning of a passage. Fikiria kweli za kikiolojia kukusaidia kufahamu maana ya kifungu.
  3. Consider geographical factors that would contribute to the understanding of the passage. Fikiria za kijiografia ambazo zinge changia ufahamu wa kifungu.
  4. Consider cultural practices of the time setting of the passage. Fikiria matendo ya kisiasa ya mpangilio wa kifungu.
  5. Consider the entire Bible when interpreting the meaning of a passage. Fikiria Biblia nzima wakati wa kufasiri maana ya kifungu.
