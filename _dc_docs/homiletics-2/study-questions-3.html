Study Questions Three: Sermon Organization Jifunze maswali sehemu ya tatu:Mpangilio wa hotuba

What is the source of Christian teaching? Nini chanzo cha mafundisho ya kikiristo?

Remember that Scripture is the source of Christian teaching. Kumbuka kwamba maandiko matakatifu ndio chanzo cha mafundisho kikiristo.

Outlining will be your primary means of what? Kutoa dondoo itakuwa msingi wako kumaanisha nini?

Outlining will be your primary means of producing quality, Bible–centered lessons, talks, and sermons. Kutoa dondoo itakuwa msingi wako kumaanisha kuzalisha ubora,Masomo ya katikati ya Biblia, maongezi, na hotuba.

List the three kinds of sermons and give a brief description of each one. Orodhesha aina tatu za hotuba na kutoa maneno machache ya kila moja wapo.

Expository sermons are those in which the subject, maindivisions and most of the details are derived from the text. Textual sermons are those in which the subject and main divisions are derived from the text. Topical sermons are those in which the subject alone is derived from the text. Hotuba zinazo elezeka ni zile katika somo ambalo, migawanyo mikuu na wingi wa habari umeletwa toka kifungu.Hotuba za kifungu ni zile ambazo somo na migwanyo mikuu imeletwa toka kifungu.Hotuba za kimada ni zile ambazo somo pekeyake na migawanyo mikuu imepatikana toka kifungu.

What is the foundation of any sermon? Nini ambayo ni msingi wa kila hotuba?

The TEXT is the foundation of any sermon. KIFUNGU ni msingi wa kila hotuba.

What is the theme? Dhamira ni nini?

The sermon in one sentence. Hotuba katika sentesi moja.

How is learning to outline like learning anything new? Jinsi gani kujifunza kwa dondoo kama kujifunza kitu kingine mpya?

Outlining, like any new activity, will take practice. Kutoa dondoo,kama shughuli yeyote mpya,itachukua vitendo.

What is a text without context? Ni nini kifungu pasipo mazingira?

A text without a context is just a pretext. Kifungu pasipo mazingira ni kama hoja tu.

What is the chief difference in textal sermons from topical sermons? Ni nini tofauti kubwa katika hotuba za kifungu kutoka hotuba za kimada?

The chief difference in textual sermon and topical sermons is the source of the divisions. Tofauti kubwa katika hotuba ya kifungu na hotuba za kimada ni chanzo cha migawanyo.

What is the source of the divisions in a textual sermon? Nini ambayo ni chanzo cha migawanyo katika hotuba ya kifungu?

The Bible supplies divisions from the text chosen. Biblia hutoa migawanyo kutoka kifungu kilicho chaguliwa.

Give at least two positive things about the textual sermon. Toa angalau vitu viwili ambavyo vinakubaliana juu ya hotuba ya kifungu.

Naturally, the textual sermon centers around the text. This is a great way to bring the actual words of the Bible before the hearers. A well–constructed textual sermon has most of the advantages of the topical sermon. Kwa asili, hotuba ya kifungu hushikiza kuzunguka kifungu.Hii ni njia kubwa kuleta maneno ya kawaida ya Biblia mbele ya wasikiaji.hotuba nzuri ya kifungu ilio jengwa inayo faida nyingi za hotuba ya kimada.

Give at least two negative things about the textual sermon. Toa angalau vitu viwili kinyume juu ya hotuba ya kifungu.

Some have said that the use of the text, especially of the short text, is often fatal to the most intelligent treatment of the Scripture itself. Because of this, the unity of the Bible is not so apparent with this method, as it is with the expository method of preaching. Another danger is that the preacher may become nothing more than a text hunter in the Bible jungle of texts. Baadhi ya watu wameshasema kwamba matumizi ya kifungu,hasa hasa vya kifungu kifupi,mara nyingi hufisha kwa utengenezaji mwingi usioeleweka wa andiko lenyewe. Kwasababu hii, umoja wa Biblia haujawa wazi pamoja na njia hii,kama ilivyo pamoja na njia inayoelezeka ya mahubiri. Hatari nyingine ni kwamba mhubiri anaweza kuwa si kitu zaidi ya kuwa muwinda kifungu katika pori la Biblia ya vifungu.

In your own words describe the results upon a person who seldom reads or studies their but for any other reason than a homiletical purpose to preach to someone else. Katika maneno yako andika matokeo juu ya mtu ambaye husoma mara chache au kujifunza kwasababu nyingine zaidi ya kusudi la kimahubiri kumhubiria mtu fulani zaidi.

Such a person may quite unconsciously excuse himself from his own private study upon the ground that he is professionally engaged with the Bible much of his time. Kama mtu aweza kujikuta kabisa akijitolea udhuru kutoka kujifunza kwake faraghani kwenye udongo ambao yeye kiufundi hunolewa na Biblia sana kwa wakati.

What is better than a man who attempts to find texts? Ni nini ambayo ni bora kuliko mtu ambaye hujaribu kuvipata vifungu?

It is far better for one to put himself in the way of texts finding him, than for him to attempt to find texts. Nibora mtu mwenyewe kujiweka katika njia ya vifungu kumpata yeye,kuliko yeye kujaribu kuvipata vifungu.

What should naturally suggest the theme of a topical sermon? Ni nini ambayo kiasili ingependekeza dhamira ya hotuba ya kifungu?

The text should naturally suggest the theme. Kifungu lazima kiasili kipendekeze dhamira.

Give an example of the advantage of the topical sermon. Toa mfano wa faida ya hotuba ya kimada.

It allows for thorough examination of the theme. (A single text rarely does this.) Huruhusu kipimo kidogo cha dhamira. (Kifungu peke yake mara chache hufanya hivi.)

Explain what is meant that the topical sermon is "climactic." Elezea ni nini iliomaanishwa kwamba hotuba ya kimada ni "ya kitaaluma."

Rises to successive climaxes reaching to a well–defined conclusion. Huinua kufikia taaluma zenye mafanikio kwa hitimisho lililo fafanuliwa vema.

Should you jump around a chapter? Explain. Je unapaswa kurukia sura ?Elezea.

Always, always go in the sequence of the text. Do not jump around! Siku zote,wakati wowote nenda katika utaratibu wa kifungu.usirukie!

What kind of order of events do we find in narrative passages? Ni nini aina ya watu wa matukio tunaopata katika vifungu simulizi?

In the narrative passages, you will find a logical order of events. Katika vifungu simulizi, utapata mlolongo wa habari za matukio.

Is it proper to list only one sub–point? Explain. Je ni kitu maalumu kuorodhesha pointi ndogo moja tu?Elezea

No. There are normally two ideas of information that should be listed. If there is only one piece of information to support a sub–topic, traditionally that information is included in the sub–topic. What is paramount and more important than the form of your sermon? Ni nini ambayo ni kuu na ya muhimu zaidi kuliko muundo wa hotuba yako?

Being true to the Lord's Word and the Divine Author's intent is paramount. Kuwa mkweli katika neno la Bwana na kusudi la mwandishi wa kiungu ni kuu.
