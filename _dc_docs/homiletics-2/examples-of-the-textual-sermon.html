Variations of the Textual Sermon Tofauti za hotuba ya kifungu

Textual–Topical Ya kifungu–yakimada

The divisions of the sermon are expressed topically, but treated textually. Migawanyo ya hotuba imeelezwa kimada,lakini imefanywa kwa kifungu.

The text itself still provides the natural divisions of the sermon.(Treated textually). Kifungu chenyewe bado kinatoa migawanyo ya kiasili ya hotuba.(iliofanywa kawa kifungu).

The thought contained within the divisions is explained using words that expound upon the topic. (Expressed topically). Wazo limeingizwa pamoja na migawanyo limefafanuliwa kwa kutumia maneno ambayo yanaeleza juu ya mada.(Yaliyoeleze kimada).

The thought rather than the words of the text is made prominent. Wazo linakwaruza kuliko maneno ya kifungu yaliyo fanywa kwa ushupavu.

EXAMPLE: MFANO:

TEXT: I Thess. 4:4 KIFUNGU: I Wathess. 4:4

That every one of you should know how to possess his vessel in sanctification and honour; Kwamba kila moja wenu ajue kuuweza mwili wake kwa utakatifu na heshima;

You need to know how to possess yourself. Unahitaji kujua jinsi ya kujiweza mwenyewe.

You need to know how to progress in Christ. Unahitaji kujua jinsi ya kuendelea katika kristo.

You need to know how to present yourself. Unahitaji kujua jinsi ya kujiwakilisha mwenyewe.

Textual–Inferential Maelezo ya –kifungu

The theme is found in the words of the text. Dhamira hupatikana kwenye maneno ya kifungu.

The divisions of the sermon are determined by the text. (Textual treatment). Migawnyo ya hotuba imeonyeshwa na kifungu (Uundaji wa kifungu).

A series of inferences is drawn naturally from the words of the text. Safu za mwisho a maneno huchukuliwa kiasili kutoka maneno ya kifungu.

Example: Mfano:

TEXT: Philippians 2:12 KIFUNGU: Wafilp 2:12

Wherefore, my beloved, as ye have always obeyed, not as in my presence only, but now much more in my absence, work out your own salvation with fear and trembling. Basi,wapendwa wangu,kama vile mlivyo tii sikuzote,si wakati mimi nilipokuwapo tu,bali sasa zaidi sana mimi nisipokuwapo,utimizeni wokovu wenu wenyewe kwa kuogopa na kutetemeka.

Salvation is a pressing necessity to every man. Wokovu unaelezea ulazima kwa kila mtu.

Every man is responsible for his own salvation. Kila mtu anahusika kwa ajili ya na wokovu wake mwenyewe.

Salvation is certain for every man who is in earnest to be saved. Wokovu ni sasa kwa ajili ya kila mtu apaswaye kuokoka.

Dividing a text into its parts: Outlining Kukigawanya kifungu katika sehemu zake:kutoa dondoo

"The Bible is divided into books,chapters, paragraphs, verses. But further dissection is necessary for clear understanding and presentation of a text. We must divide the text into "sizeable" pieces. (e., bite size). " "Biblia imegawanyika katika vitabu,milango,paragirafu,misitari.Lakini kutenganisha zaidi ni lazima kwa ajili ya ufahamu wa wazi na uwakilishaji wa kifungu. Tunapaswa kugawanya kifungu katika "usawa na "vipande usawa wa (e., kutafuna)."

Divide the Text into its parts. Gawanya kifungu katika sehemu zake.

Relate the parts of the Text Husianisha sehemu za kifungu

Simplify the parts of the Text. Fanya sehemu za kifungu.
