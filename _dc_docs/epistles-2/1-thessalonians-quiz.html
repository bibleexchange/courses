What was their attitude in those circumstances? 1:6 and 2:13,14Ni nini iliokua msimamo wao katika hali hizo? 1:6 na 2:13,14

What was their example? 1:7-9Ni nini iliokuwa mfano wao? 1:7-9

In 2:6 what was Paul's title?katika 2:6 kichwa habari cha Paulo kilikuwa nini?
