Introduction to PhilemonUtangulizi kwa Filimoni

The WriterMwandishi

We see from the first verse that this epistle was written by Paul the apostle.Tunaona kutoka msitari wa kwanza kwamba hii barua iliandikwa na Paulo mtume.

The Purpose of WritingKusudi la kuandika

Paul wrote this epistle to Philemon to intercede for Onesimus, the runaway slave.Paulo aliandika hii barua kwa Filimoni kumtetea Onesimo,mtumwa mkimbizi.

The ThemeDhamira

The theme of this epistle is Christian character.Dhamira ya waraka huu ni tabia ya mkristo.

The Outline of PhilemonDondoo kwa Filimoni

There is only one chapter in the book of Philemon, which can be outlined in at least two ways:Kuna sura moja tu katika kitabu cha Filimoni,ambayo inaweza kuelezwa kwa mhitasari katika njia mbili:

A private letter of intercessionBarua binafsi ya maombezi

Greetings (1:1-7)Salamu (1:1-7)

Testimonial concerning the changed character of Onesimus (1:10,11)Jambo la ushuhuda tabia iliobadilika ya onesimo (1:10,11)

Appeal for forgiveness (1:12-19)Ombi kwa ajili ya msamaha (1:12-19)

Promise of making good any losses (1:18,19)Ahadi ya kufanya vizuri kuhusu hasara (1:18,19)

Great wisdom with humility used (1:20, 21)Hekima kubwa na unyenyekevu uliotumika (1:20, 21)

Paul's request (1:22)Ombi la Paulo (1:22)

Closing remarks (1:23-25)Maoni ya kumalizia (1:23-25)

An illustration of the gospel methodKielelezo cha njia ya injili

A challenge to be a servant like Jesus wasChangamoto ya kuwa mtumishi kama Yesu alivyokuwa

Four wonderful blessings of practical ChristianityBaraka nne za ajabu za ukristo wa matendo

The message of end-time deliveranceUjumbe wa ukombozi wa wakati wa mwisho

For the sake of this study, we will look at the second method of outlining this epistle.Kwa ajili ya upekee wa somo hili,tuta angalia kwa njia ya pili ya kudondoa barua hii.
