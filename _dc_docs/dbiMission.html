  • Vision

    To Make the Gospel VISIBLE.

    To provide an authoritative and sincere resource on the Scripture and the Christian world view. To faithfully become that City on a Hill (Matthew 5:14) God has called his Church to be.

    To Disciple Believers

    To give spiritual bread to the spiritually hungry. To inform and educate Believers who have a burden for Christian ministry ( personal work and public).

  • Goals

    RESOURCE. Create an Online Resource of quality Christian Study materials.

    COMMUNITY. Create an Online Community that encourages and enables Christian growth and faith. To connect teachable and sincere seekers of truth with honest and quality ministers of the Gospel.

    EDUCATION. It is essential to Christian growth and to the general human condition that men know the truth. The TRUTH is Jesus Christ and the message of Salvation he brought and made possible on the cross. Knowing the TRUTH is the key our victory over sin, the flesh and the devil (John 8:32).
