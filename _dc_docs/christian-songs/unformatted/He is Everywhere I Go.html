He is Everywhere I Go

VERSE 1:

Some may wonder where is this God of Israel

They say we’ve never see Him So how are we to know

Just take a look at this homeward road I’m traveling

And you will see a mighty God everywhere I go

 

CHORUS:

He’s in the eye of my storm, The bottom of my ocean

The morning and the midnight, And every wind that blows

On the top of my mountain, The lowest of my valleys

Where is God, He’s everywhere I go

VERSE 2:

Three Hebrew Children they found Him in the fiery furnance

Bartimaeus found Him on the road to Jericho

Paul and Silas they found Him in a dark cold dungeon

Wherever we are that is where I God will go
