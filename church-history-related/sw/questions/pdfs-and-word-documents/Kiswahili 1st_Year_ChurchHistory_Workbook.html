Major Assignments Descriptions

 

ONE: Written & Oral Report on any of the Early Apostles

 

  • LIMITS: 3-6 minutes; 300-600 words
  • Each student will do one.
  • Choices will be limited to any of the original 12 disciples & the Apostle Paul.
  • Report should include details about:
    • Personality/Character
    • Birth & death
    • Accomplishments

 

 

TWO: Written & Oral Report on Christian Persecution

 

  • LIMITS: 3-6 minutes; 400-700 words
  • Each student will do one.
  • Use at least one example from Foxe’s Book of Martyrs
  • You may use more recent examples, as well.
  • Expound on the effect of persecution on the church.
  • Answer the question: “How should a Christian respond to persecution?”

 

 

THREE: Read a Biographical Book and Write a Book Report

 

  • LIMITS: 3-6 minutes; 300-600 words
  • Each student will do one.
  • The book must be at least 110 pages in length.

 

 

 

FOUR: Written & Oral Report on a Positive Contemporary Influence

 

  • LIMITS: 3-6 minutes; 300-600 words
  • Each student will do one.
  • Much attention is given to negative influences in this church age. The subject of this report is a discovery and presentation of some POSITIVE influences in this age.
