James

 

“FIVE URGENT REQUIREMENTS IN DEVELOPING FAITH”

 

 

James 1:1-12

 

 

 

1. Joy “count it all joy when ye fall into divers temptations”

 

 

 

2. Trying of our Faith “trying of your faith worketh patience.”

 

 

 

3. Patience “But let patience have her perfect work,”

 

 

 

4. Asking and Receiving of the Lord ” let him ask in faith, nothing wavering.”

 

 

 

5. More Faith “when he is tried, he shall receive the crown of life”
