#7 Essay on Preventing a Divided Heart

 

It is essential to preventing a divided heart to acknowledge that God will have no rival with our affections. It is vanity and foolishness to consider reaching for God and this world at the same time. It is a pipe dream and only a bubble the devil has designed. God desires all of our heart, or He is not Lord of our lives. Israel’s heart became divided because she allowed her eyes to wander. This wandering eye is taking counsel from the flesh and allows it to guide our decisions. Therefore, in order to maintain a sanctified devotion to God and a single heart toward Him alone, we must maintain a “single eye.” We cannot allow our eyes to wander in consideration of other things that are “good to look upon”, but set our affection on things above. The Lord He alone is God; we must serve Him alone and not another.
