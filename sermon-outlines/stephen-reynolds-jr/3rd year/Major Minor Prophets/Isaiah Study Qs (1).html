  1. What is the difference between the Major and Minor prophets?

The Minor prophets are shorter in comparison to the Major prophets, but their messages are not less important than them and they form a definite part of God’s whole revelation to man.

 

  1. What was the condition of Israel at the time of Isaiah?

Isaiah 1:2-4 They were backslidden as a nation.
