21. Compare Joel 3:13,14 with Rev. 14:14-16.

 

These passages speak of a fullness of iniquity culminating in the great wrath of God and judgment on sin. No longer is the fruit of iniquity allowed to grow, but is cut down as wheat with a sickle. This is a time of clear-cut and definite judgment.

Both use the pictures of a SICKLE, The harvest is ripe, & the measure of wickedness is full and it is time for reaping.

Joel labels the place of reaping as “The Valley of Decision” and the time of the reaping as “The Day of the Lord.”

Revelation more clearly describes the One with the sickle:

  1. Upon a White cloud One sat
  2. Like unto the Son of man
  3. Having on His head a golden crown
  4. And in His Hand a Sharp sickle
