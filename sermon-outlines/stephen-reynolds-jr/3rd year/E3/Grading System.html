Grading System:

 

For non-question assignments that cannot be graded conventionally A and B are the only passing grades:

 

Points

10 On Time

80 Poor B-

85 Good B+

90 Very Good A

95 Excellent A+

 

Study Questions and Quizzes:

 

Points

Each question will have a set value and will be subtracted from the Excellent Score of 105.

(Subtract 10 points for a late assignment.)

 

Year Grade Percentages:

 

Present:

100% 20 points

85% to 99% 15 points

75% to 84% 10 points

50% to 74% 5 points

Less than 50% 0 points

 

Study Questions:

Average Percentage will determine grade.

Example: If average percentage for year is 90% then the grade will be 13.5 points out of a possible 15 points.

 

Quizzes:

Average Percentage will determine grade.

Example: If average quiz grade is 100% then the year-end grade will be 35 points out of a possible 35 points.

Any percentage above 100 will add a bonus of 5 points making the grade 40 points.

 

Assignments:

Average grade will determine grade. A possible 35 points.

 

Bonus: Class Participation. A possible 5 points. Rated 1 to 5. Poor – Ok – Good – Very Good –Excellent.
