English III - Grading System

Teacher Stephen G. Reynolds, Jr.

 

Present:

Being present in class will account for 15% of year-end grade.

100% 20 points

85% to 99% 15 points

75% to 84% 10 points

50% to 74% 5 points

Less than 50% 0 points

 

Study Questions:

Average of Study Questions scores will account for 15% of year-end grade.

Example: If average percentage for year is 90% then the grade will be 13.5 points out of a possible 15 points.

If year average is above 100% no special bonus is awarded. Bonuses are awarded only as needed. (Each individual Study Question, Quiz, or Assignment will be subtracted 10 points for unreasonable tardiness. Definition is teacher’s decision.)

 

Quizzes:

Average of Quiz scores will account for 35% of year-end grade.

Example: If average quiz grade is 100% then the year-end grade will be 35.

If year average is above 100% then a bonus 5 points will be awarded. (40 points, not 35.)

 

Assignments:

Average grade will determine grade. A possible 35 points.

 

Bonus: Class Participation. Rated 1 to 5. (Poor – Ok – Good – Very Good –Excellent.)
