Youth Study of Revelation

General Instructions on Bible Study

  1. All Scripture is given by inpiration of God and is profitable for DOCTRINE - REPROOF - CORRECTION - INSTRUCTION IN RIGHTEOUSNESS

2 Timothy 3:16 All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness:

  1. STUDY, ENDEAVOR, LABOR

2Ti 2:15 — Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.

Its often in the process of the work, that discovery unexpected is made.

  1. KEEP THE UNITY OF SPIRIT

Ephesians 4:3 Endeavouring to keep the unity of the Spirit in the bond of peace.

Study with the intent of acquiring truth to help others as well as ourselve. NOT TO WIN A DEBATE.

It is true of Scripture as all "manifestations of the Spirit": That the manifestations of the spirit are given to every man to profit withall.

1 Corinthians 12:7 — But the manifestation of the Spirit is given to every man to profit withal.
  1. Emphasis prayer coupled with study.

Php 1:9 — And this I pray, that your love may abound yet more and more in knowledge and in all judgment;

  1. Interpretation is made by comparing Scripture with Scripture.

2 Peter 1:20,21 — Knowing this first, that no prophecy of the scripture is of any private interpretation. 21 For the prophecy came not in old time by the will of man: but holy men of God spake as they were moved by the Holy Ghost.

The Bible is a UNITED work.
