1Pe 3:8 ¶ Finally be ye all of one mind <homophron>, having compassion one of another <sumpathes>, love as brethren <philadelphos>, be pitiful <eusplagchnos>, be courteous <philophron>:

 

<homophron> like-minded, i.e. harmonious:--of one mind. ONE MIND

 

<sumpathes>, from 4841; having a fellow-feeling ("sympathetic"), i.e. (by implication) mutually commiserative:--having compassion one of another.MUTUAL SYMPATHY

 

<philadelphos> from 5384 and 80; fond of brethren, i.e. fraternal:--love as brethren.

BROTHERLY LOVE

<eusplagchnos>, from 2095 and 4698; well compassioned, i.e. sympathetic:--pitiful, tender-hearted. TENDER HEART

 

<philophron>: from 5384 and 5424; friendly of mind, i.e. kind:--courteous.

FRIENDLY MIND
