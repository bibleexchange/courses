Stephen G. Reynolds, Jr.

Poetry

May 11, 2006

A Psalm of Mercy

 

My sin has been great,

But You have cleansed me

Even when I was an enemy

You paid my debt and took my place

Rejoice, oh, my soul with all your strength

Remember the deep from which I was drawn

Call upon the name of the Lord and praise Him

In His mercy He has answered my plea

Infinite and measureless is the scope of His love

While I was rebellious He reached down

Faith took hold of His hand

And by repentance He took me as His own

Unto the highest heights I may climb

Though from the deepest deep I came

Lord, You are the Object of my Praise;

Being Just and Righteous you showed mercy.
