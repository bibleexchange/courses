To be perseverant is to exhibit “stick-to-it-ive-nss”. When anyone begins a project there is usually an initial excitement and a gung-ho attitude, but (as a wise man once said) “on the threshold of every human endeavor is disappointment.” Perseverance is the attitude to fight beyond those negative feelings and emotions in order to reach your goals.

Stop! Look! Wait! Some of the antagonists of the perseverant attitude are the voices of distraction that seem to compass us. Everywhere we turn is a voice telling us to detour here or to go there, but we must remain steadfast and persevere in order to reach our goals. If your goal and cause is just then it is a must; from all distractions veer, press on and persevere.
