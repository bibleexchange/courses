March 28, 2007

Joseph S. Dipaolo

TITLE: The Love of Christ

TEXT: John 15:13

 

The Love of God that He would die for you and I.

 

He knew me when He was on the cross. He knew the mistakes that I would make and he was still willing.

 

When the devil reminds you of your past remind him of his future.

Proverbs 18:24 Must shew myself friendlyàNothing we could do would ever merit God’s love because He loved us when we were unloving and unlovable.

 

God never falls out of love with us. He has never stopped loving me.
