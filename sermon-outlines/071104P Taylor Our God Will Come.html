TITLE: Our God Will Come

TEXT: Matthew 8:5-13

 

v.7 And Jesus saith unto him, I will come and heal him.

 

The thousands can call on their ‘god’ and they will NOT come, but our GOD WILL come and Heal! We serve a God who comes when we call Him.

 

I have called on the Lord for our finances, and the LORD CAME.

 

Centurion, a man in the Roman Military, sergeant, sergeant major having authority over at least 100 men. But He had a problem at home. You might have a problem at home tonight. He had a servant tormented with a life threatening disease. LUKE says that this servant was very dear to him; He loved his servant, cared about his servant. He was sick and in bed and about ready to die. The centurion hears that Jesus was near by. LUKE 7, the centurion did not come personally. He sent the Jewish leaders to give Christ the message.

 

It is not about what we DO. It is our FAITH in God that gets the attention of Jesus.

 

He recognized the spiritual authority of Christ.
