TEXT: Jeremiah 13:14

TITLE: No Change without God Change

 

There is one way for Godliness to be produced and that is God touching and awaking us.

 

And I will dash them one against another, even the fathers and the sons together, saith the LORD: I will not pity, nor spare, nor have mercy, but destroy them.

 

There are 5 warnings of impending judgment in this chapter.

 

I don’t want to fall into a place where God says my life is beyond the point of repair.

 

v.1-11 First Warning: Acted out by Jeremiah à take a clean linen loin cloth and wear it, but don’t wash it. Day after day wear it, but don’t wash it. Show them how they’re living. They take my favor, mercy, and grace and they then go out to do their business never returning recognizing I am God.

 

v.12-13 Second Warning: Every bottle filled with wine. Thus saith the Lord I will fill all the inhabitants of this land with drunkenness.

 

Jer 13:15 Hear ye, and give ear; be not proud: for the LORD hath spoken.

 16 Give glory to the LORD your God, before he cause darkness, and before your feet stumble upon the dark mountains, and, while ye look for light, he turn it into the shadow of death, and make it gross darkness.
