TITLE: The Immutability of God

TEXT: Ezekiel 8:15-18

 

Immutable: never changing or varying; being established as unchangeable

 

Deut. 32:4

He is the Rock, his work is perfect: for all his ways are judgment: a God of truth and without iniquity, just and right is he.

 

Jeremiah 31:3

 

Psalm 105:3

His mercy is everlasting

 

He is immutable in his attributes.

 

He is immutable in his counsel.

His word never varies.

 

Job

He is of one mind who can change Him…

 

Psalm 33:11

The counsel of the Lord standeth forever.

 

Human nature can not be relied upon but God never changes.
