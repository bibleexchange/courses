TITLE: Absolute Surrender

TEXT: Genesis 22:1-11

 

Total Commitment. It is not dead works, but living sacrifice. If you have been following dead works they lead to death, but if you have been following God being a living sacrifice it leads to life.

 

In both places the lord called Abraham he responded quickly, but here…Abraham, Abraham…

 

God is not interested in the human body being sacrificed, but in the human heart sacrificed. He wants a living sacrifice.

 

Romans 12:1

I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of God, that ye present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service.

 

If we are to come to the place of absolute surrender then we must follow some steps.
