POWER

The authority to act or do something according to a law or rule

 

  1. The Power of the Holy Ghost is a Mighty Work
  2. The Power of the Holy Ghost is the ability to Witness.
  3. The Power of the Holy Ghost is to be led by the Spirit.

 

Acts 5:32 And we are his witnesses of these things; and so is also the Holy Ghost, whom God hath given to them that obey him.
