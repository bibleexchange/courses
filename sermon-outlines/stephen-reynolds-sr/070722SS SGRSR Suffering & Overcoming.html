TITLE: Suffering & Overcoming

TEXT: Hebrews l2:2

 

Suffering and Overcoming go hand in hand.

 

He endured the cross. He never gave up.

--to abide to undergo, to stay under, to bear trials, He had to fortitude to endure

 

CHRIST WAS NOT JUST WILLING TO ENDURE, BUT HE DID IT!

 

Just enduring hardships is one thing

 

To follow is to imitate. We need to do more than just imitate Christ. (Matthew 4:19) Follow AND I WILL MAKE you. The Overcomer needs to made by God. We need to partake of His nature.

 

We have more than an example to look to and follow after, but we have a savior that will make us.

 

THE PAIN JESUS SUFFERED ENDURING THE CROSS:

  • How much pain He must have suffered.
  • A Broken heart for the sin of the world, heartache.
  • The Stripes, smote, spit, beat
  • The physical pain alone along with the emotional pain
