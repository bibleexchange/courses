TITLE: Total Victory, A Spoil, & An Inheritance

TEXT: Colossians 2:8-

 

INTRODUCTION

Total victory over sin, with a spoil, and  with an inheritance.

 

 

2:9 There is a place in God where that power which was manifested through Him is made available to every obedient Believer.

Abraham gave all he had to his son Isaac. In John, the father gave all He had to the Son.

The God of Heaven offers us a GIFT, which begins with salvation and total victory over sin.

There are things that God has done for us that has taken us further and beyond ‘restoration’, but spoils. Christ defeated sin, the devil, and took a spoil that day. If we obey Him and believe Him there is great blessing and spoil that Hhe is going to give us. Seek ye first the Kingdom of God and His righteousness and all these things shall be added unto you. Christ took victory over the penalty of sin and over the POWER OF SIN. Triumphing over the devil and taking a spoil in his death’s atonement.

2:15 “spoil” = 3:10 “put off” (same word)

Walk uprightly. According as you have received Christ, walk in Him.
