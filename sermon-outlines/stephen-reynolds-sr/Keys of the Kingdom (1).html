  1. The Authority of Christ

Who has His Authority?   It is according to His Desire.

Authority will never be perfected until we are aware of our own inability to bring about freedom to a man on our own merit and strength...  the one who rightly understands and applies Authority. The one who rightly relates themselves with God and places themselves at his mercies for his purpose, understanding his great works and the works he wills to do through us...    I Cor. 15:57-58; II Cor. 2:14; Eph.3:7-12; Song 7:10 “his desire is toward me”                                                                                         

Spiritual Authority is the privilege of Responsibility with accountability that comes with the Revelation of Christ in your life, (not just words) its sorely needed especially for this is the "end time"

The Authority of Christ will work in the life of the submissive believer that God’s Word has made strong with (“POWER” and Authority) and the FIRE of His WORD has PURIFIED.

Thank you Lord; for the Authority of YOUR WORD which is the Supreme Authority that we submit to and find to work in our life as we receive and obey you.
