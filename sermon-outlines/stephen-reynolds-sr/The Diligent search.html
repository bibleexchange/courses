  1. The Bridegroom’s Grace

 

It is not the continual knocking only but the Word of His Grace that will awaken  the Bride

 

“it is the voice of my beloved that knocketh, saying, Open to me, my sister, my love, my dove, my undefiled”

 

  1. Revealed in His knocking

“it is the voice of my beloved that knocketh”

    1. To awaken her
    2. To arouse her (to get her up and going)
    3. To gain her fellowship
