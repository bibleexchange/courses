Deliverance Center – Portland, Maine

Rev. Stephen Reynolds, Sr.

Wednesday, May 26, 2010

TITLE: Thy Plants shall be an Orchard

TEXT: Song of Solomon 4:13,14

 

I have a responsibility personally to seek the Lord.

Maturing is needed and absolutely necessary.

 

Song of Solomon 4:13,14—Thy plants are an orchard of pomegranates, with pleasant fruits; camphire, with spikenard, 14 Spikenard and saffron; calamus and cinnamon, with all trees of frankincense; myrrh and aloes, with all the chief spices:

 

The devil would love to handle your growth. To mesmerize your thinking.

The church will backslide and die. You are going to have to seek God for a demonstration of His power.

The Holy ghost will bring the Believer to full maturity.

You must have that incorruptible seed.

 

How great the orchard can be with that incorruptible seed sown.

 

If u really are true.

 

I Thessalonians 3:12 And the Lord make you to increase and abound in love one toward another and toward all men even as we do toward you.
