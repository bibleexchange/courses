Title: Shut Up Under the Law

TEXT: Galatians 3:23—But before faith came, we were kept under the law, shut up unto the faith which should afterwards be revealed.

 

You can’t produce what God has promised by your own effort. It must  be according to God’s power and we must Believe Him.

Israel never learned WHY God gave them such a law to follow. This is  such a simple verse, but Israel never understood it.
