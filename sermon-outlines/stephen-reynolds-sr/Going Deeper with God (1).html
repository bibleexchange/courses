Going Deeper with God

 

Mat.13:1  The same day went Jesus out of the house, and sat by the sea side.

 2 And great multitudes were gathered together unto him, so that he went into a ship, and sat; and the whole multitude stood on the shore.

 3 And he spake many things unto them in parables, saying, Behold, a sower went forth to sow;

 4 And when he sowed, some seeds fell by the way side, and the fowls came and devoured them up:

 5 Some fell upon stony places, where they had not much earth: and forthwith they sprung up, because they had no deepness of earth:

 6 And when the sun was up, they were scorched; and because they had no root, they withered away.

 7 And some fell among thorns; and the thorns sprung up, and choked them:

 8 But other fell into good ground, and brought forth fruit, some an hundredfold, some sixtyfold, some thirtyfold.

 9 Who hath ears to hear, let him hear.

 

Luke 6:48 He is like a man which built an house, and digged deep, and laid the foundation on a rock: and when the flood arose, the stream beat vehemently upon that house, and could not shake it: for it was founded upon a rock.

 

We need a deeper thought, understanding, meditating on the Truths of God, A deeper experience.

 

We are living in a very shallow age.

 

This is an age of very shallow sense of moral values.

 

The world is mixed up in their thinking, Elvis is still alive and they think Jesus is dead.

 

2Tim. 1:12 For the which cause I also suffer these things: nevertheless I am not ashamed: for I know whom I have believed, and am persuaded that he is able to keep that which I have committed unto him against that day.

 

Isaiah 5:20 Woe unto them that call evil good, and good evil; that put darkness for light, and light for darkness; that put bitter for sweet, and sweet for bitter!

 

What are you going to do about getting serious about serving God?

What are you doing to see the end-time Promise of Deliverance?

 

We need a vision

We need an aim

We need an action

We need a burden
