Fruits; show the reality of your repentance by forsaking your sins and obeying the commands of God. In order that repentance may be shown to be sincere, it must produce good works.

That is, the proper fruits of restoration, the proper evidence that you are genuine.

No discourse could have been more appropriate or more cutting.

Do not bring your craftiness and  deception to this work; carry not your hypocrisy into your professed repentance; but show your honesty by forsaking sins, and thus give evidence that this crowding to Jordan  or coming to the Deliverance Center Is not some act of pretence.
