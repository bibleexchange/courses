  1. The Path of Life
    1. Personal Responsibility
      1. We must walk through all things knowing that God is with us.
      2. We must understand that God is in control for good as we obey. (Rom.8:28)
      3. We must not be afraid of adversity, we have to walk through and overcome.
    2. Obstacle – (problem)
      1. Walking through a valley
      2. Walking through near death experiences.
      3. Walking through dangerous territory.
  2. Guidance
    1. As shepherd He guides, comforts and follows.
