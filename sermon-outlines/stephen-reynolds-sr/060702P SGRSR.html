Sunday Night

Title: Tragic Mistakes, Triumphant End

Text: Romans 10:1-11, 16,17

 

Deut. 30:1,2

!. It is a tragic mistake to not hear from God.

 

2. It is a tragic mistake to hear from God and not do it.

 

I Cor. 1:30 Christ is our righteousness. IT is His life in me. It is the power of God.

 

3. It is a tragic mistake listening to my own mind or the words of others and not hearing from God.

The suffering isn’t just to prepare you to go to heaven, the suffering process is that after you have crucified the flesh, you are a vessel that can help people that live in trouble, darkness, distress, and destruction.

It is from Faith to faith. We are not establishing our own righteousness, but righteousness is by faith. Joshua and Caleb wholly followed the Lord.
