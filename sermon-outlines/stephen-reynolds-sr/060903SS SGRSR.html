TITLE: Shall I Go?

TEXT: I Samuel 23:1-6

 

Real life is only possible through the cross.

Eternal life is given to the Believer through the cross. It is given as a gift. We could not earn it, but God made provision through the cross that we might receive eternal life as a gift.

At the cross Christ dealt with our sins, and purchased for us eternal life through His death and resurrection.

Christ not only dealt with our sins at the cross, but He also dealt with US at the cross. “being made conformable unto His death.” Christ through His death dealt wsith our sins, but also dealt with us making it possible for us to be made conformable to His death.
