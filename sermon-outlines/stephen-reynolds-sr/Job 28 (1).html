Ps 68:19 Blessed be the Lord, who daily loadeth us with benefits, even the God of our salvation. Selah.

 

God loads us daily with his benefits, and we, load him with our sins, how can we expect any other than that he should load us with his judgments?

 

Sinners' own consciences will be witnesses for God that he has not been wanting to them in the means of grace, so that, if they perish, it is because they have been wanting to themselves
