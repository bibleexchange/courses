Fool

  1. Mt 7:26 And every one that heareth these sayings of mine, and doeth them not, shall be likened unto a foolish man, which built his house upon the sand:

 

    • The sand is that which is earthy, carnal, fleshly and worldly
    • The sand is all that is selfish, prideful, boastful and sinful.
    • The sand is independent of the Rock

Self governing, self regulating, self determining, self sufficient self centered, self interested, self shaping and self seeking.

 

All honest and thinking persons know that sand cannot withstand the serious storms of this life. Therefore, any life built upon the sand is sure to fall.

 

 

On Christ the solid Rock we stand (we build) all other ground is sinking sand.
