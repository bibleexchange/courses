A Good Man

Ps 37:23 The steps of a good man are ordered by the LORD: and he delighteth in his way.

 

The steps of a good man

 

are ordered by the LORD: (Divine planned, structured, controlled, established, recognized)

 

The LORD:

 

He (the LORD) delighteth in his way

 

  1. The Lord orders good steps for every man
  2. The good man walks in the steps that God orders.
  3. The Lord delights in the goodness of the good man's way.

 

The ways of a good man

 

The good man is established by obeying the ordered steps of the Lord

The Lord is delighted in that the good man is in His control, care, Character or Image and likeness.

 

The goodness of God leads us to repentance

The goodness of God last for eternity
