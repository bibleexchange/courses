 3 Thou therefore endure hardness, as a good soldier of Jesus Christ.

IV. The strong farmer

 

If we would be partakers of the fruits, we must labour; if we would gain the prize, we must run the race.

We must first labour as the husbandman does, with diligence and patience, before we are partakers of the fruit; we must do the will of God, before we receive the promises

 

Heb 6:12 That ye be not slothful, but followers of them who through faith and patience inherit the promises.
