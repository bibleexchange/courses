  1. We are workers together with God. It is a great privilege to work together with God. What is the work that we do with God? "beseech," urge, and plead with men to receive the wonderful grace of God.

“Grace and Truth”

•  The Truth is  God has provided and proclaimed  Full salvation Complete Deliverance through Christ.

2.  The message of redemption is given by Great Grace and is not to be received in vain. A person must not hear the message with deaf ears. Redemption Deliverance, Healing, resurrection power, overcoming power and Full Salvation are available. God has made it possible—He calls and draws men—to be saved.

•  let the grace of God take effect in your life and ministry.

•  let God's grace work in your heart for complete forgiveness and freedom.

•  let the death resurrection and righteousness of Christ be counted as your death resurrectionand righteousness.

We must not receive the grace of God in vain. God's grace is much too wonderful—it is the only hope and way of redemption and salvation.

By Grace through faith.

  1. The decision to be saved is to be made now. Now is the only reasonable time to be saved. There may be no tomorrow; in fact, there may not even be one hour left for any of us. Any person's life can be snatched away any moment of any day by accident, disease, or heart attack.

Isaiah 49:8 two significant facts, God Himself is the spokesman,

a.  This is the acceptable time when God has heard the cries of men. This is the day of salvation when God has succoured men.

b.  Paul, the minister, cries out: "Behold, now is the accepted time; behold, now is the day of salvation."  The day is coming when the day of salvation will no longer be.
