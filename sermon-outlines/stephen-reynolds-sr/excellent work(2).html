Love makes faith work to fullest potential  Gal.5:5-7

Php 1:9 ¶ And this I pray, that your love may abound yet more and more in knowledge and in all judgment;10 That ye may approve things that are excellent; that ye may be sincere and without offence till the day of Christ;

Right Knowledge and good judgment set high marks for a growing and discerning love.

Love never focuses upon good feelings.                                                  Feelings  are never the focus—What then is the focus?

  • The focus of true love is right knowledge.
  • The force of love is good judgment
    • The word means intelligence or discernment.

 

We not only want to know a person but we want to learn all we can about that person.

 

We want to gather all the intelligence and facts possible and discern them so that we can please that person.

 

There are three reasons why we need a love that grows more and more.

 

  1. Growing love is needed to approve things excellent.

 It is not enough just to know what is right.

growing love will stir us to choose the excellent and the best.

  1. Growing love is needed to be sincere and pure.
  2. Growing love is needed to keep us from causing others to stumble.
