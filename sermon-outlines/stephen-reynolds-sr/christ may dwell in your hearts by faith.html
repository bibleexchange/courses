Christ dwelling in your heart by faith

Eph 3:16 That he would grant you, according to the riches of his glory, to be strengthened with might by his Spirit in the inner man;

 17 That Christ may dwell in your hearts by faith; that ye, being rooted and grounded in love,

 18 May be able to comprehend with all saints what is the breadth, and length, and depth, and height;

 19 And to know the love of Christ, which passeth knowledge, that ye might be filled with all the fulness of God.

 

  1. That He would grant you, according to the riches of His glory, to be strengthened with might by His Spirit in the inner man;

 

    1. There is always a need to be strengthened.

Not just because of the fact at times we have been weakened but that we might not be weakened and that we may grow and be strengthened as we grow.

    1. How does He strengthen us?
      1. With His might by His Spirit in the inner man
        1. The Power of the Blood to wash away sin.
        2. The Power of the Word to keep us out of Sin.
        3. The Power of the Holy Ghost to keep sin out of you.

 

Without the indwelling of the Holy Ghost you will not stop the lusts of the flesh.

 

Titus 2:14 Who gave himself for us, that he might redeem us from all iniquity, and purify unto himself a peculiar people, zealous of good works.

 Jas 4:8 Draw nigh to God, and he will draw nigh to you. Cleanse your hands, ye sinners; and purify your hearts, ye double minded.

 

    1. How much of His strength is promised?
      1. According to the riches of His Glory
      2. The fullness of Christ

 

And what does the Spirit use?

 

Bible study, prayer, public worship, good, clear, simple, preaching, Witnessing, Christian fellowship of like precious faith, exercising you’re faith in Christian service.

It becomes a life-long process if our faith and love does not grow in proportion.

 

By faith and love we become stronger and stronger and able to bear more of what God wants to give us, and More and more of Christ comes to dwell in our hearts.

 

  1. That Christ may DWELL in your hearts by FAITH
  2. That ye, being rooted and grounded in love,
  3. May be able to comprehend with all saints what is the breadth, and length, and depth, and height;
  4. To know the love of Christ, which passeth knowledge
  5. That ye might be filled with all the fulness of God.
  6. Not just dwell in you by faith, but He wants to manifest himself through good works, Mighty Works, greater things shall ye see.
