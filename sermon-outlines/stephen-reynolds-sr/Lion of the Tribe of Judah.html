  1. The Judgment of God to compare the men of Jerusalem with the city where David dwelt.
    1. “Woe to Ariel” – A message from God of heavy Judgment unto victory
    2. “Woe to Ariel, to Ariel”
      1. A very simple warning for complicated living, lukewarm-mundane ceremony
      2. A comparing of what is, to what was, to what will be, to what should be. Isa29:1-8, 23-24

        2. “Arieyl”- Altar   -   (Hearth of God)
        3. <har'el> – (Mount of God)
