TITLE: Cupbearers, Swordbearers

TEXT: John 18:11

 

The Lord has a word for Peter. Every joint supplying. God has a word for us tonight. It is the Word that will produce faith in our hearts. That faith will produce obedience. He wants us to act upon His Word.

 

There is a process that produces the oil. Pressing of the fruit comes anointing.

 

The Spirit of God move across the whole body.
