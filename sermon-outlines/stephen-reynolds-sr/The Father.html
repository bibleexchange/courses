The Characteristics of A Christian School

 

Col 4:5—Walk in wisdom toward them that are without, redeeming the time.

 

  1. Christian schools are designed to reveal the Bible.

 

    1. It is the scriptures that distinctively earmarks the Christian school

 

  1. Adding a bible, does not make the school Christian.
  2. Integrating the Bible into your teachings does not make it Christian.

 

  1. Eight characteristics of a Christian School

 

  1. Every Christian school should be designed and dedicated to lead a child to a saving knowledge of Jesus Christ.

 

John 3:3 Jesus answered and said unto him, Verily, verily, I say unto thee,

               Except a man be born again, he cannot see the kingdom of God.

 

  1.  Every Christian school needs to be dedicated to leading the child to the knowledge of God’s Word.

 

2Timothy 2:15 Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not     

                        to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.

 

Psalm 119:105 NUN. Thy word is a lamp unto my feet, and a light unto my path.

 

  1. Every Christian school needs to teach others how to lead another to Jesus Christ.

 

Mt 28:19 Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the         Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost:

 20 Teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you: and, lo, I am with you alway, even unto the end of the world. Amen.

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. Every Christian school needs to teach our children to fear God.

 

Prov. 8:13 The fear of the LORD is to hate evil: pride, and arrogancy, and the evil way, and the froward mouth, do I hate.

Eccl. 12:13 Let us hear the conclusion of the whole matter: Fear God, and keep his commandments: for this is the whole duty of man.

 

  1. Every Christian school needs to teach every child the way of Holiness.

 

Col 1:22 In the body of his flesh through death, to present you holy and unblameable and unreproveable in his sight:

 

2Cor. 6:17 Wherefore come out from among them, and be ye separate, saith the Lord, and touch not the unclean thing; and I will receive you,

 

Hebrews 12:10 For they verily for a few days chastened us after their own pleasure; but he for our profit, that we might be partakers of his holiness.

 

  1. We need to teach our students the stewardship of Life.

 

1Cor. 6:19 What? know ye not that your body is the temple of the Holy Ghost which is in you, which ye have of God, and ye are not your own?

 20 For ye are bought with a price: therefore glorify God in your body, and in your spirit, which are God's.

1Cor. 15:31 I protest by your rejoicing which I have in Christ Jesus our Lord, I die daily.

 

  1. We need to teach dedication to excellence.

 

Col 3:23 And whatsoever ye do, do it heartily, as to the Lord, and not unto men;

 

  1. We need to teach our children how to worship God.

 

Grow quiet before God.

 

John 4:24 God is a Spirit: and they that worship him must worship him in spirit and in truth.
