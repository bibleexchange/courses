THE  REVEALED CHRIST

 

  1. THE LAMB OF GOD

John the Baptist was given the Revelation of the Christ and the authority to speak it.

(John 1:29)

The next day John seeth Jesus coming unto him, and saith, Behold the Lamb of God, which taketh away the sin of the world.

  1. Jesus takes away the sin of the world.
  2. We are saved from and out of sin.
  3. This revelation, so seemingly simple, yet

there are many "professing" Christians, who are being truly deceived that  Christ allows and understands continual sinfulness.

It is worth repeating,

(John 1:36)

And looking upon Jesus as he walked, he saith,

Behold the Lamb of God!

 

  1. CHRIST FORMED IN YOU.

The apostle Paul's very real cry to the Galatians was "Christ be formed in you".

(Galatians 4: 19)

My little children, of whom I travail in birth again until Christ be formed in you.

 

  1. The Galatians even "bewitched" to believe lies

compelled Paul to lament for them to receive and take hold of the true revelation of the Christ.

 

  1. "of whom I travail in birth"....Paul's cry has to

be in our hearts. "until Christ be formed in you"

  1. Christ is not formed in many, because the

Truth of who Jesus is, His nature, His true Holiness, and real love is not being preached or taught, but in most churches wrestling with deception.

  1. Galatia and Corinth wrestled with deception.
    1. Re 3:7-13 Philadelphia overcomes with Authority
    2. Laodicea lives in deception.
  2. Christ in us. We are not turning to the

weak and beggarly elements of this present world, and this present day deception or concept of accepting Christ to fit our understanding and permitting Him to be the Son of God.

 

  1. A TRUE MANIFESTATION OF JESUS

 

  1. While Peter answered the question

Jesus asked, which was "Whom say ye that I am?"

Paul revealed the Son of Man's obedient choice, or decision, to please His father.

(Philippians 2: 4-8)

Look not every man on his own things, but every man also on the things of others.

Let this mind be in you, which was also in Christ Jesus; Who, being in the form of God, thought it not robbery to be equal with God; But made himself of no reputation, and took upon him the form of a servant, and was made in the likeness of men;

And being found in fashion as a man, he humbled himself, and became obedient unto death, even the death of the cross.

  1. This is the Christ manifested.
    1. Look also on the things of others
    2. Made himself of no reputation
    3. Took upon the form of a servant
    4. He took on the likeness of sinful flesh
    5. We must partake of His likeness
  1. THE DESCENT OF THE CHRIST.

 

    1. From the Son of God, from the beginning.
    2. To the form of a servant.
    3. To the likeness of a man.
    4. To the loss of His life.
    5. To the death of the cross.

 

This is why John the Baptist uttered

"He must increase, but I must decrease" John 3:30

 

    1. To the Resurrection of Life and Authority with the keys of Hell and of death.

 

Re 1:18  I am he that liveth, and was dead; and, behold, I am alive for evermore, Amen; and have the keys of hell and of death.

Re 9:1 ¶ And the fifth angel sounded, and I saw a star fall from heaven unto the earth: and to him was given the key of the bottomless pit.                                                                                             20:1 ¶ And I saw an angel come down from heaven, having the key of the bottomless pit and a great chain in his hand.

 

  1. THE REVELATION OF FAITH

 

    1. Once receiving by Grace through a gift

of faith and having His life imparted, we now can realize and experience the fullness of  faith of the Lord inhabiting us.

(Galations 2:20)

I am crucified ith Christ; nevertheless, I live, yet not I, but Christ liveth in me and the life which I now live in the flesh I live by the faith of the Son of God, who loved me, and gave himself for me. - Hallelujah!

 

 

    1. Christ liveth in me- more and more able now to recognize His voice.

 

    1. It actually pleases God to have His Son revealed in us!

(that is more profound than what most are willing to understand, what writing, what Promises)

 

  1. CHRIST'S AUTHORITY IN US
    1. The revelation of Christ was given to Peter,(Matthew 16:15-17)
    2. The Authority of Christ was given to Peter with the Revelation of Christ.

 

(Matthew 16:18-20)

And I say nto thee, That thou art Peter, and upon this rock will I build my church; and the gates of hell shall not prevail against it.

And I will give unto thee the keys of the kingdom of heaven; and whatsover thou shall bind on earth shall be bound in heaven; and whatsover thou shall loose on earth shall be loosed in heaven.

Then charged he his disciples that they should tell no man that he was Jesus the Christ.

 

    1. Spiritual Authority is the privilege of

Responsibility with accountability that comes with the Revelation of Christ in your life, (not just words) it is sorely needed especially for this "end time"

 

    1. The devil and all his unclean spirits will  recognize the Son of God if He is in you.

 

    1. We must walk in the Authority of Christ

and all the enemies of the Lord will be His footstool. Amen.
