Deliverance

2Cor. 1:10 Who delivered us from so great a death, and doth deliver: in whom we trust that he will yet deliver us;

There are three aspects of Deliverance

    1. Who delivered us from so great a death
      1. Deliverance is Salvation from the penalty of sin
    2. Doth deliver
      1. Deliverance is  Salvation from the Power of sin
    3. In whom we trust that he will yet deliver us
      1. Deliverance is Salvation in Purity of life
