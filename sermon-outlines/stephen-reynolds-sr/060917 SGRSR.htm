Deliverance Center – Portland, ME

Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

September 17, 2006, Sunday AM

 

TITLE: The Second Miracle

TEXT: John 4:44-54

 

INTRODUCTION

If you want God to give you wisdom, keep humbling yourself.

People are being sidetracked and offended, but the man in our text did not back down to the resistance but spoke the words of faith.

The Second Miracle

The Kingdom of God confronts the kingdom of the devil in the last days.

The Holy Ghost sets a pattern in our life, and if we follow the pattern the Word of God is always there. His word never changes or alters. There are times when we get out of place and out of position. We don’t always know what God is doing and where to follow Him.

CERTAIN NOBLE MAN. There is only ONE to be worshipped.
