TITLE: A New Concept of Worship

TEXT: John 4:10-32

 

v.32 But he said unto them, I have meat to eat that ye know not of.

 

He knows what we are going through, HE KNOWS.

 

This chapter begins with the Lord knowing how the Pharisees were talking about him and putting him on a level of competition with the rest of the world.

 

When therefore the Lord knew how the Pharisees had heard that

Jesus made and baptized more disciples than John,

 

True worship has to do with worshipping Him. Not just to get him to do something for me but he HAS already done something.

 

Joh 1:14 And the Word was made flesh, and dwelt among us, (and we beheld his glory, the glory as of the only begotten of the Father,) full of grace and truth.

Php 3:8 Yea doubtless, and I count all things but loss for the excellency of the knowledge of Christ Jesus my Lord: for whom I have suffered the loss of all things, and do count them but dung, that I may win Christ,

HOW IMPORTANT TRUTH IS:

            Often we are looking for a certain feeling, but feelings go up and down.

 

The truth is the foundation for true worship.

 

John 16:13 Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide you into all truth: for he shall not speak of himself; but whatsoever he shall hear, that shall he speak: and he will shew you things to come.

 

Ge 1:11 And God said, Let the earth bring forth grass, the herb yielding seed, and the fruit tree yielding fruit after his kind, whose seed is in itself, upon the earth: and it was so.

 

If you want to see revival, worship Jesus.
