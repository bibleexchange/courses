TITLE: Spiritual Landmarks

TEXT: Joshua 18:1-6

 

Every single one of us have a place and a part.

And you must fight and be brave against all evil.

There are many whose hearts and minds are set to protect these 21 men being set out.

 

God is wanting to set some boundaries in our life.

It is time to get direction from God, and receive the experience from God.

Boundaries, memorable experiences. There is no experience better to remember than that which God gives.

 

They wnted to possess the land, but were not sure what just was theres, what the boundaries were. They didn’t really know what was theirs.

 

Israel had to pass through a physical lands by God’s design.

Abraham receive an inheritance not by the Law but by PROMISE.

Every experience in our life is given to us by God. No lasting experience unless God gives it to us. He gives us the experience of life, victory, joy, faith. These things don’t just happen because you hang around church people, they are only the results of Christ’s giving and grace and anointing. We must keep these experiences and not keep the lightly. How long are ye slack to go to possess the Land.
