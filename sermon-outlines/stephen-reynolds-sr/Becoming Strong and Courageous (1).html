Becoming Strong and Courageous

 

Joshua 1:9 Have not I commanded thee? Be strong and of a good courage; be not afraid, neither be thou dismayed: for the LORD thy God is with thee whithersoever thou goest.

 

  1. This is a question to Remind them of His command:

 

  1. Have not I commanded thee?

 

  1. This is the Lord’s Command

 

    1. Be strong

 

    1. Be a good courage

 

    1. Be not afraid

 

    1. Be not dismayed

  1. This is a Reminder to encourage obedience to His command

 

  1. “The LORD thy God is with thee whithersoever thou goest”

 

  1. This is the Lord’s Promise

 

 

    1. The Lord is thy God

 

    1. The Lord thy God is with you

 

    1. The Lord thy God is with you wherever you go

 

 

  1. The Command and the Promise is God’s Word that we must absolutely obey.

 

  1. The Lord is with us to give us Strength and Courage.

 

    1. You are only strong through Him

 

    1. You are only courageous through Him.

 

    1. You are only obedient through Him.

 

“GOOD COURAGE”

 

There is the nerve to rebel against God that we must pray against
