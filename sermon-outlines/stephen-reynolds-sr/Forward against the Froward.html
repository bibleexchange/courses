  1. The Law of Christ
    1. The work of the law revealed.
      1. The design, the purpose, or object of the revealed law;
        1. To make known to man his duty
        2. To enforce the obligation to perform it.

This does not mean, by any means, that they had all the knowledge which the law would impart, but as far as they had knowledge of right and wrong, they learned from the revealed will of God given to Israel engraved in stone, the word written by the Prophets in scrolls and the spoken word by the Prophet’s continual warning.

    1. The Will of God revealed
      1. Not made known by reason but made known to reason with help.
      2. Made known by revelation
