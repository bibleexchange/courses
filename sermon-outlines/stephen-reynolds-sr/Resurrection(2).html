Ps 16:8 “I have set the LORD always before me”

Acts 2:25 “I foresaw the Lord always before my face”

 

  • Jesus as a man in the flesh experienced God's constant presence and power.
  • Jesus always saw God before His face; looked and kept His gaze upon God. He thought upon God, focused His mind and attention upon God. He concentrated and stayed His mind upon Him.
  •  Jesus always Glorified the father and was always conscious of God's presence— (captivating every thought  self exalting  against the knowledge of God 2 Cor. 10:5
  • The Son always had the Father on His right hand, that He should not be moved. Father was right there as an advocate and as a protector and defender. Father was provider looking after Jesus, strengthening, guiding, upholding, seeing that He was not moved nor shaken.
  • He is the defender in court or of a soldier on the battlefield standing at a person's right hand, protecting, looking after, and providing for his welfare. (Psalm 109:31)
