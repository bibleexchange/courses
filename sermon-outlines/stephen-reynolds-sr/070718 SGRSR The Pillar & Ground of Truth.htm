July 18, 2007, Wednesday

TITLE: The Pillar and Ground of the Truth

TEXT: I Timothy 3:15,16

 

The Truth must be understood by the Spirit. The Spirit of God makes us genuine lovers of truth, of God, the souls of men.

 

There are things we must look at to see our responsibility that God has given you. If we see that we will not be so quick to put the blame on someone else.

 

In the work of God you don’t always have a second chance. We are called to holiness. Holiness is not you do the best you can, but is the NATUR EOF GOD. The Holy God we serve does not just demand Holy living that we live properly, but put His holy life in us.

 

Pay attention to the will of God.

 

I have rejoiced much at the grace of God at work which works far beyond us.

 

They that hunger and thirst after righteousness…

 

Seek righteousness, humbleness, meekness, peace

 

What good is right doctrine without peace? My faith is only as good as my motive.

 

THE CHURCH OF GOD IS CALLED:

  • THE HOUSE OF GOD.
  • CHURCH OF THE LIVING GOD.
  • THE PILLAR AND GROUND OF THE TRUTH.

 

Salvation is an experience. Not a learned practice. Our experience is based on faith in God’s grace.
