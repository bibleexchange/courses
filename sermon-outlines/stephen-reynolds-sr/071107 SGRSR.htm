Cursed be he that doeth the work of the LORD deceitfully, and cursed be he that keepeth back his sword from blood.

Yet will I bring again the captivity of Moab in the latter days, saith the LORD. Thus far is the judgment of Moab.

 

The mind of God from the beginning is to have mercy. Knowing they will refuse and reject God still makes the offer.

 

Jeremiah’s face is as an adamant stone when standing condemning sin, but he was known as the WEEPING PROPHET, full of compassion. WITHOUT COMPROMISE.

 

Idolatry in Moab and Israel was judge. Idolatry in America will be judged. America will be judged more severely than Moab because of the truth that has been preached to her. We must not be deceitful, but HOLD NOT BACK but loving with a pure heart fervently hating sin and loving God and the souls of men.

 

With a stroke of His judgment in a short time, God is going to smite the idolatrous. In three short years he brought judgment on Israel and silenced them.
