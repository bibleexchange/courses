Like Precious Faith

2Pe 1:1 Simon Peter, a servant and an apostle of Jesus Christ, to them that have obtained like precious faith with us through the righteousness of God and our Saviour Jesus Christ:

 

  1. Obtained like precious faith
    1. Addressed to them that have obtained like precious faith with us
    2. The righteousness of God and our Saviour Jesus Christ

produces like precious faith.

    1. Like precious faith will produce like precious practice
      1. We find the apostles active in the cause of God.
      2. We find that the apostles' practice in love (very affectionate)—they spoke in love to those who surrounded them.
        1. When you volley with the world are you slamming them back or are you making a difference.
        2. Are you firing on them or are you pulling them out of the fire.
