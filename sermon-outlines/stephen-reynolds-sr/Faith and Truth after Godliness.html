  1. God’s People are challenged

 

Truth is imparted to us by God's Word.

One of the marks of the elect is "the acknowledging of the truth." God's people believe the Bible. The word translated "acknowledging" here indicates "a full or thorough knowledge," "thorough acquaintance." The elect recognize God's Word for what it is and commit themselves to becoming better acquainted with it because "faith cometh by hearing and hearing by the Word of God" (Rom. 10:17).
