PEARL OF GREAT PRICE

Margaret means PEARL

 

Mt 13:46 Who, when he had found one pearl of great price, went and sold all that he had, and bought it.

 Re 21:21 And the twelve gates were twelve pearls; every several gate was of one pearl: and the street of the city was pure gold, as it were transparent glass.

 

Margaret \m(a)-rga-ret, mar-garet\ as a girl's name is pronounced MAR-gret. It is of Greek origin, and the meaning of Margaret is "pearl". A saint's name. Historical: the name of nine queens of England, Scotland, France, and Austria. The name came into use among English speakers via Latin (as Margarita) and OldFrench (as Marguerite).
