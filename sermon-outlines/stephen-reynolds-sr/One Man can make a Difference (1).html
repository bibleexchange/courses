One Man can make a Difference

Luke 22:31—And the Lord said, Simon, Simon, behold, Satan hath desired to have you, that he may sift you as wheat:                                                                                                           32 But I have prayed for thee, that thy faith fail not: and when thou art converted, strengthen thy brethren.

Jude 1:22—And of some have compassion, making a difference:

LEARNING THE DIFFERENCE ONE MAN CAN MAKE

I . There is a need for Intercession.

  1. One man can intercede in prayer.
  2. Learn the difference one man can make though intercessory prayer.

II. The need for Repentance and Faith unto Conversion.

  1. Learn the difference One converted man can make to strengthen others.

Men may see miracles, and receive the greatest temporal mercies, and yet remain enemies to God.  No means, without the converting influences of his Spirit, will ever lead them to love and serve him.

Jude 1:22—And of some have compassion, making a difference:

Make a difference-

  • There is a difference between those who have been led away by weakness and imprudence,

Eph 1:8 Wherein he hath abounded toward us in all wisdom and prudence;

Jer 29:11 For I know the thoughts that I think toward you, saith the LORD, thoughts of peace, and not of evil, to give you an expected end.

Heb 5:2 Who can have compassion on the ignorant, and on them that are out of the way; for that he himself also is compassed with infirmity.

  • And those who, in the pride and arrogance of their hearts, and their unwillingness to submit to wholesome discipline, have separated themselves from the Church, and become its case-hardened enemies.
