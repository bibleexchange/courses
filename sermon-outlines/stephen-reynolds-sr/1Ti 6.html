Pr 11:3 ¶ The integrity of the upright shall guide them: but the perverseness of transgressors shall destroy them.

Pr 11:5 ¶ The righteousness of the perfect shall direct his way: but the wicked shall fall by his own wickedness.

Integrity is innocence, upright is straight; a sure guide; it leads men by a safe path, and brings them out in a safe place.

Integrity is steadfastly adhering to high moral principles

His principles are fixed, His rule is certain, and His way is plain; his sincerity keeps him steady, and he needs not talk about every time the wind blows, having no other end to drive at than to keep a good conscience.  Integrity and uprightness will guide and preserve men,

Ps 25:21 Let integrity and uprightness preserve me; for I wait on thee.

Integrity is being innocent and upright is walking straight
