He Staggered Not At The Promise of God

Rom 4:20

"He staggered not at the promise"

 

  1. What Does It Mean To Stagger?

diakrino,  dee-ak-ree'-no

to separate thoroughly, to withdraw from, oppose;, to discriminate (decide), hesitate contend, make (to) differ(-ence), discern, doubt, judge, be partial, stagger, waver.

 

To stagger means to:

separate thoroughly  withdraw from   oppose   hesitate   doubt   be partial   waver

make adifference from the Promise; decide against the Promise; contend against Promises discriminate against the Promise   Judge against the Promise; waver from the Promise

Stagger not means to

  • Judge so that you separate your thinking thoroughly from the problem, focus only on the Promise, everyone of the Promises are for you that will believe.
  • Don’t Be partial; to any one Promise; Receive the exceeding great and precious Promises.
    • Salvation, Deliverance, Healing, Justification, forgiveness, Humbling, Purifying.
    • Baptism of the Holy Ghost
    • Sanctification – The continual working of His Grace, Truth and Power
    • Glorification – The complete work of His Grace, Truth and Power
  • Don’t hesitate to believe God’s Promises.
  • Will not vacillate between two opinions.
  • Discern without doubting the Promise.
  • Judge without wavering from the Promise.
  • Contend without withdrawing from the Promise.
