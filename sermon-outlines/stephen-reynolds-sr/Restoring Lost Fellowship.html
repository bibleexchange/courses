Restoring Lost Fellowship

S.O.S. 3:1-4

Finding Him for someone else?  

 

Finding Him by His Grace through faith that we might by His power, share our experience with others.

 

  1. There is a need within us all to search for Him until we be found of Him.

 

2Peter 3:14 Wherefore, beloved, seeing that ye look for such things, be diligent that ye may be found of him in peace, without spot, and blameless.

Jer 3:10 And yet for all this her treacherous sister Judah hath not turned unto me with her whole heart, but feignedly, saith the LORD.

Jer 29:13 And ye shall seek me, and find me, when ye shall search for me with all your heart

 

Romans 10:20 But Esaias is very bold, and saith, I was found of them that sought me not; I was made manifest unto them that asked not after me.

 21 But to Israel he saith, All day long I have stretched forth my hands unto a disobedient and gainsaying people.

Ps 119:2 Blessed are they that keep his testimonies, and that seek him with the whole heart.

 

1John 1:3 That which we have seen and heard declare we unto you, that ye also may have fellowship with us: and truly our fellowship is with the Father, and with his Son Jesus Christ.

    1. WE always get separated when the Holy Ghost is grieved.
    2. The Holy Ghost is grieved when our fellowship is broken.
    3. Things have a way in separating us.
    4. She sought Him because she deeply feels her need.
    5. The more precious and real He is to us the more deeply we feel the loss.

(Many do not recognize these obvious things you point out on your paper because of their findings)

My prayer is that our finding Him will always be to restore fellowship with Him and others.

  1. The time we need to search for Him.     “By Night”
    1. When we first feel our loss; at the first sense of darkness, at the very first sign.
    2. It is always night when He is not there for fellowship.
    3. Distance always implies darkness, anything can darken our experience, He is the Light.
    4. Don’t wait so long to seek Him with all your heart; Find Him to restore fellowship, lest you lose your sense of need.
  2. The manner of our own Searching’s.
    1. She sought Him on her bed  – “On my bed”
    2. She sought Him on the streets – walks, wanderings, work, witnessing and worship

In society (street meetings as well)

    1. She sought Him among the “Watchmen”
      1. Most Watchmen of today are not at all in touch with Him.
      2. Few Watchmen In fellowship with Him, are sensitive to the deepest needs.
