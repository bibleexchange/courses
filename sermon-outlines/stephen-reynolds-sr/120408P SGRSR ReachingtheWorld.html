(Testimonies and thoughts in memorial of the life of Rev. Clayton Alward.

A Five Star General in the Army of God. Great encourager of the saints and soul winner for the Kingdom of Heaven.)

 

TITLE: Reaching the World TEXT: Matthew 24:14

 

Matthew 24:14 And this gospel of the kingdom shall be preached in all the world for a witness unto all nations; and then shall the end come.

 

I Peter 4:6 For for this cause was the gospel preached also to them that are dead, that they might be judged according to men in the flesh, but live according to God in the spirit.

 

God can give you the ability to reach a nation, but we also believe God to reach a person.

 

I Peter 4:12

 

Arm yourself with the same mind of Christ--this Gospel must be preached.

 

Matthew 11:4 Jesus answered and said unto them, Go and shew John again those things which ye do hear and see: 5 The blind receive their sight, and the lame walk, the lepers are cleansed, and the deaf hear, the dead are raised up, and the poor have the gospel preached to them. 6 And blessed is he, whosoever shall not be offended in me.

 

Preach the Gospel to the poor.

Preach the Gospel in my neighborhood.
