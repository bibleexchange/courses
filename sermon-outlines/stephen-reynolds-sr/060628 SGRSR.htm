Rev. James R. Reynolds, Sr.

June 7, 2006

Wednesday Night

Title: An Urgency and Hunger for the Lord to Work

Text:  Psalm 119:126

 

Pay attention to every moment. Even to the seconds, what is God saying and what is God allotting us. Men have chosen to make void the 6,000 y ears we have already been allotted. Men cannot stop time.

 

Eph 3:9 And to make all men see what is the fellowship of the mystery, which from the beginning of the world hath been hid in God, who created all things by Jesus Christ: Make – to shed rays, i.e. to shine

 

Believe God and His Word. Without the Word of God, darkness will set in. The devil will find a way to discourage and pull you down.

Psalm 119:121 àI need the Lord, I need His Word and His help. Believe God to do what we cannot do and what He has promised. Let God be the most wanted person in your life.

Psalm 119:1 His Word was as law. It cannot be options or suggestions or it won’t change you. The word of God produces the order of an undefiled life.

Psalm 119:34 Give me understanding, and I shall keep thy law; yea, I shall observe it with my whole heart. If there is no urgency I will never have a WHOLE HEART experience.That understanding comes by revelation as I believe and obey what I already know. Don’t complain about what I don’t understand, but believe God’s Word.

 

The Word is not just LAW but TESTIMONY/WITNESS.

Psalm 119:119à Our God is great, good, wonderful, and marvelous. We are living testimonies of what God can do in a life that will believe, follow, and serve Him.

Psalm 119:14 Does what God has done in my life mean more to me than ANYTHING and EVERYTHING else???

 

Ephesians 1:18 The Riches of His glory, 1:12,14 Faith and trust and confidence in God It is Time for the Lord to Work. Before this work can go on, thee is a stripping of our heart. Getting rid of the flesh and carnality. Getting rid of self.
