Title: Spiritual Wisdom

Text: Job 28:12

 

There are two kinds of wisdom: Wisdom of Man/Natural & Wisdom from God/Spiritual

 

Job 28:1

 

Spiritual wisdom is priceless. Vv.15-19

Better than anything in this earth.

He is comparing wisdom with the mining of gold, brass, silver. It takes a lot of work to get these out of the earth. More than even gold of Ophir.

 

God understands the way and He understands the price.

 

Wisdom is precious and costly.

 

God’s Wisdom is Perfect
