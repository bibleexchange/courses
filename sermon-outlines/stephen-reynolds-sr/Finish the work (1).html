This anointing put a mighty soldier on his feet in diligent search of every spiritual weapon he would need to accomplish what the call had set before him!

     

     TO REACH THAT SET GOAL!!!

        Then came the proclamation that even the regions of Demons and the Devil could hear. As well as fellow soldiers who were hungry to be encouraged in their fight.

        He often shouted mighty and strong with fist clinched and raised purposefully HIGH in the air!

         Never heard him even "Whisper" the language of defeat though we watched him carry the bodies of many fallen comrades and press on. The voice of this great warrior could be heard saying for all to hear. We cannot Stop now. We must reach the Goal! Now, FINISH THE WORK

     

    • Is that the heat of the desert I feel pressing around me he would ask? But then instantly the determination and anointing would say; None the matter. I will trust for his refreshing, I must fight on!
    • Is that the cold of the winter pressing hard against me he would ask? But then here came the same resolve! It never wavered. He again would say; None the matter. I will look for the warmth of his Love, I must fight on!
    • Is that the darkness of the night pressing on me as he would call for Annabell or one of his son’s to bring him a light. We cannot stop now we must press on, we must reach the goal, we must fight on.
    • Is that the evening shadows falling?
    • Is that the sun coming up , if he wasn’t under a vehicle he was on his knees praying, and in both cases some had the priviledge of being there with him many times and we have precious memories, but best of all we have that mind for Reaching the Goal, now, finish His work,  

     

    FIGHT THE GOOD FIGHT OF FAITH

     

        Are they the rocks of the steep mountainside that are cutting and hurting my feet he would ask? The resolve never failed! Again he would say;MAKE MY FEET LIKE HINDS FEET I will find the healing of his Grace to make it to the TOP! I Must Fight On!

     

        How many attacks did he endure?         He didn’t count     Answer:      All of them

    How many injuries were suffered for long periods of time, even while never leaving the battle field?

    That number could never be accounted, because he covered most wounds and injuries with a crisp clean uniform, usually refreshed in a closet of fasting and prayer.

    When he appeared he made sure that he was a representative of his faith in an unfailing God, and never let his wounds produce a mortal pity from man that would in any degree reduce the character of His God!

        

    The steps made slow by the passing of Mrs. James Reynolds! The fist not raised quite as high, and the voice may not thunder! But that does not mean he was loosing the long hard fought battle! NO! He was just preparing for the final victory! The Goal was always in Sight! He prepared for the final war with this assault! A foe he has now faced! An enemy against the body perishing but the victory of the eternal soul will be revealed over all Death!

     

        This is where he and the angels must take on the enemy.

     

    He finally sees the Master of faith coming and saying to him! " No need to give up now" Then he whispers! I want the last word!!!

     

    The family and friends may not of heard it but the final words as he was led across chilly Jordon were " Oh Death, Where is Your Sting " I have reached for the set goal.
