Luke 24:49 And, behold, I send the promise of my Father upon you: but tarry ye in the city of Jerusalem, until ye be endued with power from on high.

 

“Tarry...Until”

 

Salvation is received only through the prayer of faith.

Sanctification is received only through the prayer of faith.

Sermons are made through prayerful study but seasoned with anointed prayer.

 

The unction of the Holy Ghost comes by faith in the promise through much prayer.

The unction cannot be learned, but received as the Holy Ghost comes upon you.

 

The unction must be experienced to be explained and expressed.

The unction is not received unless the unction is upon you.

The unction is not lived unless the unction is within you.

 

Victory is won or lost in prayer before you ever say anything.

 

The prayer of faith will put selfishness to death.
