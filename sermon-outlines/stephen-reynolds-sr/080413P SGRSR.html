TITLE: Every Degree of Movement, God is Watching

TEXT:

 

Every degree of movement, God is watching and trying. His countenance doth behold the upright.

 

v.2 privily, lurking in some dark places shooting at the upright in heart. There eyes are set on the upright to destroy them, but God has His eyes set to deliver. Keep your eyes on the Lord, and allow the Lord to establish hope and strength and courage.

 

People are in and they are out, but the Lord says that ought not to be. When we hear His and Word and Know His will, there should be hesitance.

 

You will never be without trial in this life.
