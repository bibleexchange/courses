Eph 1:18 The eyes of your understanding being enlightened; that ye may know what is the hope of his calling, and what the riches of the glory of his inheritance in the saints,

 

  1. Newness of life
    1. effectual knowledge
      1. To know upon some mark
        1. So to recognize, perceive, discern
      2. To become fully acquainted with

 

Newness of life consists in knowledge which transforms man to the image of God his maker, that is to say to the sincerity and pureness of the whole soul.

 

  • He speaks of an effectual knowledge.
