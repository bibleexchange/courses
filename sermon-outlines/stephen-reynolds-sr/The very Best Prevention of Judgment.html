The very Best Prevention of Judgment

Because it is The ONLY Prevention of the Judgment of God

Isaiah 32:17 And the work of righteousness shall be peace; and the effect of righteousness quietness and assurance for ever.

 

Don’t wait for Judgment to then seek peace and then try to quiet yourself down, start now.

 

The very best Prevention of Judgment is let God work His Righteousness in your heart now and forever.

 

  1. The Work of Righteousness is what God does in a believer’s life.

  “And”            The Lord wants to add to our faith His Righteousness

“and” more and more of His Righteousness.

 

             “work” He works by His Grace that we through faith might receive His Righteousness.

 

His work of Righteousness is a work of Grace that produces the right effects through Faith.

 

“The Work “Of” Righteousness”     The Work “Of” God      The Gift “of” God     “Of” the Right Work

  1. An Inward Peace---(The Peace given and received within as a great gift from God as righteousness is imputed through faith)
  2. Inward experiences outwardly Expressed---Quietness and assurance

 

Romans 8:28 And we know that all things work together for good to them that love God, to them who are the called according to his purpose.

 

His work of Righteousness is to produce a Righteous effect in us.

 

  1. The Effect of Righteousness is what the believer experiences as he corrects his own life by fully relying on the Grace and Truth of God through diligent faith.

 

    1. Purity of Heart
      1. Holiness
      2. Perfection
    2. Conformity of Heart
      1. Faithfulness---Unwavering in Character and actions.
      2. Exactness---Righteous in Character and actions
      3. Integrity ---Sound and quiet in character and in actions.

 

  1. The Fruit of Righteousness is Peace, Quietness and Assurance
    1. Peace
    2. Quietness
    3. Assurance

There cannot be True lasting Peace without the Righteousness of God.

Righteousness is The ONLY Prevention of the Judgment of God
