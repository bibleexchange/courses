TITLE: A New Determination to Serve the Lord

TEXT: Habakkuk 2:1-3

 

Our eyes are on the Lord and He is in control. He is kind, loving, true, Savior, master. But we are not always going to feel this security physically, we won’t always hear God speaking to us but will pass through times of silence.

How would you describe this world? Chaotic, out of control, lost, sinful, in unrest.

We must live a life of determination, having a made up mind. There is nothing out of God’s control; He never loses His place.

Habakkuk 2:1 “I WILL STAND.”

Psalm 73 The judgment of God will one day visit the wicked. There seems to be no hardship or sorrow for the wicked, but the judgment of God WILL one day come. God will never forsake His own. We need a new determination to serve God in a dark, difficult world.

 

DETERMINED:

  • unwavering faith
  • a made-up mind
  • a quality of mind that is set up with boundaries and limits

 

DETERMINATION:

  • To live within those set limits
  • ACTION; How you live your life

 

à The Word is simple for those who are determined to serve God.ß

 

Habakkuk 2:4 If you live your life upright before God, your natural senses are not to be trusted. Above your natural senses we must rely upon faith. Trust, Believe, Fear God.

Proverbs 8:9

 

Be simple concerning evil. Believe God.

Proverbs 14:6 --A scorner seeketh wisdom, and findeth it not: but knowledge is easy unto him that understandeth.

A new determination to KNOW God better.
