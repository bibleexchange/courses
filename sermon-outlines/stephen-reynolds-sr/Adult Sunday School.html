  1. Gnosticism

 

What does the heresy of Gnosticism deny and what does it teach?

  1. Denies that faith is essential to salvation.
  2. Teaches that knowledge is essential to salvation.

Gnosis(NO-sis) - knowledge rather than Pistis-Faith

Gnosticism is not by faith but intellectual, so-called rational knowledge but in fact irrational.

A Gnostic concept is more subtle than most cults in that it unites Monotheism, Polytheism, Theism, Deism and Pantheism.

The teachings of Gnosticism have continued to plague the church down through the centuries. In one form or another similar teachings are always being used to attack the church.

There is no better way to counteract false teaching than to present the truth. The false teaching was an attack against Jesus Christ, against both His work and Person. Therefore, Paul proclaims God and the Person of Christ.

b.  There is one Lord Jesus Christ, only One Lord who stands supreme between God and man.
