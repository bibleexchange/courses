The ultimate test of Faith is not just to have the (courage) to live but to glorify God.

 

Faith is not just the (strength) to try but Obedience to God’s Word that overcomes.

Faith operates in the Power of God and in the knowledge of His Son Jesus Christ.

Faith in Jesus Christ is the Victory that causes one to overcome with patience in all things.

 

Have Faith for the great sorrow of life, and patience for the smaller ones; and when you have obediently accomplished your daily task, Rest in peace.  God is Alive, God is Alert, God is Aware.

 

Faith knows what to do and Faith knows what not to do.

 

To want to live is an act of courage, but to Glorify God is Faith.

 

Ps 25:12 What man is he that feareth the LORD? him shall he teach in the way that he shall choose.
