Preach the Gospel                                                                                                              Mark 16:15-18

Preach

To herald  the Gospel (as a public crier), especially divine truth):--preacher, proclaim, publish, declare, Demonstration of the Holy Ghost                                                                                                             Protest solemnly

1 Sam. 8:9 Now therefore hearken unto their voice: howbeit yet protest solemnly unto them, and shew them the manner of the king that shall reign over them.

 1 Cor. 15:31 I protest by your rejoicing which I have in Christ Jesus our Lord, I die daily.

Ps 94:16 Who will rise up for me against the evildoers? or who will stand up for me against the workers of iniquity?

  1. REASON TO PREACH

What are the reasons for which we preach?

  1. God chose preaching as the method to relay the truth of the gospel.
  2. That people may know and understand the gospel.

 

Why Preaching?

 Ac 28:31 Preaching the kingdom of God, and teaching those things which concern the Lord Jesus Christ, with all confidence, no man forbidding him.
