“put them in remembrance”

 

The apostle's argument, why we should not contend about these things; because they tend to beget strife and contention among Christians, by dividing them into factions and parties, and also tend to the subversion of the hearers, causing them to doubt of the truth of the faith, about which the contending parties cannot agree;

 

 "Charge them therefore, that they strive not about words to no profit, but to the subverting of the hearers."

 

The solemn charge given to Timothy, as to the matter, manner, and method of his preaching;

 

  1. The matter of it be the word of truth, the pure word of God,
  2. The manner of preaching that it be divided rightly, to every one his portion, to every hearer his due,
  3. The Method
    1. methodizing and distributing truth, as God would have it; terror to whom terror is due, comfort to whom comfort belongs.
