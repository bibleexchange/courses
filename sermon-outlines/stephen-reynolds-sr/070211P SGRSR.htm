Deliverance Center – Sunday Evening

Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

February 11, 2007

 

TITLE: With His Promise Comes Power

TEXT: Mark 9:23,24

 

INTRODUCTION

Many Pray but do not pray in faith.

There is a power that comes in BELIEVING THE NAME OF JESUS.

Circumstances do not produce faith but are merely opportunities for us to exercise our faith in believing God.

There is a promise of power and it is to everyone that believes. V.23

We need the power, we need what God can do even in this service. God is requiring us to believe Him. With the promise comes a manifestation of power to help us yield and believe Him. Jesus wants all his disciples to know that He is the answer to everything and anything.

We are POWERLESS because we have been PRAYERLESS.

 

There is a spirit of depression getting in our hearts.

Discouragement is a sin. We must exalt Christ and Believe Him.

Surrender my will to God. Do not hold onto my own will. Don’t go and do, say IF THE LORD WILL. Find out what His will is.
