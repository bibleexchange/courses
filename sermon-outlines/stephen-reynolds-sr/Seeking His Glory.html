Seeking His Glory

The Glorious Inheritance

Seeking That God would Glorify His Son in Us

John 7: 17 If any man will do his will, he shall know of the doctrine, whether it be of God, or whether I speak of myself.                                                        18 He that speaketh of himself seeketh his own glory: but he that seeketh his glory that sent him, the same is true, and no unrighteousness is in him.

  • The true doctrine of salvation sets forth the glory of God.
  • False doctrine dishonors the glory of God.

The true doctrine of salvation differs from the false in this, that the true doctrine sets forth the glory of God, and the false doctrine by puffing up men, dishonours the glory of God.

“If any man wilt do his will”

  1. I will give you a sure rule by which ye may judge of my doctrine:
    1. If you really will do the will of God,
      1. begin the practice of it;
      2. take my doctrine, and apply it to all that you know God requires of man;
    2. he shall know of the doctrine

“He that speaketh of himself”

  1. I will give you another rule,
    1. whereby you shall know whether I am from God or not:
      1. If I speak so as to procure my own glory, to gratify vanity, or to secure and promote my secular interests, then reject me as a deceiver and as a false prophet.
      2. But if I act only to promote the glory of God, to induce all men to love and obey him;
      3. if I propose nothing but what leads to the perfection of his law, and the accomplishment of its ordinances, you cannot help acknowledging me at least for a true prophet;
      4. if you add to this the proofs which I have given of my mission and power, you must acknowledge me as the mighty power of God, and the promised Messiah.
