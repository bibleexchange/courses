TITLE:

TEXT: Galatians 5:1

 

Ga 5:1

Stand fast therefore in the liberty wherewith Christ hath made us free, and be not entangled again with the yoke of bondage.

 

 

Rom. 8:2
For the law of the Spirit of life in Christ Jesus hath made me free from the law of sin and death.

 

  • When the law in our heart and the Law of God and the LAW WORKING IN OUR LIVES agree, there is LIBERTY!
  • You entangle yourself very quickly if you don’t give your heart to Jesus.
  • God will have no rival with our affections.
  • If we don’t make the commitment now to serve God no matter what we won’t stand when the opposition comes.
