TITLE: Finishing the Unfinished Responsibilities

TEXT: Zechariah 9:17

 

God wants to do something in us so He can do something with us.

Knowing Jesus and having Him reveal Himself to you is life-changing.

The task, responsibity of what God requires of me.

Some people have it hard difficulty just to make it to church.

 

In these last days is required that which is beyond the ordinary. We have a responsibility to His Word.

 

Zechariah 1:13 -And the LORD answered the angel that talked with me with good words and comfortable words. <<God is not interested in you laying in a bed of easee and not finishing your responsibility ahead of you. The task is great and great strenght will be required of the Lord. We must not be at ease always allowing others to bear the burden and do the work. We have a responsibility to God until He shows His will to us and reveals Himself in us.

 

His Word is good because it comes in the fashion that we NEED it. God’s good Word won’t always make you just feel good at first. It comes as we NEED it and not necessarily how we desire it. And it is effectual in its work.

 

9:17 We have a responsibility to receive a revelation of Who He is and for that to change us. What is it that will put a joy inn our soul and not quit in the night hour? It is going to be the Word of God given to us that will sustain us.

 

ENDURANCE, SUSTAIN, CONTINUANCEàKEY. Not just the quick, fast, feel good song or exciting emotions. And the Word of God revealed to us is the key to our LONG DISTANCE success.

 

10:1--Ask ye of the LORD rain in the time of the latter rain; so the LORD shall make bright clouds, and give them showers of rain, to every one grass in the field.

We must pray. We must know Him, His promises, His Word, but it is going to take a fervent and prayer life. We must plant seed and we must water it with prayer. Pray and not faint…catch the burden. Extraordinary prayer. It is going to take an extraordinary prayer life in order for God to work in finishing that which has been started.

John Mark & Peter’s Work ACTS 12

Us & Measuring up to the Stature of the Fulness of Christ Zech. 10:1

Do what it takes to pray through until it happens. Extraordinary prayer to help the unity of the Spirit and then to keep it. And then to come to the unity of the faith. Eph. 4:3,13 If people are going to believe they must be influenced by the Spirit of God and the Word and not just our own zeal and excitement.

 

Zechariah 8:19 --Thus saith the LORD of hosts; The fast of the fourth month, and the fast of the fifth, and the fast of the seventh, and the fast of the tenth, shall be to the house of Judah joy and gladness, and cheerful feasts; therefore love the truth and peace. The joy is to those that know what the fast is all about. That understand the greatness of His love, goodness, and beauty. 8:20,21 I SENSE MY NEED TO SEEK THE LORD. LET US. There is a unity that is needed. Matthew 18:20

 

Let us go on the strength of the Word that God has revealed to us. Inconsistent results in some places becauase of excitement inspired action with no revealed Word. 8:22,23—God still reveals Himself to the Jews even though their heart is hard. I will live an unfinished life until the Spirit of God comes upon me as he desires.
