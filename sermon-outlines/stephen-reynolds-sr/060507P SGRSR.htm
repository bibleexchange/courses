Title: A Simple Night of Victory

Text: Judges 7:19

 

My life can be a cause for revival.

It doesn’t have to be a long hard fought battle or complicated but we can know victory tonight.

Grace works through faith.

We need a Word from God to bring us to that place of victory.

 

LITTLE CHILDREN: those that know God, but don’t know Him as they should.

 

When you know Him and see Him there will be a cry in your heart to be like Him.

 

2 Cor. 4:6 It doesn’t have to be difficult, complicated, or a long night of struggling and wrestling.

Is it night? God has commanded the light to shine out of darkness. Genesis 1:3

 

I must stay prayed up and keep a continually open channel between me and the Lord in order for me to avoid pitfalls and remain out of personal controversy.

 

We need to avoid the place where we do whatever we feel or want, but back to “DO AS I DO”

 

The dark night is coming. I JOHN 1:1 He bore our sin, shame, suffering, and was rejected of God: there was no light in that. The day was turned to night for 3 hours.

OBEY and BELIEVE GOD. Acts 5:29 Gideon got direction from God. He was following God. Following Gideon was therefore the same was following God for those men in Judges.

DON’T EVER GET OUT OF THE WILL OF GOD. YOU GET OUT OF THE WILL OF GOD, AND YOU DON’T JUST GET BACK IN.

It is not simple with disobedience, but it is with OBEDIENCE.

 

Try to do great feats and I will fail. Keep it simple: Obey God.

Gen 32 It wasn’t so much Jacob wrestling with the angel as the angel was wrestling with him.

2 Cor. 8:9; 9:8 Grace requires faith.
