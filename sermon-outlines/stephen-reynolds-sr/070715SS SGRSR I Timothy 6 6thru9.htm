TITLE:

TEXT: I Timothy 6:6-9

 

There are some things to learn from Paul’s exhortation to Timothy whether you are called to ‘preach’ or not. All Christians must live it. There is not a separate standard of loftier expectations as far as God is concerned for a preacher as from a Lay Christian.

 

1Ti 6:6-9

 But godliness with contentment is great gain.

7 For we brought nothing into this world, and it is certain we can carry nothing out.

8 And having food and raiment let us be therewith content.

But they that will be rich fall into temptation and a snare, and into many foolish and hurtful lusts, which drown men in destruction and perdition.

 

I as a Christian need to work. The best worker in town. The best worker on the job.
