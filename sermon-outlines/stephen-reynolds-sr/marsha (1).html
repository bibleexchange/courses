EPISTLES III

STUDY QUESTIONS-II PETER

 

14. Name and describe one hard scripture that you have learned and you can teach someone else.

 

(This question comes from II Peter 3:15-16) because it mentions hard things).

2Peter 3:15—And account that the longsuffering of our Lord is salvation; even as our beloved brother Paul also according to the wisdom given unto him hath written unto you;

 16—As also in all his epistles, speaking in them of these things; in which are some things hard to be understood, which they that are unlearned and unstable wrest, as they do also the other scriptures, unto their own destruction.

 

 

SPIRITUAL LIFE DEMANDS GROWTH

2 Peter 3:18—But grow in grace, and in the knowledge of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ. To him be glory both now and for ever. Amen.

 

 

“But” means: (other than, on the contrary)

 

2Peter 3:17—Ye therefore, beloved, seeing ye know these things before, beware lest ye also, being led away with the error of the wicked, fall from your own stedfastness.

 

  1. We must beware of two things

 

    1. “being led away with the error of the wicked”

 

    1. “fall from your own stedfastness”

 

 

  1. We must grow in two things

 

    1. “Grace”

 

    1. “Knowledge”

 

  1. We must glorify God in our lives

 

    1. “Now”

 

    1. “and Forever”
