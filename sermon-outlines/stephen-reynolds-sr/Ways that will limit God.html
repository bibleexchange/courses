Avoid Ways that will limit God

Heb 3:10 Wherefore I was grieved with that generation, and said, They do alway err in their heart; and they have not known my ways.

 

Faith in His Promises will limitless you from believing and receiving from God.

 

Limitless – (unlimited, unbounded, infinite, unending in receiving from God)

 

Faith is a green light to allow the Lord to work unlimited in your life.

Unbelief is a red light that stops God from working in your life.

 

Three major ways that limit God in your life.

 

  1. Not yielding because of being misinformed, a misunderstanding or misappropriating the promises will limit God in your life, for many reasons when faith is neglected.
    1. Being misinformed leads to deception.
    2. Misunderstanding leads to error
    3. Misappropriating leads to waywardness
  2. Unbelief or Ignorance will harden your heart from receiving from God.
  3. Having been in error, then remain feeling guilty, remain unworthy and unfaithful will limit God.
