The Cup My Father gave me

 

John 18:11 Then said Jesus unto Peter, Put up thy sword into the sheath: the cup which my Father hath given me, shall I not drink it?

 

  1. A Drinking Cup

 

    1. A Cup of Sorrows
      1. Bear the sin of the whole world
      2. Bear the penalty of the sin of the whole world

 

    1. A Cup of Sufferings
      1. It Is Bitter
      2. It is full
        1. It shall be empty
          1. He shall drink it all
          2. He shall drink it for all who would believe
    2. A Cup of Salvation
      1. He drank the punishment of our sin that we might have Joy
      2. He drank the wrath of God that we might have life
        1. He gave His life for a sacrifice for our sin that we might be saved
          1. Through death He destroyed the power of death
          2. Though death He destroyed the works of the devil.
        2. He offered His disciples to drink of it
        3. He offered it to Peter now

 

  1. An  unsheathed sword

 

    1. A Sword of the flesh—selfishness
      1. A sword of self righteousness
        1. Sword fighting instead of cup bearing
      2. A sword of revenge
    2. A sword lifted up instead of a cup lifted and drank
      1. “Put up thy sword into the sheath”

 

  1. The sword sheathed

 

    1. The Cup offered and drank

“shall I not drink it?”

 

      1. Salvation gained
      2. Salvation preached

 

        1. The cup is for the humble
        2. The cup is for the righteous
        3. The cup is for the faithful
