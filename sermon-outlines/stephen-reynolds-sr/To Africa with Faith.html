God has opened the door in Tanzania Africa for the Deliverance Bible Institute to go forth and help teach them the gospel, that God would call and make many of them His disciples.

Africa is the world’s second largest and second most populous continent after Asia, covering 20% of the world’s total land area, and home to 14% of the world’s human population, yet remains the world’s poorest and most underdeveloped continent.

It has been prophesied that God will show His whole hand in Tanzania Africa.
