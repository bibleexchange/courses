Willard Warren Rice Jr., 63, of Casco, passed away on Nov. 21, 2012, at Maine General in Augusta following a courageous battle with acute melanoid leukemia.

Billy was born on Nov. 17, 1949, in Portland, the son of Willard Warren Rice Jr. and Evelyn Mary (Buotte) Rice Brockett. He attended schools in Portland and Boston. 

He married Margaret Long in 1974 and had two daughters, Joanne Lynn Rice and Donna Marie Rice. He then went on to have another daughter, Lisa Marie Olsen with Tracy Olsen- Curran. He loved and adored his daughters, family, and girlfriend and soulmate, Betty Willett. 

He was predeceased by his father, his mother; sisters, Jean G. Chisson and Judy A. Rich; and brother-in-law, Lawrence C. Rich.

 

He is survived by his daughters, Joanne Lynn Rice, Donna Marie Rice and Lisa Marie Olsen; sister, Virginia A. Whittier; girlfriend Betty Willett; seven grandchildren; nieces and nephews. 

Relatives and friends are invited to attend a memorial service at 1 p.m. on Dec. 11, at Evergreen Chapel in Evergreen Cemetery, 672 Stevens Ave., Portland. Interment will follow in Forest City Cemetery, 232 Lincoln St., South Portland. A celebration of life will be held at a later date.
