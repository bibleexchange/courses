TITLE: Know Know

TEXT: I John 2:1-3

 

He has a burden fro these people like he would a little child. Here it is considered an honor to be considered a child of the apostle John. John is not in any measure putting them down, or putting them in some level that is below him. “you are down here and I am up here.” He is recognizing that there is power and authority in his life so that he would be able to help somebody. These are words of encouragement ad challenge, and not a put down to distress or discourage.

I would be a fool to take upon myself the credit for the respect given to me by people. The glory and honor goes to God, our part is to act as faithful stewards of the trust and respect God has blessed us with. The POWER IS WITH GOD!!! There is power and authority from the Word of God that helps us to meet the challenge. “MY little children these things I write unto thee that ye sin not.’ “That ye commit no ‘no, no’s.’ God wants to help us but we must learn how to let Him. The Word of God and the Christian life is a paradox to the carnal mind. God will give you wisdom and insight to understand His word. His word is not hard to understand in a Christian Experience as you obey. There is a source and strength in God that we do not have to sin. You are human; you must recognize the dangers of sin. They teach, “You are human you have to sin a little everyday.”

APPLY the Word to my life. We don’t want death; we want life. The law itself cannot change us. Grace is the means to save us, transform us, and make like His Son. Obey want you do know.

We are going to use the phrase KNOW KNOW. Know what you know.

Your flesh will never subject itself to the truth in pure motive.

I John 2:3 KNOW that we KNOW. Not just knowledge in our mind. How do we know that we know? IF WE KEEP HIS COMMANDMENTS. Keeping His commandments is going to help us KNOW Him, and know that we know. Philippians 3:10.
