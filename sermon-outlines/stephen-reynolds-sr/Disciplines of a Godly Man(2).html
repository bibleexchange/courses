    Disciplines for God-given duty of Godly Men

    God created man—male and female, this is God’s Divine order. He created the man from the dust of the ground; God took a rib from Adam and made Eve. God created man to govern; He created the woman to be his helper. God gave commandment regarding what to eat in the garden, He commanded the man. The man had the responsibility to communicate the command to the woman and then to the children.

    There is an undeniable order in creation. By creating man first, God did not bestow upon man privileges or superiority, but rather He bestowed responsibility and service. Men have the responsibility of leadership in the home, church, community, city, nation, and the world . The proper performance of this duty demands a life of integrity, purity, passion and power that result from following Christ. Only as a man walks with Jesus, his head and master, will he be able to fulfill the role that God has given him.

    In this ungodly, foolish age there is a clarion call for Godly men to take a stand for true worship. There is the need through the knowledge of the Truth of Jesus Christ to bring men to the awareness of the Biblical responsibility and disciplines of manhood at home, in the church and abroad; and as you see the holy calling we further desire that you may receive grace with the Truth of the Word to fulfill your God-given duty.
