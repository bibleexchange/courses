The snare of the devil. in this connection the snare operated through the distorting of doctrine in men's minds, leading them through a maze of reasonings having the form of godliness but not the reality (2Ti 3:5).  See Eph 4:14; 6:11; also Ro 1:21,25; 1Co 8:13; 2Co 10:5.
