    Error of the heart

    Psalms 95:10 Forty years long was I grieved with this generation, and said, It is a people that do err in their heart, and they have not known my ways:

    Heb 3:10 Wherefore I was grieved with that generation, and said, They do alway err in their heart; and they have not known my ways.                                                                                                    17 But with whom was he grieved forty years? was it not with them that had sinned, whose carcases fell in the wilderness?

    The causes of their sin.  

    1. See what God imputed it to:
      1.  It is a people that do err in their hearts,
        1. and they have not known my ways.
          1. Men's unbelief and distrust of God,
          2. their murmurings and quarrels with him, are the effect of their ignorance and mistake.
            1. First, Of their ignorance:
              1. They have not known my ways.  
              2. They saw his work (Ps 95:9) and he made known his acts to them (Ps 103:7); and yet they did not know his ways, the ways of his providence, in which he walked towards them,
              3. or the ways of his commandments, in which he would have them to walk towards him:
                1. they did not know,
                2. they did not rightly understand
                3. and therefore did not approve of these.

    The reason why people slight and forsake the ways of God is because they do not know them.

            1. Their sin –what some call their mistake: many say its okay.
              1. They do err in their heart;
              2. they wander out of the way;
              3. in heart they turn back.

    Sins are errors, practical errors, errors in heart; as fatal as errors in the head. When corrupt affections pervert the judgment that God shows, sin leads the soul out of the ways of duty and obedience, there is an error of the heart.
