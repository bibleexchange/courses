TITLE: A Change for the Better

TEXT: Isaiah 55:13

 

We have every way to make and no chance to fail if we trust God.

Put your trust in God and guarantee victory. God deals in changes. He deals with our will and allows us to make the choice instead of just making us a certain way at the start.

 

God is looking for growth and development in our life. He will not cut us off if we obey and yield to Him.

 

God is true to His Word.

 

Isaiah 5:4

What could have been done more to my vineyard, that I have not done in it? wherefore, when I looked that it should bring forth grapes, brought it forth wild grapes?

And now go to; I will tell you what I will do to my vineyard: I will take away the hedge thereof, and it shall be eaten up; and break down the wall thereof, and it shall be trodden down:

 And I will lay it waste: it shall not be pruned, nor digged; but there shall come up briers and thorns: I will also command the clouds that they rain no rain upon it.

 For the vineyard of the LORD of hosts is the house of Israel, and the men of Judah his pleasant plant: and he looked for judgment, but behold oppression; for righteousness, but behold a cry.

 

  • And that cry a selfish cry—‘why me’.

 

Isaiah 55:13

Instead of the thorn shall come up the fir tree, and instead of the brier shall come up the myrtle tree: and it shall be to the LORD for a name, for an everlasting sign that shall not be cut off.

 

  • INSTEAD – if you are going to have a change you must first see what is.

 

  • Faith calls that which is not as though it is. We live not by sight but by faith.

 

  • Instead of the THORN shall come up the FIR tree <= a change for the better

 

  • There is a covenant of peace.
