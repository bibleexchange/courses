Tent Revival Crusade

Lewiston, Maine

Rev. Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

July 8, 2007, Sunday Night

TITLE: Rare & Simple Things

TEXT: John 3:14

 

(65 people)

 

A rare thing is needed tonight.

 

It is going to take God to free you from your sin. God can save the Lost and that is the most important experience. While God’s Word is preached, his Word brings healing to the soul and body.

 

And as Moses lifted up the serpent in the wilderness, even so must the Son of man be lifted up:

 

These are the words Jesus gave to Nicodemus a teacher of religion.

 

v. Except a man be born again…there was something that Nicodemus was missing. Simple things. Because it had to do with the spiritual.

 

It is rare to see people really love God and continue and not get distressed and discouraged.

 

It is a rare thing to see a man transformed.

 

Our God still saves and delivers. God can heal! We need that change in our heart and spirit and transformed mind. We serve a God that saves, delivers, and works the miracle. Trust God over the opinion of doctors and man.

 

Ex 23:22

But if thou shalt indeed obey his voice, and do all that I speak; then I will be an enemy unto thine enemies, and an adversary unto thine adversaries.

 

Obey the truth, do the truth. He will bring down the things that destroys our life.

 

In the Wilderness God told Moses to make a brazen serpent. It had to be made.

 

In a time when God has promised to be an enemy to our enemies. He that cometh to the light and doeth the truth will find power.

 

It is a rare thing to see fiery serpents. God did something special that day to help those believers.

 

Isa 9:8 The Lord sent a word into Jacob, and it hath lighted upon Israel.

 

There is an experience to be had.

 

God gives peace, healing. When Israel was rejecting God, God said make a brazen serpent that all who look to the serpent can live. (Make it simple.)

 

You will not find a more rare thing than as in a time of difficulty you find a man like Daniel who in the face of the lions was steadfast.

 

The supernatural things of God are for those that believe and trust Him.

 

Only God can produce a pure heart.
