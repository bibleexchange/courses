The Only True Answer

 

Micah 7:7—Therefore I will look unto the LORD; I will wait for the God of my salvation: my God will hear me.

 

The Godly always loves going to God for help.

 

  1. I will look unto the LORD

 

    1. Looking to the Lord.
      1. The One to look to
      2. The One to keep looking to.
      3. The One to never stop looking to.
    2. Watching, praying and believing go together
    3. The look of faith

 

 

  1. I will wait for the God of my salvation

 

    1. The God of my salvation
      1. Looking for the Lord for Salvation
      2. Looking for the Lord for Complete Deliverance
      3. Looking for the Lord for Healing
      4. Looking for the Lord for Cleansing
      5. Looking for the Lord for Total victory
      6. Looking for the Lord for Comfort
      7. Looking for the Lord for Perfection
    2. Waiting Patiently in faith makes things work together
    3. The Hope of Salvation

 

 

  1. My God will hear me

 

    1.  My God
      1. God sees and hears when we call in faith
      2. God knows where we are looking
      3. God knows how long we have waited
      4. God knows how much patience we have
      5. God knows what our hope is in
      6. God knows what our motive is
      7. God understands our call
    2. Faith, Hope and Love are gifts from God answering my every need
    3. The Love of God
