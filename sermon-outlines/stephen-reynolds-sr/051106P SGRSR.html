Rev. Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

November 6, 2005

Sunday Evening

Title:  "Fighter’s Children"

Text: Psalm 144:1-5

 

God has been faithful in dealing with and speaking to us and we must be faithful in listening. Learn to be faithful no matter what the circumstances are.

 

The fight of faith is a good fight.

You receive the word of God and His presence into your life EVERYDAY.

v.1 The Lord is my TEACHER. I want to be taught the fight of faith. I want to learn that these fingers need not quit in hard times. We can fight and we can win.

 

v.2 He is my shield and buckler. He can handle big things and He can handle little things.
