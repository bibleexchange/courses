The very first of five annual Feasts:

 

  1. Passover Feast also called the Feast of Unleavened Bread
  2. Feast of Weeks
  3. The Day of Atonement
  4. Feast of Tabernacles
  5. Feast of Trumpets

 

The Israelites were about to be established as a Nation, rooted in the fact that God delivered them through a sacrifice, which signified the death of Christ on the Cross.

a. To prove their faith in God’s provision.

b. The Passover signified deliverance from death through obedience.

c. The future generations were to ask and prepare for Lord’s sacrifice.
