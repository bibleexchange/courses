The Right way is the Write way

 

How to ask questions the right way.

 

  • Ask questions the (write) way.

 

  • The right way is the only way.
  • The right way is the (write) way for me.

If I ask a question any other way than the (write) way, than it is the wrong way to ask the question.

 

Before you ask the question to someone else; ask the Lord first.
