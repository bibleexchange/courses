Every minister of the gospel must provide whatever is needed for the growth of the people. Every true believer has been chosen by God to serve Christ Jesus the Lord. If there is anything in the life of a believer that hinders his growth and service, the matter is serious to God. It must be dealt with and straightened out no matter what it is.  It is the minister's duty to provide for the growth of the believer.                  Jn 21:15-17; Heb 13:17; IPt. 2:1-2                                        He can do nothing unless the believer will receive his ministry.                                         The believer must be a humble follower of God who loves his minister and who is vitally interested in serving his Lord.
