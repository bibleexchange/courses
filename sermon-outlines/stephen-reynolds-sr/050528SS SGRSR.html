Rev. Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

Sunday School

 

Title:

Text: I John 2:13,14

 

God will not override your will. It isn’t that people pass conviction, but we can override it or become so that we cannot sense it.

 

God has a plan and provision for me beyond where I am. You can’t believe God’s Word and not feel the strength of that Word. It is like dynamite.

 

Lord, Make me Faithful.

 

Get Up in the morning before your flesh.  Don’t give it a chance to give you advice. The moment you are consciously awake, tear those sheets off.

 

Pay attention to what God is doing. Take notice.

 

I John 3:24 And he that keepeth his commandments dwelleth in him, and he in him. And hereby we know that he abideth in us, by the Spirit which he hath given us.

 

II Timothy 2:1-4

Faithful men that understand the will of God and call of God. That pulls at our heart everyday. Faithful men that are able.

 

Following. Do everything my leader does.

 

I John 3:24 Promised is the fullness, 4:9,13 “of” Must be strong in me. No weak effort.
