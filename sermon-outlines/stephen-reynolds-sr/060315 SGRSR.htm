Title: Don’t Live In Between

Text: Isaiah 57:15-19

 

Pay attention to the Word. As we are in harmony with his word then will we see His name.

v.15 His name is holy

We need to be in harmony with his word, character, and name.

v.16 Some instead getting rid of the hardnes in their heart, the sickness in their gut, by believing God over the problem rather yield to the problem. His spirit will not always strive with men. He is contending but not for long.

 

1. Yield to his holiness and righteousness.

2. Contend for the Lost. (Abraham searching Sodom for some to repent.)

      There is nothing worth losing out in eternity with Jesus.

      Psalm 113:5,6 he is the source of “longsufferng”/contending for the lost

     

Luke 1:48 If you want to dwell in between, He will let you go. Demas left the things of God and was a fool. Though she was just a human being, God took that body to indwell that. He put something in her spirit to know Him and love Him. I am blessed because I have Jesus in my heart!
