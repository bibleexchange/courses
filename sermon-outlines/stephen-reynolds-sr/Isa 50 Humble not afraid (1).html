                                                        True Humility

Job 18:1-21 Lessons for true humility.

  • Humility is longsuffering not long winded and tedious in remarks.  V.2
  • Humility not arrogance.   V.2
  • Humility not resentment.                              V.3
  • We must be known for our humility not anger.  V.4
  • Humility not fear.   V.5-11
  • Humility not hungerbittern. V.12
  • Humility with obedience even unto death.  V13-21
