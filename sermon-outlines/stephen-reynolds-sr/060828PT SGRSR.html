TITLE: His Glory is Risen Upon Us

TEXT:  Isaiah 60:1

 

It takes faith to arise, to stand up. Especially when surrounded by gross darkness.

The possibilities the Gospel brings to us. Heb. 11:6 The possiblity of coming to God.

 

It is not by our might or ability. It is not just inour thinking and in our doing. It is the glory of God that helps us to keep walking.You put  your trust in God and you will never lose. Some people need to be taught to believe God.

Only God can reveal Himself to you. The faith that He gives you you can walk in that. Let us come boldly unto the throne of grace. A BOLD FAITH.

Hebrews 10:19   God give us an understanding how faith works. It is a holy boldness.
