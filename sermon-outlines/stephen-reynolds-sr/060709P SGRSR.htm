July 9, 2006

Rev. James R. Reynolds, Sr.

Sunday Morning

 

TITLE:::::::::::::::::: A Call to Quality Service

TEXT: Joshua 24:14,15,19-22

 

à God is calling us to quality service.ß

 

You cannot serve God as things are, There are things that God must work out in your heart and spirit. God is looking for quality

men and lives that will present themselves before God. V.21,22 The people began to testify and devote themselves with

committed hearts. Do I hear the call to work for God to His glory and honor in quality service and not ‘just to work’, or do ‘just something’.

 

God anoints the yielded and obedient vessel. We lack much. There is a great difference between where we should be and where we are.

 

The People Said, We Will Serve the Lord:

  v.15 Preparing them to be anointed for quality service.

  Often times we find ourselves overworked. We  appreciate the opportunities to work. It is God’s workings and doings, when things don’t go right we simply need to be obedient to Him.

  v.19 You can’t serve God. Your lust inside you will destroy you. It must be dealt with. But, If I surrender my life to Him, He will change me into that new creature that will change us. 

  Samson was in places that he should not be. In the vineyards of Timnath. He had been committed to the Nazarite vow. Judges 13:5; 14:15

  We have got to have a greater vision of the Gospel and understanding God’s greatness. Joshua called the people out in the presence of God what God was calling them to. 24:1 Am I here for one reason and purpose: to serve the Lord?

  In all learning and effort I will go backwards until God comes on the scene and I live my life before God. That is what produces QUALITY service.

  Men serve themselves today, having many idols. There is only ONE GOD.

  My life is not my own.

 

JUDGMENT

  There are things that we need correction on and chastening ,but we do not necessary require judgment. We are not called to wrath as faithful followers. Your obedience will produce life. Ex. 20:12.

  He isn’t just going to do it all for us. We see in Scripture 100 performance from God, but a great absence of Israel’s effort. God is faithful. I John 1:9

 Small things lead to bigger things.

 There is a zero chance of me living to the fullness as a Son of God without the leading of the Spirit.

 God is looking for quality life, quality faith, total commitment, and perfection. There is a mark to reach for and gain. Phil. 3:14

 

What am I Going to Serve?

  People serve things that are so small.

  God can strip us like a Saul of Tarsus. It is going to take a humbling process so that it is ALL of Jesus and NONE of me.

  2 Thessalonians 3:5-Direct these hearts into the love of God. It is not in US to serve God. God gave some things to Jacob and some things to Esau. God has a plan for EVERY person’s life.

  The one thing I reserve for myself will be the thing that will destroy me.

  What if Samson was here tonight to preach to us how wicked and evil it is to multiply our sin and desire…?

  Jacob went down to Egypt. Israel served what? Getting what? For 400 years. Even after getting out of Egypt, there was yet Egypt in them.

  Get alone with God and wholly follow Him.

  God raised up Jeremiah to tell them there would be 70 years of captivity.

  There is coming another mount of years that will be God’s last move of judgment and wrath. It is going to be a period of 7 years.

  God is calling and it is our decision. I must be on guard from this moment one what I confess and commit. There is destruction waiting on every corner. We are witnesses to ourselves.

  No Decision: Is a decision against Christ.

  The most dreadful place to be in is the place where God is and to not sense His presence.

  God’s  Best, not just mine: Quality Service.
