TITLE: Persecutor Became Preacher

TEXT: Galatians 1:23,24

 

Galatians 1:23 But they had heard only, That he which persecuted us in times past now preacheth the faith which once he destroyed.

 24 And they glorified God in me.

 

The Persecutor Become Preacher

 

Anyone that doesn’t like Jesus doesn’t understand Jesus.

 

You can become a preacher of the Gospel

 

In Matthew He was a witness of what was going on and he heard from Jesus many times that He would go to a cross and die. The Gospel message to the humble is simple

 

The preaching of the cross happened and will never happen again,, When Christ returns He will do so as King of kings and Lord of lords.

 

Luke 9:44,45

 

Many times people have questions that they are too afraid to ask. None have to go away with questions about Jesus.

 

It is simple yet not possible for any one of us to figure it out all by ourselves.

 

If there has ever been a generation of young people to arise and say I’ll stand for Jesus, we need it NOW.
