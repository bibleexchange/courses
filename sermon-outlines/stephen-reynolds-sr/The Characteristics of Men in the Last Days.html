The Characteristics of Men in the Last Days

2Ti 3:1 This know also, that in the last days perilous times shall come.

 2 For men shall be lovers of their own selves, covetous, boasters, proud, blasphemers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy,

 3 Without natural affection, trucebreakers, false accusers, incontinent, fierce, despisers of those that are good,

 4 Traitors, heady, highminded, lovers of pleasures more than lovers of God;

 5 Having a form of godliness, but denying the power thereof: from such turn away.

 

  1. The Last days are characterized as perilous times.
    1. “in the last days perilous times shall come”
      1. The most terrifying days will be the last days
      2. We can know these things that we may persuade men to turn to God.

  1. The Last days are considered to be powerless times.
    1. “Having a form of godliness, but denying the power thereof”
      1. The chief danger of the last days is Religion without Power.
      2. We are taught that we may teach others to turn away from these things.
      3. We are to have the Power.
        1. To Know and to make Jesus Known.
        2. To be - selfless, give, humble, praise, obey, thankful, Holy, affectionate,

        5. To Witness
