A Powerful Witness

Col 4:5  Walk in wisdom toward them that are without, redeeming the time.

Eph 5:15 See then that ye walk circumspectly, not as fools, but as wise,

1Th 4:12 That ye may walk honestly toward them that are without, and that ye may have lack of nothing.

 A Powerful Witness is when Character, Conduct and Conversation are all working together to win the Lost. (the Outsider)Not just the backslider.

Character—Who you are.

Conduct    — What you do.

Conversation—How you live your life

  • Speech
  • Manner of living
    • Disposition---what you are like.
      • Personality-being a certain way.     Quality
    • Attitude-Showing what you are like.          Tone
      • Point of view-thinking a certain way

The Christians walk and talk must be compatible.

A Christian’s walk will make his witness Powerful.

The Pattern is found in Jesus Christ.
