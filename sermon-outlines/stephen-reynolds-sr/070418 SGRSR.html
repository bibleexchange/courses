TITLE: The Way of Holiness
TEXT: I Peter 1:13-16

 

There is a time when we need to be taught the principles of the Bible.

God restores virtue. We stand complete in Christ and we have got to Belive that. There is only one safe place and that is in Him.

 

v.16

àBecause it is written, Be ye holy; for I am holy.

 

Holiness is not just when you learn certain things to do or not to do.

Holiness is the very nature of God. And as a born again Christian we must be holy. There is no two ways to be Christian, but only ONE way to be a Christian and that is the way of holiness.

 

Hebrews 12:14

àFollow peace with all men, and holiness, without which no man shall see the Lord:

 

When I see someone failing, I need to have enough compassion to see that they are restored. We have a responsibility to live in the way of Holiness BY THE WORD. At no time am I the one to try to bring conviction, but that is the work of the Holy Ghost.

Some bring no WORD for the Holy Ghost to work through to bring conviction, and they ruin any opportunity for that person to be helped by God.
