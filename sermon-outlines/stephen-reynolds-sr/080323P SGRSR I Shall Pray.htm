Resurrection Sunday, March 23, 2008

TITLE: I Shall Pray

TEXT: Mark 14:32

 

Mr 14:1 ¶ After two days was the feast of the passover, and of unleavened bread: and the chief priests and the scribes sought how they might take him by craft, and put him to death.

 2 But they said, Not on the feast day, lest there be an uproar of the people.

 

Mr 14:32 ¶ And they came to a place which was named Gethsemane: and he saith to his disciples, Sit ye here, while I shall pray.

 

 

There is a principle involved here. He not only said it, but he did it. I SHALL PRAY. Are you looking in your bible and asking the Lord to help you know what you need to do in the conflicts in your life.

 

v.1 death, today is the day we recognize his resurrection from the dead.

 

The realm of the spirit, more and more people are not understanding the battle getting caught up in the conflict, but Christ’s example was I SHALL PRAY.

 

v.1 in a time of feast and thanksgiving. A time of rejoicing and thanksgiving for deliverance out of Egypt and at this time they are thinking about this Jesus. A mind to destroy life.  Their minds aer turned to crucifying the Lord, Though not wanting to do it on that day, their day and cause an uproar with the people. Yet Christ died on the day of the Passover to fulfill a type. (just as the Holy Ghost fell on the day of Pentecost in perfect type) God is in perfect and complete control.

 

v.26 Mr 14:26 And when they had sung an hymn, they went out into the mount of Olives.

Singing hymns, worshipping God, glorifying Him.

 

Then we see Christ going into gethsemene to pray. They that sang and rejoiced, could not pray the prayer. Judas was in the temple selling his own soul.

 

There are a lot of things that we do that we should not be doing.

 

Eight disciples remained outside of the garden. Were not allowed to enter.

 

 

Peter James and John were called to a deeply committed life. A sign to us and to them that we can’t make it unless he calls and deals with us. And his calling is not free trip. We must be COMMITTED to Him.

 

Will I pray? There is not always going to be singing.
