“it shall be told thee What thou Must Do”

 

Ac 9:6 And he trembling and astonished said, Lord, what wilt thou have me to do? And the Lord said unto him, Arise, and go into the city, and it shall be told thee what thou must do.

 

Ps 2:11 Serve the LORD with fear, and rejoice with trembling. Ac16:25-34 Php2:12-13 1Pe4:17 Mt25:46

 

  1. The Lord wants us to draw near Him with

broken hearts, contrite spirits, calling upon Him from the depths of our being.

 

  1. The Lord wants us to seek Him in prayer,

asking Him to meet our needs and to help us through the problems, difficulties, and trials of life.

 

  1. The Lord wants us to praise Him and give Him

thanks when He meets our needs and supplies whatever provisions we lack.

 

  1. The Lord wants us to walk with Him by faith

and not by sight, through the good times as well as the bad. God never wants us to walk alone facing temptations of this world and the difficult, distressing trials that so often confront us.

The Lord wants to take care of us.   (Heb.13:17)

The Lord wants to infuse power within our hearts and minds, power to conquer, triumph, and overcome any and all enemies who oppose us or hate us or assault us.

The Lord wants us to walk with Him in love, He will empower us to live victoriously as we walk day by day throughout life.

This is the desire of the Lord's heart.

  2. Fellowship with Him
  3. Drawing close to Him
  4. The Lord Working through us
