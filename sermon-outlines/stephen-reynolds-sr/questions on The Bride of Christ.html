A couple of questions on The Bride of Christ.

    P. 134 (near the bottom of the page.)  The devil's place was not left empty, but was found no more.  The "Man-Child" now occupies that place.

Is the "Man-Child" to be the "covering cherub"?  Won't the "Man-Child" have a place of his own that God has prepared from the beginning?

 

 

    P. 126 – The devil has the power of death.  ("has" - present tense as though it is something he still retains when scripture says "had" past tense.)

Heb. 2:14 says that the devil had the power of death.  (Before Jesus died and rose again.)

Rev. 1:18 Jesus Christ says, “I . . . . have the keys of hell and of death.”
