We need the Gospel in our Hearts

 

There is a need of Salvation

There is a demand to live Holy

There is a requirement for perfection

There is a promise for the fire of conviction from the power of the Holy Ghost.

There is a Divine provision that God has made to bring you up out of despair and death.
