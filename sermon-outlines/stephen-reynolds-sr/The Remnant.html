The Remnant

 

Romans 11:5 – “Even so then at this present time also there is a remnant according to the election of grace.”

 

Remnant – “1.  small part still left: a small part of something that remains after the rest has gone.” – Encarta World English Dictionary

 

  1. The Remnant Remains Faithful to Him while Others Fall Away

The Apostle Paul stated that in the End Times before the return of Christ that there would a great falling away. Today we are seeing the start of the Great Falling Away. Falling away does not mean only losing the salvation experience though many are and will, but it also means a falling away from truth and fundamentals of a true Christian life.

 

Falling away from:

 

  1. Truth
  2. Holiness
  3. True Worship
  4. God’s Preserved Word – The King James Bible
  5. Sanctification
  6. Fundamental Doctrines of the Bible
  7. Holy Ghost Baptism
  8. Modesty
  9. Prayer
  10. Healing
  11. Deliverance from Sin

 

Falling to:

 

  1. Falsehoods
  2. Loose living
  3. Charismatic Spirit
  4. Perverted Versions of the Bible
  5. Contemporary Music
  6. False Doctrine
  7. Sinful Lifestyles
  8. Worldly Dress
  9. Immodesty
  10. Demonic Influences
  11. Jezebel Spirt

 

 

But God has a remnant that has and will remain faithful to Him even as others fall away from Him. Throughout history, no matter how dark and bad national and world spiritual conditions have been, there has always been a remnant. In the Old Testament, in Elijah’s day, while most of Israel had fallen away to idolatrous baal worship there were seven thousand men who had not bowed to baal. Today while many are falling to apostasy and luke-warmness, God has preserved His remnant. The Remnant are the true and the faithful who love Him, and are following His commandments and plans.

 

 

 

 

  1. The Remnant Follows His Word

The Remnant loves and follows His word. While others see following and living by the Word as an annoyance and a hindrance, the Remnant takes joy in living by and following His Word. Sadly many today are turning to modern perversions of the Bible that distort, downplay and omit many Fundamentals truths. How can one follow the roadway to heaven while being lead by an erroneous roadmap? But the Remnant remains faithful to and follows the unadulterated Word of God.

 

  1. The Remnant Lives a Life of Separation and Holiness

For the Remnant, living a life of separation and holiness is not hard, a burden or a drudgery it is a joy. Today most of the church world has thrown away and despises the message of holiness and gladly embraces worldliness. Modesty has become a lost practice, while immodesty has become the norm. The Bible no longer sets the dress code, the world does instead. Sin runs rampant under the false covering of a perverted idea of the function of the Blood of Christ. Today many are being told that the Blood is a covering for sin instead of being a cleanser of sin. One can run the gauntlet from smoking cigarettes to committing adultery and then get along comfortably and even be part of ministry with the apostate church world. But the Remnant lives a life of holiness and separation from the world and sin.

 

  1. The Remnant Belongs to Him

Many are going to stand before God one day and cry that they have done many things in His name, and He will say to them to depart – that He never knew them. It takes more than professing Christ to belong to Him - we must love Him and follow Him and His Word. Jesus said if you love Him, then you will follow His commandments. Today many in the apostate church world are showing that they really don’t love Him, as they refuse to follow his commandments fully. But the sold-out, redeemed Remnant belongs to Him.
