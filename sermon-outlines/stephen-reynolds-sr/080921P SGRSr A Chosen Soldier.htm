TITLE: A Chosen Soldier

TEXT: James 3:16-18; 4:1-7

 

A chosen soldier isn’t just you choosing to be a soldier, but the LORD choosing you. For many are called but few are chosen.

 

 

L         Let God arise

            In Your heart

 

A        Abide in His Love

            No fear can remain

 

U        Unmoved by winds

            Unchanged by time

 

R         Remember His Promises

            Every Verse, Every Line

 

A        As the tears come

            Ask for His presence
