Title: The Shepherd's Voice

Text: John 10:14-16

 

INTRODUCTION

There is a Good Shepherd and a Path of Life. We are never alone. There is a purpose for every test. We must understand from the Word what we are doing so that we are never in the dark. If you don't have Jesus in you you are no light to the World. He is the Shepherd of life. He leads us into pastures of life. If we are simply "just making it" there is something wrong. God has somethings in store for His people and He is no respecter of persons. Let us desire to know the Life of Christ and follow Him every step of the way.

 

Romans 2:11--God has given us insight, but just because we have recieved a revelation of truth does not mean we have a "special" position with God. We must KNOW God and OBEY Him. The Jews boasted in that to them was committed the law by God, but Paul exhorted that all men were given the law in their conscience. There is no many ways to eternal life, but ONE way.

 

There is something lacking in each of us, but we have a Good Shepherd in Whom is no lack. "You will fail yourself and those around you." Jesus will NEVER fail you.

 

John 10:1-6  v.1 Judas had been called out by Jesus to be His disciple, and yet he became a thief.

God has given to us and we too often don't share as we should and if that is so we will lose it. People have lost things by the thief and they become hard and hold grudges.

Barabbas was a robber. Took advantage of people. Low, mean, would take a person's life in his stealing.

v.6--they did not understand nor recieve the first 5 verses.

The pope died with a false hope. (1/6 of the world follow him and the path he took.) We must make sure the life of Jesus is working in our heart for that life is the light of the world. His Word in us in life and a light to the world.

There is a thief around! He doesn't just steal but robs, kills, and destroys. He is trying to destroy our faith.

 

People must see Jesus. Your words are not enought, "I am a Christian." They MUST SEE JESUS IN YOU. Not just willing to give but GIVING.

 

Divide. Destroy. Is the goal of the theif.

Know, believe, obey, and give yourself to truth. and the thief won't be able to hinder us.
