To walk after the Spirit is to Glorify God.

1Pe 1:22 Seeing ye have purified your souls in obeying the truth through the Spirit unto unfeigned love of the brethren, see that ye love one another with a pure heart fervently:

 

Ac 6:7 And the word of God increased; and the number of the disciples multiplied in Jerusalem greatly; and a great company of the priests were obedient to the faith.

Principle-- do not neglect an inch of truth or Power.

Being a man of Principle is one that is always obedient to the Truth.

Obeying the Truth is Liberty.

Not obeying the Truth is bondage.

(Yielding to sin, self, sickness, world, compromise or pressure is bondage)

Self-denial-- go to any lengths to show the fruit of the Spirit.

Evidence of Christian character is the spirit and practice of self-denial.

You cannot have Two supreme principles of action governing your life.

True religion is self-denying.

A new creature living by the regenerating power of the Holy Ghost

A supreme attachment to the glory of God is the principle which regulates your conduct both toward God and toward man?

Which do you pursue most, your interest or your duty?

Which do you think of most, your interest or your duty?

 Can you renounce your ease, your profit, your honor when they come in competition with your duty?

Can you renounce everything which is inconsistent with the glory of God and the highest good of your fellow men?

Are these the natural breathings of your heart—“Thy Kingdom come! Thy will be done in earth as it is in heaven?

Is the highest interest of the Kingdom identified with the object of your highest desire, and with your most vigorous exertion?

Is the cause of Christ your concern?                                                                                                                       Is the dishonor of Christ your burden?                                                                                                                         Is the cross of Christ your glory?

 Be not a stranger to the cleansing power of the blood of Jesus and of the Word of God, the convicting power of the Word and the Holy Ghost, be not a stranger to bearing one another’s burden and the spirit of self-denial.

Always show conclusive evidence that you are born from above.

Self-denial is the nature of Jesus.

The more you forget yourself in a supreme regard for God’s glory, the more will you advance your own interest both in this world and that which is to come. But the more you seek a selfish, private, separate interest in opposition to the glory of God, the more are you seeking an interest which God has determined to destroy.

We need an absolute Surrender to God his Will, Obedience to the Work and complete assurance in the Witness of the Holy Ghost.

 

Affections set on things above, body, and soul.

 

Let him not seek his own happiness as the supreme object, but be willing to renounce all, and lay down his life also, if required.
