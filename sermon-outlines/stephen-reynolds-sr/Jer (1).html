Refrain

04513.  enm,       mana`,  maw-nah'

To debar – To refuse entry, exclude, expel, bar, ban, veto and prohibit.                               deny, keep (back), refrain, restrain, withhold.

We are not to mourn beyond natural affection.  We must never allow anything to hinder our joy in the Lord at all, or take us away from our duty to him.  Though we mourn, we must not murmur, nor must we resolve, as Jacob did, to go to the grave mourning. In order to repress inordinate grief, we must consider that there is hope in our end, (the trouble will not last always), that it will be in the end peace. It ought to support us under our troubles that we have reason to hope in the Lord. The righteous has hope even in his death;
