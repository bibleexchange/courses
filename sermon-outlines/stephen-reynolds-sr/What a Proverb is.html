an adage - a traditional saying that expresses something considered to be a general truth.

an enigmatical - having a quality of mystery and more than one meaning and so difficult to understand or interpret but put in a way that if we listen we will hear the truth.

 

Parable - a short simple story intended to illustrate a moral or religious lesson

Proverb - a short well-known saying that expresses an obvious truth and often offers advice
