Jesus Christ is Lord

Great and Mighty is The Lord our God

Holy is The Lord our God And what a Great Name!!!

Great is the Lord and Greatly to be praised—Holy is the Lord and Mighty is His Name.

 

Ezekiel 36:23 And I will sanctify my great name, which was profaned among the heathen, which ye have profaned in the midst of them; and the heathen shall know that I am the LORD, saith the Lord GOD, when I shall be sanctified in you before their eyes.

 

  1. God's Concern For His Name—“I will sanctify My Great Name”
  2. God Loves a backsliding, rebellious people—“ I shall be sanctified in you”
  3. God is so Gracious to the Heathen.

 

Sanctify; make holy and honorable—The Lord God will make Himself known in you.

 

The Jews were carried off into captivity, which made the other nations say,

"What is going on?        Aren't these 'God's people'?

If their God is so great, then why were we able to remove 'God's people' from the land that God gave them.?"

 

God's response is that He would not tolerate this profaning of His name.

He tells Israel that He is about to act - not for their sake, but for the sake of His name.

He is going to "vindicate the holiness" of His name.

 

“Sanctify” (Kaw-DASH), meaning "make holy, consecrate, dedicate, hallow, purify."

God is going to insure that His name is set apart from all the idolatry of the nations.

He is going to show Himself strong by bringing the Jews back into the land in plain sight of the nations.

 

He will regather his people from among the nations. He will bring them back to

honor his name.

 

Israel gave the heathen occasion to think meanly of the Lord God, but He will shew He is great and good.

When God performs what he hath sworn by his holiness, He sanctifies his name.

 

I shall show my hatred to (proud service) or any vice, and my love to holiness: but it is not for your sakes, but for my holy name's sake

 

When God’s people fail, it profanes the name of Jesus Christ our Lord.

The Lord wants us to be Holy as He is Holy and Merciful and Righteous as He is.
