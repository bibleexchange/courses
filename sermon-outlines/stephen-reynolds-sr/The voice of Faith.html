Ps 94:16 Who will rise up for me against the evildoers? or who will stand up for me against the workers of iniquity?

 

In all ages, men who neither feared God nor regarded man have combined together and formed alliances, to carry on the works of darkness. And they have promoted the kingdom of their father the devil,

Men who fear God, and desire the happiness of their fellow creatures, have in every age found it needful to join together in order to spread the knowledge of God our Saviour, and to promote His kingdom upon the earth. The Lord has instructed every believer to do so. From the very beginning He hath taught men to join together in his service, and has united them in one body by one Spirit. And for this very end he has joined them together,

"that he might destroy the works of the devil;" first in them that are already united, and in them that are round about them

  • God demands obedience; therefore, at the first He expects dependence.     
  • God orders Love; therefore, at the first He requires faith    
    • It takes faith for the Impossible
    • It takes faith for the Difficult
    • It takes faith for the Simple   
    • The very smallest works can’t go by without faith    

God gives special attention to weak faith,

  • Weak faith requires immediate attention
  • Strong faith can handle the test of time.
  • Perfect faith is being as He is in this world.
