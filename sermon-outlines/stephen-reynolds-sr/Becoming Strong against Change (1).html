Being Strong against Change

 

2Peter 3:17 Ye therefore, beloved, seeing ye know these things before, beware lest ye also, being led away with the error of the wicked, fall from your own stedfastness.

 

“seeing ye know these things before”

to know beforehand, foresee, foreknow (ordain), know (before).

 

“beware”

to watch, be on guard, to preserve, obey, avoid, be ward, keep, observe, save.

 

“being led away”

to take off together, transport with (seduce, passively, yield):--carry (lead) away with, condescend.

 

“fall”

to drop away; be driven out of one's course; to lose, become inefficient, be cast, fail, fall, take none effect.

 

“stedfastness”

Stability—(not changing)—strengthen
