Faith in Relationship to Divine reward.

 

1.         1.    God is the rewarder of them that diligently seek Him

2.          2.    God is the Author and Finisher of our faith.

3.      3.    God rewards faith with Eternal life

4.       4.      God rewards faith by imputing and imparting the Righteousness and Holiness of Christ by grace through faith .

5.        5.      Obedience to faith can bring the blessing to the whole world.

 

    The Righteousness of God Revealed

a.     By Faith

b.    We have the promise of more revelation as we diligently exercise our faith. “from faith to faith”

c.     Faith comes by the Holy Ghost speaking to our heart.

d.    We must act upon the Word by faith so that The Holy Ghost can reveal to us the righteousness of Christ.

 

I.     Righteousness has to be seen, not just believed and talked about.

II.    Righteousness must be demonstrated.

III.   Righteousness is revealed by the New Birth, from seed to fruit.

IV.   Righteousness is revealed by answered Prayer from seed to fruit.
