Pr 8:13 The fear of the LORD is to hate evil: pride, and arrogancy, and the evil way, and the froward mouth, do I hate.

 

  • It is impossible to hate evil without loving good
    • hatred to evil will lead a man to abandon the evil way;
    • love to goodness will lead him to do what is right in the sight of God, under the influence of the Holy Ghost which has given the hatred to evil, and inspired the love of goodness:

 

    • The substance of true religion is faith.
    • The substance of true religion is the fear of the Lord.
    • The substance of true religion is faithfulness.

  1. Jesus Christ is the Son of the living God,                                 Mat. 16 :18

  3. Jesus Christ is the Sum Heb.8:1
