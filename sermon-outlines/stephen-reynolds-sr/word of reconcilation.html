There is an enmity in the heart of man against sacred things; the grace of Christ alone can remove this enmity.

 

“exchange” to change thoroughly; and the grand object of the Gospel is to make a complete change in men's minds and manners.

  1. The first object is the removal of enmity from the heart of man, that he may be willing to accept the salvation God has provided for him, on the terms which God has promised.  
  2. The enmity in the heart of man is the grand hinderance to his salvation.

 

A new creature, and creation is the proper work of an all-wise, almighty Being; then this total change of heart, soul, and life, which takes place under the preaching of the Gospel, is effected by the power and grace of God: this is salvation, and salvation must ever be of the Lord;

 

It is only by the grace and Spirit of Christ that the proud, fierce, and diabolic nature of men can be changed and reconciled to God, and by and through this sacrifice God can be propitious to them.
