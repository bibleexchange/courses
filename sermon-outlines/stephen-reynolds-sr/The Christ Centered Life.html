The Christ Centered Life

“Do all things through Christ”

 

Php. 4:13 I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me.

 

  1. Professing the Ability of Christ through a Christ centered life.

 

This is a Profession of a great saint of God that really understands the importance of perfecting our obedience through Christ.

 

    1. “I can do all things through Christ”

 

      1. Able to undergo every trial.
      2. Brave all suffering.
      3. Face any storm.
      4. Overcome every temptation.
      5. Perform each duty.
      6. Obey all His commands.
      7. Please God in all things.

 

  1. Proclaiming the Sufficiency of God’s Grace in a Christ Centered Life.

 

This phrase “I can do” is not self-sufficiency, but an understanding of the Sufficient Grace of God.

 

    1. “I can do”

 

      1. Pray “thy will be done” and then know Him to fulfill it all.
      2. Sing “I am weak but thou art strong”
      3. Praise Him for His the Christ.
      4. Testify to all His saving Grace.
      5. Witness in the strength of the Lord.
      6. Preach in the strength of the Lord
      7. Worship in the strength of the Lord
      8. Work in the strength of the Lord.

Paul learned Dependence on the Lord in every circumstance.

He was armed for Adversity as well as Prosperity.

 

For with the ignorant adversity brings bitterness; but with prosperity comes pride and arrogance,

 With the obedient adversity brings blessings, and with prosperity humility and tenderness.

  1. Preaching Christ is the strength of the Christ Centered life.

 

    1. “Do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me”

 

      1. Mind centered in Christ’s strength not your weakness.
      2. Heart centered in Christ for full protection and provision.
      3. Soul centered in Christ doing all His Will.
      4. Spirit centered in Christ’s life for life and light.

  1. Performing through Christ in His Perfection

 

By Faith Performing through Christ’s Righteousness

Seeing His Perfections makes us aware of our weaknesses.

 

What we thought to be our strong points are strongholds, become weak.

 

    1. Pride is not strengthened but weakened.

 

    1. Self life is not strengthened but weakened.

 

    1. Self righteousness is not strengthened but weakened.

 

    1. Desires of the flesh are not strengthened but weakened.

 

    1. Stubbornness is not strengthened but weakened.

 

    1. Bitterness is not strengthened but weakened.

 

    1. Jealousies are not strengthened but weakened.

 

  1. Producing through Christ His Strength to Perfection.

 

    1. His strength in Salvation—Souls saved

 

    1. His strength in Healing—Bodies healed

 

    1. His strength in Restoration—Lives restored

 

    1. His strength in Deliverance—Mind, Heart and Soul Delivered.

 

    1. His strength in Perfection—Body, Soul and Spirit Perfected.
