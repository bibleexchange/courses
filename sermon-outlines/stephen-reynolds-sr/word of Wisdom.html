 9—To another faith by the same Spirit; to another the gifts of healing by the same Spirit;

 10—To another the working of miracles; to another prophecy; to another discerning of spirits; to another divers kinds of tongues; to another the interpretation of tongues:

There are three groups of the Nine Gifts

 

Those that impart power to know supernaturally.

The Gifts of Revelation

  1. A Word of Wisdom
    1. A supernatural revelation of Divine purpose
  2. A Word of Knowledge
    1. A supernatural revelation of facts in the Divine mind
  3. The Discerning of Spirits
    1. A supernatural insight in realm of the Spirit

 

Those that impart power to act supernaturally.

The Gifts of Power

  1. Faith
    1. A supernatural trust in God for miraculous
  2. The Working of Miracles
    1. A supernatural intervention into the ordinary course of nature.
  3. The Gifts of Healing
    1. A supernatural power to heal

 

Those that impart power to speak supernaturally.

The Gifts of Inspiration

  1. Prophecy
    1. A supernatural utterance in a known tongue
  2. Diverse kinds of Tongues
    1. A supernatural utterance in an unknown tongue
  3. Interpretation of Tongues
    1. The supernatural showing forth of the meaning of unknown tongues
