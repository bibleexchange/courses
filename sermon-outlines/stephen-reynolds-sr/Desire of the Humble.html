Micah 5:2—But thou, Bethlehem Ephratah, though thou be little among the thousands of Judah, yet out of thee shall he come forth unto me that is to be ruler in Israel; whose goings forth have been from of old, from everlasting.

 

We may be considered little or small, but when the King comes to town, all that is changed! To Bethlehem he announced his coming (700 years before), but did they desire his coming as they should have?  We need to have the same desire for God that He has for us. God wants us to be like Christ, therefore we must desire to be like Christ.

His birth has now already taken place, but there are still things yet things to take place. Isaiah 66 prophesied the Messiah coming. Micah 5:3 is heralding something else.
