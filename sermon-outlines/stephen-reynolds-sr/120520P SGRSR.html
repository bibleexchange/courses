LIVING WATER

 

I. The Lord knows when and how to give Living Water.

a. This Living Water is Everlasting Life.

b. This Living Water is what He gives to those that ask Him.

John 4:34 Jesus saith unto them, My meat is to do the will of him that sent me, and to finish his work.
