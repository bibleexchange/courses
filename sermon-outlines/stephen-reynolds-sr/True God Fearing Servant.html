True God Fearing Servant

 

Psa. 119:38 Stablish thy word unto thy servant, who is devoted to thy fear.

 

The True Servant devoted to fear God desires to be established with the Word  

 

Thy Word is truth and thy word establishes us as a True God Fearing Servant

 

 

“Thy Word”

 

  1. Stablish thy word unto thy servant
    1. Thy Word causes thy servant to be established
    2. Thy Word continues to produce thy fear
    3. Thy Word is confirmed to thy servant

 

“Thy Servant”

 

  1. Stablish  thy servant with thy word
    1. Thy servant is caused  to receive of the promise
    2. Thy servant continues to be strengthened in thy word
    3. Thy servant must have the fullness confirmed in him

 

“Thy Fear”

 

  1. Stablish thy fear in the servant of the word
    1. Thy fear devotes thy servant to thy word
    2. Thy fear stablishes thy servant with thy word
    3. Thy fear serves thy word

 

If we are going to be effective intercessors we must first be devoted to the Lord, to His Word and to His Love.

 

Our devotion to the Lord must be true Love for Him so that we can even intercede for the lost and the needy.

 

Many lack devotion

  • to the fear of the Lord
  • to the Word of God,
  • to the Love of God
  • to serve God

 

All True God fearing servants prove their love for God by being established in the Word to win the lost
