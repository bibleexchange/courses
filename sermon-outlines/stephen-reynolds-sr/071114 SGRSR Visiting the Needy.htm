TITLE: Visiting the Needy

TEXT: James 1:21-27

 

·         Anything that is a hindrance to my faith –get it out of the way-- That I may help the needy.

 

·         v.21 Wherefore lay apart all filthiness and superfluity of naughtiness, and receive with meekness the engrafted word, which is able to save your souls. There are some things God considers filthy.

 

·         Isaiah 64:6. Isaiah says to put away the filthy rags. Thinking we can do God’s will OUR WAY. We NEED God’s help in order to make it. You cannot live RIGHTEOUSLY without the help of God. We must BELIEVE God’s Word. When He speaks His Word performs the life and power and victory for us. We must receive it.

 

·         LAY APARTà Cast off, put aside those things that will hinder our experience with God. There are things that weigh us down and are a burden. What we cannot do in our flesh, the spirit of God can do within us if we LAY APART & RECEIVE.

 

·         RECEIVE WITH MEEKNESS THE ENGRAFTED WORD.

 

·         v.27 KEEP UNSPOTTED. It is one thing for God to impart to us and work in us by His grace and it is another place altogether to come to where we fellowship with Christ intimately maintaining a pure heart and life, having no place for this world in us. That place is one where you don’t have to Fight the same problems and temptations everyday, but can GET ABOVE THEM.

 

·         There are some simple things that we have been taught that we have neglected that cause us to turn away from God, but as our relationship increases in closeness with Christ, His power, His life will manifest which is a testimony to the world and us that THERE IS HOPE.

 

·         Samuel àHo 13:11 I gave thee a king in mine anger, and took him away in my wrath. Ps 106:15 And he gave them their request; but sent leanness into their soul. 2Co 10:5 Casting down imaginations, and every high thing that exalteth itself against the knowledge of God, and bringing into captivity every thought to the obedience of Christ;

 

·         James 1:23 Look into the Word of God and see what it is we should be and (v.27) what we should be doing. It is going to take the grace of God to be patient in a lot of such situations. (“You are having a good day today.” “I am not. Don’t you talk to me!”)

·         We need to care about the souls of men. Unless we have a right heart and right spirit to handle their rejection rightly.

 

It is absolutely necessary that we be prepared for the temptations.

Not just of the flesh as in lust, but also ANGER, SELFISHNESS, etc.

CONCLUSION

  • Israel loved their sin and yet cried to God for the honey to flow
  • The Honey represents the Word of God.
  • The things of the World satisfied for a moment, but the honey satisfied their soul. So, they ran after their sin trying to bring the honey with them. God did not allow this.
  • HONEY EXPERIENCES.

Ø  Samson found honey in the carcass of the lion. The young lion represents the things that we need to cast off, put away, and REND THAT LION TO PIECES.

Ø  Samson didn’t learn his lesson and faced some serious consequence.

Ø  When he was conceived the promise was give he would be the man of God’s choice to begin to deliver Israel. In his death, that promise came to pass.

Ø  Judges 14:6; 15:14 The spirit of God giving him the power to overcome in many situations.

Ø  Put sin away, put selfishness, and the things of this world, away

Ø  With meekness (of wisdom) receive the engrafted Word.

Ø  So, that as we visit the needy there will be POWER and ANOINTING TO help them. God’s Word promises it; we must believe it.

Ø  The mighty, great, honourablel will make  a name for themselves, but the base will humble themselves before God and see Him work in their lives.
