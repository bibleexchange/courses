TITLE: A Diligent Faith is The Key of Love                 TEXT: Philippians 1:9

Php 1:9 ¶ And this I pray, that your love may abound yet more and more in knowledge and in all judgment;   (James 5:15-16) Phil.1:6-9,11,25,27,29

Luke 11:52 Woe unto you, lawyers! for ye have taken away the key of knowledge: ye entered not in yourselves, and them that were entering in ye hindered.

●     A Diligent Faith is going to please God.

●     If our faith is diligent, God will add to it.

 

I.                Love with proper knowledge   Jn. 21:15-17

 

1.    Phileo  fil-eh'-o ---moral love; To BE a friend,

●     To know by a certain feeling chiefly of the heart,  To have affection, sentiment or feeling,

●     To know by principle of the head, The embrace of good judgment (Phil. 1:9), The deliberate act of the will, Respect, principle or duty kiss (a mark of tenderness)

 

It is of great importance to know HOW to be a friend!

 

2.    agapao,  ag-ap-ah'-o ---mutual love  (much)

●     TO Know You Have a Friend

●     to love (in a social or moral sense), To love and be loved

 

3.     agape,  ag-ah'-pay

●     affection or benevolence; specially (plural) a love-feast:--(feast of) charity(-ably), dear, love.

 

Lovest thou me more than these?

a.    Do you love Him more than you love others?

b.    Do you love Him more than others do?

c.    Do you love Him without them?

d.    Do you love Him with them?

e.    Do you love Him with the love He has given you?

 

Mt 16:19 And I will give unto thee the keys of the kingdom of heaven:...

Ro 5:5 …the love of God …given unto us.

Eph 5:2 …love… given …

2Ti 1:7 …given …love, and of a sound mind. (2 Cor. 6:6; 8:7)

 

A THOROUGH, HARD WORKING, CONSCIENTIOUS FAITH Is the Key to Love

II. Fruitful increases of Love; in, above and beyond as knowledge increases.

 

Love makes faith work to fullest potential  Gal.5:5-7

The Key of Love; Is a Diligent Faith         (2Peter 1:1-9)

 

Be well natured and exercised in all that He gives.

 

a.     Is obtaining like precious faith and exercising diligently through His righteousness and knowledge.

II Peter 1:1-2; Gal.5:6; Eph.5:2

1.    Love and Faith… command, requirements

●     Love and Faith…gifts-(1Cor.12:8-9; 13:13),

●     Love and Faith…fruit (Gal.5:22)

●     Love and Faith…shield I Thes. 5:8;Eph.6:16

 

2.    A diligent faith must be exercised before faith is added to.

 

3.    Virtue is given to faith that is diligent

 

4.    Knowledge is given where faith and virtue are exercised diligently.

 

5.    Temperance is given where faith, virtue and knowledge are exercised diligently.

 

6.    Patience is given where faith, virtue knowledge and temperance is exercised diligently.

 

7.    Godliness is given where faith, virtue knowledge, temperance and patience is exercised diligently.

 

8.    Brotherly kindness is given where faith, virtue knowledge, temperance, patience and godliness is exercised diligently.

 

Charity is given where faith, virtue knowledge, temperance, patience, godliness, Brotherly kindness is exercised diligently.
