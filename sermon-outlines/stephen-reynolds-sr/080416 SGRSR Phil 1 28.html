Deliverance Center – Portland, Maine

Rev. Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

April 16, 2008, Wednesday

TITLE:

TEXT: Philippians 1:12-28

 

I want to be amongst those that know God and are mature in their knowledge and experience. Putting away childish things.

 

This is a dangerous time. 100 things could be said, but the devil can twist our words and change them.

 

The victory lies in holding to God.

 

Php 1:28 And in nothing terrified by your adversaries: which is to them an evident token of perdition, but to you of salvation, and that of God.

 

Pray for the supply of the Spirit of Jesus Christ. There is a pure source that we can draw from. Beyond our understanding, God can supply to us.
