Isaiah 12:3 Wells of Salvation

 

Sometimes we love truth to a point and get blinded to where people are.

 

Gather ourselves together and eat at the master’s table. There will be a shepherd feeding people. Some will know the voice of God.

 

We must do the will of God to make it. I believe in Jesus. I believe in making to Heaven. I believe in escaping Hell and the problems that come our way through faith in God.

 

If you don’t have faith then you will lose what you have.

 

I Timothy 6:12 Fight the Good Fight of Faith, Lay hold unto eternal life.

 

Don’t get discouraged and in debt. Live content with what you have for a while.

 

The devil deceives people. Some will produce 30-fold, 60-fold, & some a hundred fold.
