The Preacher’s Cry

“PRAY FOR US”

 

1Th 5:25 Brethren, pray for us.

 

  1. Pray for ministers. Note that Paul does not say, "please" or "if you will." He emphatically charges: "Brothers, pray for us." All believers must pray for their ministers.

 

  1. And note: Paul says "us" not me—all of God's chosen ministers are to be prayed for by believers. We must not omit a single minister;

 

  1. and the idea is that we must pray often, not just every day, but often every day. What an impact would be made upon the world if we obeyed this one charge.

 

  1.  Prayer helps Him personally and us all collectively.                                        strength ,wisdom , anointing, --- creates a spiritual atmosphere most favorable for Revival.

 

  1. The Spirit of prayer in a congregation believes for the Word of God to be heard, accepted and retained. We are not the power but the power is to be in us. We are not the agent to make the Word of God effective but we can believe the Holy Ghost to. The Holy Ghost will make this Word most effective in our lives if we believe when we Pray.

 

  1. Col 4:3 Withal praying also for us, that God would open unto us a door of utterance, to speak the mystery of Christ, for which I am also in bonds:         arresting         reviving  soul searching     soul saving      Holy ghost filling.

 

  1. Prayer is a major part of the Work of the Ministry.
