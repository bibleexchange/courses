TITLE: God’s Word TEXT: Psalm 119

  1. Descriptions of God’s Word
  • God is the Source - It is GOD’S Word.
  • God’s Word is Eternal
  • God’s Word is True Psalm 119:160
    • Demon powers rage against the truthfulness of God’s Word.
    • God’s Word is true when I need to be corrected!

 

We need the inspiration of the Holy Ghost to get after this old man. Get this old man praying and reading Scripture every day.

    • God’s Word is Pure
    • God’s Word if Faithful and you can depend on it! Psalm 119:138
    • God’s Word is Wonderful Psalm 119:129
      • Many wonderful things promised in God’s Word!
      • Many of these things go unnoticed.
      • Wonderful = to astonish, amaze
  • God’s Word is Exceeding Broad Psalm 119:96

    • 119:24 Thy testimonies also are my delight and my counsellors.
    • 119:70 Their heart is as fat as grease;but I delight in thy law.
