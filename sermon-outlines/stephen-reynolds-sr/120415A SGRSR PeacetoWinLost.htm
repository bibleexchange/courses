Title: Peace to Win the Lost                                             Text: Psalm 119:1,2,35,176;

 

Psalm 119:1,2,35,176--1 Blessed are the undefiled in the way,who walk in the law of the Lord. 2 Blessed are they that keep his testimonies,and that seek him with the whole heart. 35 Make me to go in the path of thy commandments;for therein do I delight. 176 I have gone astray like a lost sheep; seek thy servant;for I do not forget thy commandments.

 

Mark 9:50--Salt is good: but if the salt have lost his saltness, wherewith will ye season it? Have salt in yourselves, and have peace one with another.

 

Our text is not just the world of a man and about his experience, but it is inspired by God.

There are 74 people in America that make over 10 million dollars a week. God’s peace is more valuable than all their money! The Believer has something better--God’s peace.
