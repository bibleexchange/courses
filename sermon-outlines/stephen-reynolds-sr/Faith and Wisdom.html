even though there are uncertainties to avoid and overcome.

Faith is not a leap in the dark

Faith is not an unknown

Faith is not just an act but acting on what is known from the Lord.

Faith appropriates, a revealed Word from God.

Faith is knowing the outcome by a Word from God before the trial gets started.
