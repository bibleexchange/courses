There is the birth by the Spirit and there is the gift of the Spirit, then there is the leading of the Spirit. Then the gifts and fruits of the Spirit. Then ministry

The birth by the Spirit is the new birth .                                                                              The new birth is a spiritual birth.       Supernatural radical change of heart mind spirit and soul                                                                                                 A birth by the Word – “hearest the sound” “born of the Spirit”

Nicodemus was puzzled by the words "born again." He did not know what Jesus meant. He thought a man must be born a "second time."                                                            Jesus answered Nicodemus by saying five things.

1.    Jesus gave the source of the new birth. (5) It was being born of water,

(The Word of God) and of the Spirit (The Spirit of Truth) Together

2.    Jesus repeated the importance of being born again.

a.  3:3 says: a man "cannot (never) see" (grasp, understand, know, experience) the Kingdom of God;

b.  3:5 says a man "cannot (never) enter" (gain entrance to) the Kingdom of God.

3.    Jesus gave the nature of the new birth.

a.  It is spiritual,     (A Spiritual birth)

b.   not physical or material

3:6 That which is born of the flesh is flesh; and that which is born of the Spirit is spirit.

The flesh cannot bridge the gap between flesh and spirit. Flesh is only flesh; it has no power to be born again, or to become alive spiritually.

4.    Jesus re-emphasized the absolute necessity of the new birth.

a.  "Ye must be born again." The word "must" (dei) means absolute necessity, an imperative.  Jesus commanded “ye must”

b.  3:7 Marvel not that I said unto thee, Ye must be born again. {from above, born anew, your spirit made alive}

c.   Ye must be born of the Spirit

5.  Jesus illustrated the point by picturing the wind. The Spirit of God works just like the wind. We may not know how the wind works, but we can see the effects. It is the same with the Spirit of God: we may not know how He works, but we can hear the Word and see the effects of His working.

The Holy Ghost bears witness of the Word

        Five wonderful truths

 

Rom.8:16 The Spirit itself beareth witness with our spirit, that we are the children of God:

 17 And if children, then heirs; heirs of God, and joint-heirs with Christ; if so be that we suffer with him, that we may be also glorified together.

 

I.                The Holy Ghost bears witness that “we are the children of God”

a.  The Holy Spirit quickens our hearts with perfect knowledge and complete confidence that we are children of God.

b.  The Apostle prays for the promise to be complete

Eph 1:16 Cease not to give thanks for you, making mention of you in my prayers; 17 That the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of glory, may give unto you the spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of him:

18 The eyes of your understanding being enlightened; that ye may know what is the hope of his calling, and what the riches of the glory of his inheritance in the saints,

19 And what is the exceeding greatness of his power to us-ward who believe, according to the working of his mighty power,

20 Which he wrought in Christ, when he raised him from the dead, and set him at his own right hand in the heavenly places,

c.   We have the most solid and convincing testimony of God's love.

Ro 5:5 And hope maketh not ashamed; because the love of God is shed abroad in our hearts by the Holy Ghost which is given unto us.

d.  Every believer should demonstrate that love.

 

Ac 14:17 Nevertheless he left not himself without witness, in that he did good, and gave us rain from heaven, and fruitful seasons, filling our hearts with food and gladness.
