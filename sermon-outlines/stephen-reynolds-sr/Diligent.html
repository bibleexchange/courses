Diligence

(Hebrew)   Discern, give ear, (cause to listen, let it be declared, make to hear)                                                                                           (hardworking, meticulous, conscientious, thorough, attentive, careful and painstaking)

 (Greek) "speed", despatch, eagerness, earnestness:--business, (earnest) care(-fulness), forwardness
