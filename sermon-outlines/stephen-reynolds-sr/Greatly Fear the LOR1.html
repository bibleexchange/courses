People have lost their respect for the Men of God and too many people are full of the fear of the devil and have not the fear of God in them. It should be a greater concern to us that God’s Will is not being accomplished than that the devil should threaten us. We fear being sick, evil-doers, and the devil when, all the while, the power of all things is in the hand of God Almighty.

A proper fear and reverence for God must govern our hearts, instead of a terror of the devil and what his plans are. As our understanding of God’s Will and relationship with Him is perfected in each situation, fear is conquered in those areas.
