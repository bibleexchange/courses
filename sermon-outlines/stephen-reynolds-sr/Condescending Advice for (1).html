Condescending Advice for a Cloudy Day

 

Psalm 50:15 And call upon me in the day of trouble: I will deliver thee, and thou shalt glorify me.

 

The Word of God can instill hope in your life.

  • A Promise and a promise indeed From God
  • A Problem and a problem indeed without God.

 

Facing a very urgent occassion:

 

Romans 12:16 Be of the same mind one toward another. Mind not high things, but condescend to men of low estate. Be not wise in your own conceits.

 

Don’t look at the problem, look at the promise.

Don’t look at the sore look at your Source.

Don’t waver at the promise with doubt, look at the Promiser.

 

Look at the Promiser with praise and Glorify Him.

 

Psalm 50:15 And call upon me in the day of trouble: I will deliver thee, and thou shalt glorify me.

Ps 116:10 I believed, therefore have I spoken: I was greatly afflicted:

 

Ps 119:67 Before I was afflicted I went astray: but now have I kept thy word.

 

Ps 119:71 It is good for me that I have been afflicted; that I might learn thy statutes.

 Ps 119:75 I know, O LORD, that thy judgments are right, and that thou in faithfulness hast afflicted me.

 Ps 119:107 I am afflicted very much: quicken me, O LORD, according unto thy word

 

Zep 3:19 Behold, at that time I will undo all that afflict thee: and I will save her that halteth, and gather her that was driven out; and I will get them praise and fame in every land where they have been put to shame.

 

  1. A Day of Trouble
  1. Seasonal Visitations
    1. A Cloudy Day, dark even at noon

 

Don’t disassociate yourself from the fact that there is trouble, but don’t let the storm enter in you, Don’t stay in the storm without praising Him..

Don’t let the trouble in your mind, soul or spirit.

Don’t let the trouble stay in you drive it out with praise..

 

Don’t look at how much trouble David had; look how much he praised God.

 

 Amos 8:9 And it shall come to pass in that day, saith the Lord GOD, that I will cause the sun to go down at noon, and I will darken the earth in the clear day:

 

Mat. 27:45 Now from the sixth hour there was darkness over all the land unto the ninth hour.

 

Ex. 10:22 And Moses stretched forth his hand toward heaven; and there was a thick darkness in all the land of Egypt three days:

 

God understands when no one else does, He always understands.

    1. Very difficult situations exist
    2. Pressed on every hand
    3. Days short and full of trouble

 

Job14:1—Man that is born of a woman is of few days, and full of trouble.

 

1Peter 1:8—Whom having not seen, ye love; in whom, though now ye see him not, yet believing, ye rejoice with joy unspeakable and full of glory:

 

The Promise that God made is Timely

Ps 50:15—And call upon me in the day of trouble: I will deliver thee, and thou shalt glorify me.

 

  • This Promise was written in the middle of a Cloudy Dark Day.
  • This Promise burst forth, It came on the scene in the middle of the darkness, the cloud and trouble.

 

Who but God can do such things as this?

 

Who but My God can look at the dilemma and arrive on the scene and make a promise in the middle of the Darkness, in the middle of the trial and heartache, and say I am the God that can make and produce a Promise, a Hope and a Remedy.

 

Who but God is not afraid of a lion’s den?

Who but God is not afraid of a fiery furnace?

 

  • We will not bend and we will not bow

 

Dan. 3:18—But if not, be it known unto thee, O king, that we will not serve thy gods, nor worship the golden image which thou hast set up.

 

Who but God could make a man on a ship in a storm fixing to wreck, say An Angel of the Lord visited me (in this storm-in this wave tossed ocean, boards are wreaking and nails are popping, the dow pins are breaking, the mast is snapped like a twig and the main sails are ripped to shreds.

Who but God could walk in the face of the storm and tell you stay with the ship, I am your God and there will not be a life lost.

Hang on to the promise, It may come at the darkest part of the day, but God is able to produce the promise.

Reach up and get a hold of the hand that will not and cannot fail you.

 

  1. All  of God’s advice is Condescending

All of God’s advice comes from above.

All of God’s Promises are from above.

Many try to manage His advice or custom fit God to their thought of a proper remedy.

James 3:17 But the wisdom that is from above is first pure, then peaceable, gentle, and easy to be intreated, full of mercy and good fruits, without partiality, and without hypocrisy.

 

God must remain in that High position until we call on Him in faith AND UNDERSTAND His Word and His Ways by His Spirit and then He will condescend to deliver us.

Call on Me.

I am the God that will deliver you

 

  • God’s Word must always condescend to man and you never want to change that.
  • God is Holy, Righteous, Almighty, He is the Ascended God.

 

Isaiah 57:15 For thus saith the high and lofty One that inhabiteth eternity, whose name is Holy; I dwell in the high and holy place, with him also that is of a contrite and humble spirit, to revive the spirit of the humble, and to revive the heart of the contrite ones.

 

1Peter 1:7 That the trial of your faith, being much more precious than of gold that perisheth, though it be tried with fire, might be found unto praise and honour and glory at the appearing of Jesus Christ:

 

When He in that Condescending position says:

Call on Me, let me hear you call on My name and I will condescend and deliver you.

God will never condescend to our level and stay, He’s listening for a call and He will deliver us to Himself.

 

Psalm 50:15—And call upon me in the day of trouble: I will deliver thee, and thou shalt glorify me.

 

  1. Call upon me in the Day of Trouble
  1. It looks dark and troublesome but call upon me.
    1. God is above it all and He is never reduced

To your command or demand, you can only call when He condescends and says call on me and I will deliver you.

Isaiah 6:3 And one cried unto another, and said,

Holy, holy, holy, is the LORD of hosts: the whole earth is full of his glory.

 

    1. He is beckoning you to a level you cannot attain on your own.

The professing church has tried to bring Christ down to our level with an anxious cry instead of the cry of faith.

 

The professing church has tried to make the bible like a cookbook.

B-I-N-G-O has been hollered louder and louder than they cry JESUS SAVE ME, JESUS HEAL ME, JESUS DELIVER ME, JESUS FIND ME, JESUS REACH ME or JESUS HAVE MERCY ON ME.

We don’t want a god where we practice what we do!

M-E-R-C-Y  Heb.4:16 “obtain mercy in the time of need”

  1. All God’s Mercy condescends to Faith
  1. The Greatest Mercy that He afforded us is the liberty to praise Him.
    1. The greatest Mercy is that He allows us to praise Him.
    2. He allows us to lay out our case before Him
      1. Lay out your request
      2. Tell me your troubles
      3. Tell me your heartache
      4. Lay out your case
      5. Give Me the little details

The small stuff that nobody else understands, I want to see it, to read it.

What more could the Lord do for you?

 

Isaiah 5:4 What could have been done more to my vineyard, that I have not done in it? wherefore, when I looked that it should bring forth grapes, brought it forth wild grapes?

 

2Kings 19:14 And Hezekiah received the letter of the hand of the messengers, and read it: and Hezekiah went up into the house of the LORD, and spread it before the LORD.

Isaiah 37:14 And Hezekiah received the letter from the hand of the messengers, and read it: and Hezekiah went up unto the house of the LORD, and spread it before the LORD.

 

  1. I Will Deliver You
  1. I will be your God
  2. He personally will take care of us with His hand.

Whatever your trouble, He makes no exceptions

 

He doesn’t subcontract, He does the Job Himself.

If you need a touch He says, I will do it personally.

 

We need to trust only the Lord with our Soul.

 

The Ultimate result of Him touching you is that you will Glorify Him.

 

THE LORD JESUS CHRIST

 

  1. Glorify the Name of the Lord.
  1. Magnify the Name of the Lord.
    1. By faith we lay claim to the promise
    2. By faith we look up
    3. By faith we praise for our deliverance

When my deliverance comes I am going to glorify Him.

 

Expect the condescending Power of God to reveal Himself as the only one that can help you out of this trouble.

We must Pray.

We must obey the Lord.

We must walk in the light of the Word of God revealed to us by the Holy Ghost.

 

BUT WE MUST HAVE A VISITATION FROM THE LORD.

 

Luke 19:44 And shall lay thee even with the ground, and thy children within thee; and they shall not leave in thee one stone upon another; because thou knewest not the time of thy visitation.

 1Peter 2:12 Having your conversation honest among the Gentiles: that, whereas they speak against you as evildoers, they may by your good works, which they shall behold, glorify God in the day of visitation.

 

Psalm 115:1 Not unto us, O LORD, not unto us, but unto thy name give glory, for thy mercy, and for thy truth's sake.
