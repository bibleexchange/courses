Salted with Salt

Mark 9:49—For every one shall be salted with fire, and every sacrifice shall be salted with salt.                                                                                                                               

50 Salt is good: but if the salt have lost his saltness, wherewith will ye season it? Have salt in yourselves, and have peace one with another.    (The effects of the Gospel)

 

I                      Every sacrifice shall be salted with salt

a         Every action counts – a good Attitude (He4:16)

b         A name with a good Reputation/not emotion

 

●      Salted with The fire of God’s Spirit to purify

○    (fire of affliction and thorough self-denial)

●      Salted with Hell fire

●      Salted with salt                     With Grace or wrath

Tried with fire here or hereafter.  Self-denial and endurance of our Lord's will, must be the salt and the fire of our sacrifice in this life, or else the endless woes of the wrath of God shall be both preserving salt and consuming fire to us in Hell.

 

It is far better to accept the light afflictions of today, than to endure the fierce flames of perdition forever.

 

The fire and salt of the Lord is - PERFECT JUDGMENT

 

Those who don’t present themselves as living sacrifices to God's grace, shall be made for ever dying sacrifices to his justice, and since they would not give honor to him, he will get him honor upon them if they will not be salted with the salt of divine grace.     Rom.12:1; 2:2; 9:21,22; II Tim. 2:20

 

They would not admit  their corrupt affections, they would not submit to the operation, could not bear the corrosives that were necessary to eat out the proud flesh, it was to them like cutting off a hand, or plucking out an eye; and therefore in hell they shall be salted with fire; coals of fire shall be scattered upon them (Eze 10:2), as salt upon the meat, and brimstone (Job 18:15), as fire and brimstone were rained on Sodom; the pleasures they have lived in, shall eat their flesh, as it were with fire, Jas 5:3.

The pain of mortifying the flesh is no comparison to the punishment for not mortifying it,

Compare salting with burning. Salt will hurt but will produce a cure; fire will purify or punish.

 

The fire of hell shall not be quenched, by the power of God it shall be made to last forever. Mt.10:28;Jn.3:36;Jude7

Those that are cast into hell, will find the fire to have not only the corroding quality of salt, but its preserving quality; whence it is used to signify that which is lasting: a covenant of salt is a perpetual covenant, and Lot's wife being turned into a pillar of salt, made her a remaining monument of divine vengeance.

This will certainly be the doom of those that do not crucify the flesh with its affections and lusts.

 

2Co 5:11 Knowing therefore the terror of the Lord, we persuade men; but we are made manifest unto God; and I trust also are made manifest in your consciences.

II                    Fire and Salt have to do with a process to purify and perfect our faith.

a         Our faith is tried in the coals of fire of His love in us.

i     Being tested in the fire of His love makes our faith pure.

b         Our faith is tried by the influence of the salt of God’s love in us.

i    Being proven with salt keeps us pure.

A man with an experience, seasoned with the Word, Truth, Wisdom, veteran, tested and proven, weathered.

 

To season our conversation with salt; that is, we are to flavor and turn the conversation to tasteful and enjoyable subjects and away from corruptible and tasteless subjects.

Lu 14:34 Salt is good: but if the salt have lost his savour, wherewith shall it be seasoned? (How will the world change?)

Salt is an emblem of purity, integrity, and a continual experience. Goodness faith…     Salt preserves and keeps 

 

Pride, hypocrisy, and sinful indulgence of every sort must be avoided, humility, sincerity,  self-denial diligently cultivated,

 

Perpetual Covenant-Perfect reconciliation - lasting friendship

 

A covenant of salt simply means an unbreakable, indestructible covenant. It is established forever;

 

Dedication: a man must have the salt of discipleship which is self-denial, endurance, renunciation, the sacrifice and giving, of all one is and has

 

Full hearted choices, not half hearted indecisions.

Penetrate and change the very thought (taste) of the earth.

 

1         Salt purifies , is distinctive,  purity "unspotted from the world" godly and righteous

2         Salt preserves. It keeps things from going bad and decaying. It cleanses and disinfects.

Believers, just as salt, are to cleanse and preserve the world. They are to disinfect the world and keep the germs of the world from causing things to go bad. They are to save the world from corruption.

3         Salt penetrates. It inserts a new quality, substance, LIFE, It changes that upon which it is put

4         Salt compensates for what is lacking or needed. Helps to count the cost

 

1.  Search oneself and labor. Evaluate one's "saltiness," one's purity and usefulness.

2.  Be sure to be salted, to have salt. One must be pure and useful.

3.  Live in peace one with another. This is essential.
