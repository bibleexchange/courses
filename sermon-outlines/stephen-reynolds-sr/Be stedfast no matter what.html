Be stedfast no matter what

 

  • Joab was stedfast though David was failing
  • Ruth was stedfast though Naomi was failing
  • Job was stedfast though his family and friends were failing
  • John was stedfast though the other disciples were failing at the cross
  • Peter stood up at Pentecost
