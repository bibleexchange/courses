Title: Going in the Same Direction

Text:

 

Trying to run a home, or sell a product is a hard thing when everyone is going in their own direction. We all need to go in the same direction.

 

We all go to the same book.

 

Psalm 133:1 God wants his people united

Amos 3:3 Can two walk together except they be agreed.

 

Enoch walked with God and was not for God took him. This man’s walk with God was very important to Him. Walk in the direction of God.

 

Col. 3:16

Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly in all wisdom; teaching and admonishing one another in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing with grace in your hearts to the Lord.

 

Psalm 119:11

 

If you desire to sin less against God, then put more of God’s Word in your heart.

 

I Peter 3:15

But sanctify the Lord God in your hearts: and be ready always to give an answer to every man that asketh you a reason of the hope that is in you with meekness and fear
