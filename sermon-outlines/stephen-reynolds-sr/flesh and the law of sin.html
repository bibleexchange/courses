The Conflict between the flesh and the law of sin.  7:23-25

The root of the problem, the cause of our constant defeat by sin: our own carnal mind, the “body of this death” (verse 24).

O wretched man that I am! who shall deliver me from the body of this death?

 

There is an ancient custom of certain tyrants, who bound a dead body to a living man, and  made him to carry the dead body, until it took away his life!”

 

Paul painted a startling picture in order to awaken his readers to the reality of the gruesomeness of sin.

Why professing christians struggle so in the flesh.

 

  • They don’t understand what the law of God is doing for them (pointing them to Christ)
  • nor what the law of sin is doing to them.    (Blinding them from Christ)

 

The difference is between professors and Possessors

 

The Battle Within

Romans 7:23—But I see another law in my members, warring against the law of my mind, and bringing me into captivity to the law of sin which is in my members.

 

The Battle is Won or Lost in the Mind

 

  1. The battle for the mind

 

    1. The devil is after your mind
      1. “warring against the law of my mind”
      2. We can’t win on our own we are in captivity to the law of sin without Christ.
        1. The Devil attacked Eve’s mind—Eve lost
        2. The Devil attacked Christ’s mind—Christ won
    2. The devil is making prisoners
      1. “bringing me into captivity”
      2. Without Jesus Christ all men are taken captive
        1. The devil takes advantage of you as you sin.
        2. The devil can’t resist the Grace of God In your life.
    3. The devil battles through a sinful nature
      1. “the law of sin which is in my members”
      2. You can’t stop your sinning on your own.
        1. The devil is judged by the laws of God.
        2. The works of the devil are destroyed by the Grace of God.
