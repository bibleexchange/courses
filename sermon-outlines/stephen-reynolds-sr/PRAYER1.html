No Condemnation to sentence you.

Ro 8:1 There is therefore now no condemnation to them which are in Christ Jesus, who walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit.

There are two principles in the regenerate, "contrary the one to the other."

Ga 5:17 For the flesh lusteth against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh: and these are contrary the one to the other: so that ye cannot do the things that ye would.

Ga 5:16 This I say then, Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfil the lust of the flesh.

Ga 5:18 But if ye be led of the Spirit, ye are not under the law.

Ga 5:24 And they that are Christ's have crucified the flesh with the affections and lusts.

Ga 5:25 If we live in the Spirit, let us also walk in the Spirit.

Ga 5:26 Let us not be desirous of vain glory, provoking one another, envying one another.

Php 2:3 Let nothing be done through strife or vainglory; but in lowliness of mind let each esteem other better than themselves.

Ro 8:7 Because the carnal mind is enmity against God: for it is not subject to the law of God, neither indeed can be.

The general was usually arrayed in a rich purple robe,

He was carried in a magnificent chariot, adorned with ivory and plates of gold, and usually drawn by two white horses. His children either sat at his feet in the chariot, or rode on the chariot horses.

(Other animals were also used: when Pompey triumphed over Africa, his chariot was drawn by elephants; Mark Antony, by lions;  Heliogabalus, by tigers; Aurelius, by deer.)
