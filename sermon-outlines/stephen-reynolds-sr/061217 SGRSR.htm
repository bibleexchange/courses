TITLE: Highly Favored

TEXT: Luke 13:6-9

 

INTRODUCTION

He was looking for something in HIS vineyard to be PRODUCTIVE.

A fig tree planted IN HIS VINEYARD. Israel is the fig tree and we are the spiritual Israel of God.

V.6 “He came and He sought fruit thereon” He came seeking fruit. That is what the Lord is looking for in our life.
