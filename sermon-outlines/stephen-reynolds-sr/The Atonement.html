  1. God reconciles us by Christ's death
  2. God saves us by Christ's life. "His life" means the life of the living Lord.
  • Christ is seated at the Father’s right hand

Atonement has been provided, redemption has been accomplished, salvation is now.

  • Christ stands before God as our great Intercessor and Mediator. Standing, He stands as the Sinless and Righteous Son of God, as the Ideal and Perfect Man. When we believe in Christ, God takes our belief and counts it as righteousness. The Ideal Righteousness of Christ covers us, and God accepts and saves us because we trust Christ as our Saviour, the living Lord, as our Intercessor and Mediator before God.

 

"Who is he that condemneth? It is Christ that died, yea rather, that is risen again, who is even at the right hand of God, who also maketh intercession for us" (Romans 8:34).
