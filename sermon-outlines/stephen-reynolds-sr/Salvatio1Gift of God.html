Salvation: The Gift of God

  Eph 2:8 For by grace are ye saved

through faith; and that not of

yourselves: it is the gift of God:

 

  1. Salvation is by Grace through Faith—“saved”

 

    1. Salvation is the

Will of God.

    1. Salvation is the

Work of God

 

    1. Salvation is the

Promise of God

    1. Salvation is the

Gift of God

 

 

 

John 4:10 Jesus answered and said unto

her, If thou knewest the gift of God, and

who it is that saith to thee, Give me to

drink; thou wouldest have asked of him,

and he would have given thee living water.

Romans 5:17 “the gift of righteousness

shall reign in life by one, Jesus Christ”

  1. “gift of God”— “Living water”
  2. “gift of righteousness”

“shall reign in life”
