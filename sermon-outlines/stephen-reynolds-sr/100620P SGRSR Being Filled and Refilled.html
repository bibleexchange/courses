Title: Being Filled and Refilled

Text: Philippians 4:11

 

Going by your own emotions will lead you to be destroyed.

Be filled with the fruits of righteousness by Jesus Christ.

The question is not will the obedient be blessed of god, but ’will we be obedient?’. People get tired of doing the things of God.

 

Gentleness, Goodness, faith, temperance, longsuffering…

 

We need to be saved first, followed by the baptism of the Holy ghost and eventually completed with the Fullness of the Spirit.

 

Ezekiel 3:3 Spirit entered into him.

Like honey – its sweet, a blessing, victorious

But men are not feeding on the word of God. Talk about Jesus but live worldly and selfish.

 

We need that oil.

Revelation 3 “Buy of Me” This is not a one time experience! Be filled and refilled!

 

We can’t allow other people’s thoughts, feelings, priorities to dominate our hunger and seeking for God.

 

His joy must remain in us. “In this world you will have tribulation of be of good cheer I have overcome the world…”
