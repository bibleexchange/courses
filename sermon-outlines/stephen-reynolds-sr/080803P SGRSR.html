TITLE: Praise the Lord for His Merciful Kindness

TEXT: Psalm 117:1,2

 

How great has God been toward me?

 

Romans 15:11

And again, Praise the Lord, all ye Gentiles; and laud him, all ye people.

 

Men have learned by precept of men and become bored by instruction.

 

Mark the things God has done and praise Him for that.

Remember the things God has promised and believe Him for that.

 

We need the genuine power of the Holy Ghost and no substitute.

 

  • The expectations of many are bound to what they feel.

 

Romans 15:6

That ye may with one mind and one mouth glorify God, even the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ.

 

  • God has revealed his mercy and will reveal greater mercy in these last days.
  • If there was something in my life that God was not pleased with would I change that tonight?

 

Romans 15:7

Wherefore receive ye one another, as Christ also received us to the glory of God.

 

v.5 God of Patience

v.13 God of Hope

v.33 God of Peace

 

à Praise God through the difficulty, our God is a God of patience.

à God is bigger than the test. He is the God that overcomes in all situations.

 

à God has been true to His Word. We need to set our heart and mind on the God of hope.

à His hope is the unseen hope.

 

à The Spirit of God is more powerful. Worship God and God does the working. We don’t have to work in nervous energy and but learn to worship God in the realm of the Spirit.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Ro 15:2

Let every one of us please his neighbour for his good to edification.

 

  • Empty out of self. Get free from yourself.
  • No room for self and only room for God.
  • Believe God for it.

 

Praise à Laud : not just one time; for a service our two, but each time, each place. There is an order that God has for this service.

 

The order of the Holy Ghost is to exalt the CHRIST/ the SAVIOUR.
