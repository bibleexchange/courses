Rev. Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

June 25, 2006

Sunday Night

Title: A Vision for Restoration

Text: Proverbs 29:18,19; Luke 10:23-28

 

Restore Faith, Confidence, the joy of salvation. These things are to be in abundance.

We must be awake. We cannot grow half dead.

 

“Am I my brother’s keeper?” In one sense Cain was not Abel’s keeper because he did not love him. Cain was Abel’s killer. Where are we? Are we our neighbor’s keeper or killer? They are troubled.

 

If we have vision of the Word and do that we will be happy.
