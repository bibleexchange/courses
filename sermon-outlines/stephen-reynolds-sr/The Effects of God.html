The Effects of God’s Grace

 

Ps 41:11 By this I know that thou favourest me, because mine enemy doth not triumph over me.

 

  1. I know God’s Grace is favoring  me.

 

 

  1. I know God’s Grace is working in me.

 

 

  1. I know God’s Grace is stopping the enemy from working in me.

 

 

  1. The Grace of God is effecting me.
  1. “By this”

 

  1. By this grace that belongs to me through faith reveals to me that I don’t have to be stopped.

 

  1. By this grace that stops my enemy helps me to get going.

 

 

  1. By this grace that favor’s me helps me to keep going

 

 

  1. By this grace we will finish the task unto completion

 

“By this”God’s grace helps me to affect people

God would have the righteous see hurting people not just problem people.

 

The cause (of our past experiences) serves only as a rudder to direct us to trust God again rather than an anchor to hold us to the past.

 

Help people make wise decisions to come out bad situations by depending on God’s grace.

 

Help people to understand and choose the effects that God’s grace causes, for the end results always is Peace with God.
