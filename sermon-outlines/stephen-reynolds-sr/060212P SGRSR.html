Rev. Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

February 12, 2006

Sunday Evening

Title: Championing God’s Cause

Text: Romans 12:21

 

God has a cause to show us the good in life.

Good doesn’t just come, but is produced by the Word of God. It isn’t just a certain mindset that is going to guarantee certain victory, but if you put your disposition in line with the Word of God then you will find victory. It isn’t just looking at evil and seeing it for itself, but we need to be of a different character, conduct, condition, condemnation that the world is in, but let us receive the nature of Christ and change in our lives.

 

Be not overcome of evil.

The possibilities of being overcome of evil are multiplied as sin is magnified. But if you look at God’s Word, God’s word gives victory over sin. The Word of God is able to make us Overcomers over sin in our own life and sin in the world. Overcome evil with good with a right character and right condition of the heart.

 

He who knows God best and loves God best will resemble Him the most. If want to love God then get the Word of God in you.
