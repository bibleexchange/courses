THESIS: The Flood left evidence of the judgment of God.

TEXT: Psalm 136:6

 

We have our Bible and ought to use it.

 

God is merciful.

God brings Judgment.

 

God brings judgment now, so we won’t have to live in judgment forever.

We see the judgment of God very clear. What makes the judgment more important to us is that we see the mercy of God in it. He sends mercy. How? He sends you and I. Blessed are the merciful for they shall obtain mercy.

 

If the house next door was on fire you would do all you could to rescue those inside.

 

Where is the feeling of mercy to save today those that are lost.. We have not time, we will have plenty of time, they aren’t that bad, God won’t judge them---are some of the lies the devil feeds us.

 

--God sent rain for forty days and forty nights.

--God opened up the fountains of the deep.

            (God works in parallel to His own laws.)

            Laws are there to protect.

 

The effects of the flood are seen still today. We do not know how long we have. The Bible warns us against “Oh, I think I have time. I feel like we have another year.”

 

A true scientist will read his Bible. Judgment is not only possible, but is a must. Mercy as well is a must.

 

You become the most miserable person if all you see is the judgment of God. When God feeds your soul with His mercy, you could be at your lowest point still, it changes you. Our focus should be on the mercy of God.

 

(Judgment is imminent; Mercy is imminent. = focus on mercy)

 

God has judged, he filled the earth with a flood. 150 days

There are things that are not sinful in themselves, but we must be careful the motive that moves us to dress. Our motives must be pleasing to God.

 

God has left evidence, he knows how to judge. But has God shown mercy, longsuffering, forbearing? How long? How far? Lets not rely wholly on that for there will come an end.
