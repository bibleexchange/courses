Gal. 2:16—Knowing that a man is not justified by the works of the law, but by the faith of Jesus Christ, even we have believed in Jesus Christ, that we might be justified by the faith of Christ, and not by the works of the law: for by the works of the law shall no flesh be justified.

20—I am crucified with Christ: nevertheless I live; yet not I, but Christ liveth in me: and the life which I now live in the flesh I live by the faith of the Son of God, who loved me, and gave himself for me.

 

“the life which I now live in the flesh”

 

  1. Man in the flesh
    1. Existing without Jesus Christ
      1. Is unjust- utterly corrupt
      2. cannot be justified- condemned
    2. living by the faith of Jesus Christ
      1. Justified – old nature /crucified - Newborn
      2. Justified by faith – christ liveth in me
  2. Man without faith
    1. Cannot be justified
    2. cannot please God     heb.11:6
    3. Cannot live for God
  3. Man Crucified that the life of the faith of Jesus Christ might be made manifest
  4. Man Crucified with Christ, receives  Christ, by the faith of Christ
