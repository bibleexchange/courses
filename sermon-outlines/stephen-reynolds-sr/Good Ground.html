Good Seed-Good Ground-Good Results

Text: Mt 13:8 But other fell into good ground, and brought forth fruit, some an hundredfold, some sixtyfold, some thirtyfold.

“But other fell” (falling seeds)     according to your understanding and knowledge

“good ground”    according to your receptiveness     How much, what and who?

The seed doesn’t change but peoples understanding of the Word changes and fluctuates.

Ro 2:7 To them who by patient continuance in well doing seek for glory and honour and immortality, eternal life:

“in well doing seek” well-doer seeks: "by patient continuance" (hupomonēn) - be stedfast and constant; to endure, persevere, stick to, and continue.

cheerful (or hopeful) endurance, constancy:--enduring, patience, patient continuance (waiting).

The well-doer is faithful in doing good works.

  • He does not just start, he finishes.
  • He does not live an inconsistent, up and down life. He continues and keeps on doing good deeds.
  • He does not give in to hardships, difficulties, or opposition. He endures and perseveres, always doing good.
