Accountability and Discipline Are Signs of God’s Love

Church discipline “accountability” is criticized in this modern age. It is desperately needed in all ministries and one of the greatest blessings God has given to his people.

The Bible never presents church discipline as being negative, legalistic or harsh, as modern society does. True discipline originates from God himself and is always presented as a sign of genuine love.

The Lord disciplines those he loves
