A stairway leads to the platform upon which stands the figure of Faith. The whole monument is about 150 feet high, and 80 feet at the base.                                                                                                 The Statue of Faith rests her foot upon the Forefather's Rock; in her left hand she, holds an open Bible; with the right hand lifted up she points to heaven. Looking downward, as to those she is addressing, she calls them to trust in God. The sitting figures are emblematic of the principles upon which the Pilgrims proposed to found their Commonwealth.                                

 

De 32:20 And he said, I will hide my face from them, I will see what their end shall be: for they are a very froward generation, children in whom is no faith.

The first is Morality.

  1. She holds the Ten Commandments in her left, and the Scroll of Revelation in her right hand.
  2.  Her look is upward, towards heaven.
  3.  In a niche, on one side of her throne, is a Prophet, and in the other, one of the Evangelists.

The second is Law.

  1.  On one side of his seat is Justice;
  2.  On the other, Mercy.

The third is Education.

  1. On one side of her seat, is Wisdom, ripe with years;
  2. On the other, Youth, led by Experience.

The fourth is Freedom.

  1. On one side, Peace rests under his protection;
  2. On the other, Tyranny overthrown by his prowess.

The Statue of Faith itself is 70 feet high, and the sitting figures are 38 feet high, — Making it in magnitude the greatest work of its kind in the world; It will afford insight and pleasure to every American citizen.
