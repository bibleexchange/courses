Eight powerful Characteristics for Measuring Motherhood

 

1. She seeks always the good of her husband which develops his trust in her.      Proverbs 31:10-13

 

2. She is industrious and, therefore, has no time to be a busybody.              Proverbs 31:13-15, 19, 21-24

 

3. She is constantly looking after the welfare of her family.      

                         Proverbs 31:27

 

4. She has good judgment when it comes to the financial well-being of her family.

       Proverbs 31:16-18

 

5. She spends the time in ministering to the needs of those outside her own family.

       Proverbs 31:21

 

6. She is honorable, wise and kind.

       Proverbs 31:25-26

 

7. She is one who firmly fears (reverences) the Lord.

 

Proverbs 31:30—Favour is deceitful, and beauty is vain: but a woman that feareth the LORD, she shall be praised.

 

8. She is blessed and praised by her children and her husband.

 

Proverbs 31:28—Her children arise up, and call her blessed; her husband also, and he praiseth her.
