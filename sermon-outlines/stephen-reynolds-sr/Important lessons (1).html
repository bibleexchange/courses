Important lessons: love, kindness, selflessness and humility.

A boy loved a blind girl. She too loved him. On one day the girl had an operation and got back her eyesight. Then she realised the boy was also blind. So she said she could not marry him since he was blind. The boy committed suicide out of depression. Later when the girl went to his house she saw a letter in which he had written "Take care of my eyes my dear!"
