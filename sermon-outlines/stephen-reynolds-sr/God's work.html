What hath God wrought!

 No plan of men or devils can succeed against the elect of God.  We have no cause to fear evil omens, in fact, it would be sinful to do so.  It is wicked to feel the superstitious fear of the old heathen.  No magical arts, Satanic devices, or malicious plotting can really injure the beloved of the Lord.

 

God's work shall baffle man's work and excite wonder when human malice is forgotten.
