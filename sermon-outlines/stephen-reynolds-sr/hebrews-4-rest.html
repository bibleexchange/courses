Heb 4:12 For the word of God is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any twoedged sword, piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit, and of the joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart.

 a.  The Word of God is "quick" (zaw  zao,  dzah'-o) lively, alive, living and life giving. The idea is that the Word of God is always alive and active; it is always working and quickening its message to the human heart. Therefore, God's word of rest is not a dead and meaningless promise; it is living, full of life and will quicken that life to the heart of the believer.

b.  The Word of God is "powerful" (energhv  energes,  en-er-gace') active, effectual, operative, working, energizing. God's word of promise, His rest of salvation, is powerful. It is not dead, dormant nor inactive. It is actually active and working, operating, energizing the heart of the believer.

c.  The Word of God is "sharper than any two edged sword." The word "sharper" (tomwterov  tomoteros,  tom-o'-ter-os) means to cut. It is penetrating and convicting. It does not leave a soul alone to suffer in sin. The Word of God will not let a soul ignore God's promise of rest.

d.  The Word of God is "piercing" (diikneomai  diikneomai,  dee-ik-neh'-om-ahee) to reach through, penetrate or pierce. It goes right through  the soul and spirit of man. It pierces and separates a man's soul and spirit just as a sword pierces a man's joints and marrow.

It is the Word of God that pierces man's soulish nature and separates it from the spiritual.

It separates an unforgiving soul from a forgiving spirit

It separates a proud soul from a humble spirit.

It separates a sinful soul from a righteous spirit.

It separates a rebellious soul from an obedient spirit.

It separates an unbelieving soul from a believing spirit.

It separates an evil soul from an excellent spirit

It separates a lustful soul from a loving spirit

It separates  from death    

It makes the soul alive and quickens the spirit.
