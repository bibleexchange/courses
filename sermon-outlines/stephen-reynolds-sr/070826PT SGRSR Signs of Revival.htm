TITLE: Signs of Revival

TEXT: Mark 16:17

 

And He said unto them, it is and clarified hwo they are in verse fourteen…unto the eleven….as they sat at meat and he spoke unto them. Verse Fourteen says that they needed encouragement. If you need help and encouragement don’t fight the messenger or fight God. We are against unbelief, and doubt and not against you.

 

How many know Job’s life was an object lesion for us to believe God. James 5:--trust God through the same. You can believe God above any hardship or trouble. There are signs of revival in the land, but the signs need to increase in my heart. There is a demand. A commandment of God that these signs follow us. We MUST believe. We cannot look for signs ad then believe. I have a responsibility to a man of God for the hour. Like a Daniel or Noah.

 

The signs of Revival are in the air.

 

They are being shown.

 

Sometimes people let the things just pass on by.

 

Acts 8:4

 

What is a sign of revival? But when someone realizes the WORD must be preached.
