TITLE: Taught by the Lord

TEXT: Isaiah 54:13

 

ISAIAH 54:13 ‘And all thy children shall be taught of the Lord…’

 

the Lord is going to speak, he is going to teach, have that in your spirit, in your heart. The devil is going to fight that and divide that and destroy that. The devil would like you to doubt and fear, but God is going to teach us. Teaching does not come without training. With training comes a task. What is it that God wants us to do? What would the Lord have us to do?

 

There are a lot of things we could do, but our responsibility is to follow God, do his will, serve Him, love Him. IT is the Lord that is teaching us.

 

There is peace in knowing that we are serving Him and loving Him. It is not just these hands that He wants working, but He wants our heart working. I NEED TO BE TAUGHT BY HIM.

 

JOHN 6:45 There is something that God wants to do in these last days that He has not taught to or spoken to other people before. Things we have never known and things the prophets have never known. We need to be taught of the Lord.
