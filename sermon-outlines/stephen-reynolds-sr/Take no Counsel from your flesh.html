Don’t be thinking and having feelings without writing.

 

Don’t be talking about your thoughts and feelings with yourself or anyone else.

 

You must be in prayer and let the Lord Help You.

 

  1. You have a thought you write it down.
  2. You take it to the Lord in prayer immediately.
  3. Every time that thought is going to change as the Lord helps you.
  4. Now write, also after prayer, your thought has changed so you go back and write what your thought is now and how your thought has changed with the Lord’s help
  5. This gives all the Glory to Jesus, with a record for a reminder that you can’t trust yourself.
