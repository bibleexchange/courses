Trees of Righteousness

  1. Trees bearing fruit – (a fruit bearing tree)

Mt 3:8 Bring forth therefore fruits meet for repentance:

Show the reality of your repentance by forsaking your sins and obeying the commands of God.

  • Repentance must follow faith.
  • Faith must work by love
  • Faith must produce good works.
  • Faith must follow chastening
  • Faith must follow peace

2Pe 1:2 Grace and peace be multiplied unto you through the knowledge of God, and of Jesus our Lord,
