  • The Abundance of Spiritual Blessings

All Covenant Blessings

Eph 1:3 Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who hath blessed us with all spiritual blessings in heavenly places in Christ:

 

Five things should be noted about this.

 

  1. Spiritual blessings are for your Spirit.

 

It is man’s Spirit that should control man not the circumstances that surround him.

A man may feel bad; his thoughts down, depressing and oppressing; but if his spirit is strong, he arises and conquers his feelings. He controls his own spirit and overcomes the oppressing circumstances, and he lives a victorious obedient life.

 

But if his spirit is weak, whether at work or at play, he often wallows around in self-pity, grumbling and griping and living in defeat having no victory.

 

The days turn into weeks and months into years until a person's life is down more than it is up because the spirit is too weak to conquer.

 

The major blessings of God are spiritual that enable a person to control his life.

 

Eph 1:3 Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who hath blessed us with all spiritual blessings in heavenly places in Christ:

 

Mat. 26:41 Watch and pray, that ye enter not into temptation: the spirit indeed is willing, but the flesh is weak.

 

  1. Spiritual blessings are the very opposite of temporal blessings. They are the blessings of the inner man, the blessings for the new man, But of all blessings, they are the most glorious and satisfying.

 

They are the blessings that erase the loneliness and purposelessness of man.

They are the blessings that give man an over-abundance of life.

The most glorious and satisfying. but must guard from pride, lukewarmness, lack of (prayer and bible reading)

3.  Spiritual blessings are superior to material blessings. They are permanent and perfect and eternal, lasting forever. They are of the very same nature as God Himself. Spiritual blessings exist and can be experienced upon earth

 

4.  Spiritual blessings are found only in Christ.

 

Jesus Christ has been raised from the dead and exalted to the right hand of God the Father. He is in heaven, surrounded by all the heavenly atmosphere and blessings.

All heavenly blessings are His; He is Lord and Possesser of all blessings. Therefore, if a person is to experience the spiritual blessings, he must be in Christ. If a person is in Christ, then he sits in heaven with Christ. How is this possible?

 

When a person believes in Christ, truly believes, God takes his faith and counts it as righteousness. God counts the person to be the same as Christ, righteous and acceptable. In God's mind faith in Christ makes a person just like Christ: holy and righteous and acceptable for heaven. Therefore, when a person believes in Christ, God's mind sees the person in Christ;

 

God sees the person identified with Christ, seated in heaven.

 

And being seated in heaven, the person can experience all the blessings of heaven.

 

Simply stated, to be in Christ means to believe in God's Son so much that God  counts the person to be just like Christ: acceptable and worthy to be blessed with all the blessings of heaven.

 

Ga 2:16 Knowing that a man is not justified by the works of the law, but by the faith of Jesus Christ, even we have believed in Jesus Christ, that we might be justified by the faith of Christ, and not by the works of the law: for by the works of the law shall no flesh be justified.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

5.  God dealt with man in material blessings.

a.  Earthly inheritance’s are subject to being lost, stolen, deteriorateing or else we leave our material possessions behind for others.

b.  Earthly nation’s and material inheritance’s cannot bring peace and security. Peace and security are of the spirit.

c. Earthly nations and material things are of the earth and of a corruptible nature.

d. Nations and material things do not solve the spiritual struggle that man senses within his own being.

e. Nations and material things do not erase the spiritual divisions between men and between man and God.

f..  Man has within his inner being a basic selfishness and greed. Man finds a tendency, an unregulated urge, that desires and seeks the material and hoards the corruptible to the neglect of the spiritual.

    1. Must be born again and receive blessings to be freed of this urge, this tendency that causes so much bondage, disruption, and division within one's self and between men.
    2. Man must be born again, made into a new creation, created into a new man—spiritually, permanently, perfectly, eternally. Man must be recreated by the hand of God Himself.

  • Redemption is made possible by the sacrifice of the Son of God.
  • Redemption is a purely legal term, used in scripture to denote the atoning work of Jesus Christ
    • Man is encumbered Property
      • The legal process for redeeming
        • There must be the right to redeem, we need a near kinsman.
          • No man can’t redeem his own soul
    • There must be payment by death of a sinless sacrifice
      • As the payment is believed upon the encumbrance is lifted.
      • The time was ordained Gen.3:15; Gal.4:4
