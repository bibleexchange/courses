Brethren Beloved of the Lord

From “Bound to give thanks” to

Comforted and established

 

2Th 2:13 ¶ But we are bound to give thanks alway to God for you, brethren beloved of the Lord, because God hath from the beginning chosen you to salvation through sanctification of the Spirit and belief of the truth:

14 Whereunto he called you by our gospel, to the obtaining of the glory of our Lord Jesus Christ.

15 Therefore, brethren, stand fast, and hold the traditions which ye have been taught, whether by word, or our epistle.

16 Now our Lord Jesus Christ himself, and God, even our Father, which hath loved us, and hath given us everlasting consolation and good hope through grace,

17 Comfort your hearts, and stablish you in every good word and work.

 

The contrast between the followers of the antichrist and the followers of Christ. It is a passage that should speak with force to the heart of the believer.

 

“Chosen you to salvation”

 

  • God wants us to be SAVED
  • God wants us to be with Him.

  • God does not want us to be judged and condemned and separated from Him.
