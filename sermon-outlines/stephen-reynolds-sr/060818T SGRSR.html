TITLE: They Shall See the Glory of God

TEXT: Isaiah 35:2

 

John 11-Take ye away the stone, hindrances, they did not know exactly what COULD be done at this time. If thou wouldest believe, thou shouldest see the glory of God, then they took away the stone. The Word of God broke up their unbelief and hesitance. Unbelief will choke out the Word you do have. The bad apple will always cause the good to rot. The church has not be challenged to excersise their faith. They pray that someday Good will pour out and show His glory, but for them to actually wait on God and believe God to do something at THIS TIME.

 

Isaiah 32 The hosts of heaven in position and under His control and direction and authority. Where there is a Believer no matter what resistance there is, they don’t change the Word any. If we obey the Word, God will glorify Himself.

 

Isaiah 6 The cherubiums job is to glorify God and magnify His name.

35:2,4 He’ll come with a vengeance against sin and the wickedness of men. Against the things that hinder your faith. He will cause brooks and streams to appear in the solitary and desert places. Beccause of the glory of God and His presence and power. His glory fills the earth. If we can BELIEVE HIM. Where men believe Him, He manifests His glory.

 

Paul was beaten and his companions, left with open sores and cast into a prison. They praised God in song at midnight. They called for a light. Christ is the true light. He asked what must I do to be saved?

 

John 4 Leaving Cana and then returning, Christ is met by a man requestion help for his son. THY SON LIVETH. (the word given at the 7th hour.) It was believing the Word that helped Him to believe to see the power of God manifested for his son. He can do all things. V.53 Himself believed and his house also. He believed and stood upon the Word. If thou wouldest believe, thou shouldest see the glory of God. This is the work of God that men might believe. John 6:29
