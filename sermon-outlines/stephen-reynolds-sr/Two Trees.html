Two Trees

Jer 10:4

"one cutteth a tree out of the forest"

 

I. The Christmas Tree

 A. Pagan in origin

    1. The Cross is being replaced by the Christmas tree and becomes the center of the Christmas holiday.

    4. They place a star at the top of the tree.

     a. Why? Remember the star the wise men followed? The Devil is so clever.

    3. They even worship the Christmas tree.

     a. They sing songs to the Christmas tree.

     * (a). Oh Christmas tree

      b. They place their gifts at the foot of the tree.

II. The Tree Of Life (The Cross)

  A. Not the tree itself but what the tree means to us is holy in origin.

     1. What Jesus did through the cross brings to us the greatest gift ever given.

      a. Eternal life through his own shed blood.
