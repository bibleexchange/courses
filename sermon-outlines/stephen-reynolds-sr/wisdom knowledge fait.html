I Cor. 12:8 For to one is given by the Spirit the word of wisdom; to another the word of knowledge by the same Spirit;  9  To another faith by the same Spirit;

I Cor. 12:1  Now concerning spiritual gifts, brethren, I would not have you ignorant.

 

  • I Cor. 1:5 That in everything ye are enriched by him, in all utterance, and in all knowledge;
  • Eph. 1:8 Wherein he hath abounded toward us in all wisdom and prudence;
  • Php. 1:9 And this I pray, that your love may abound yet more and more in knowledge and in all judgment;
  • Col. 1:9 For this cause we also, since the day we heard it, do not cease to pray for you, and to desire that ye might be filled with the knowledge of his will in all wisdom and spiritual understanding;

 

Though Wisdom and Knowledge are difficult to separate and operate without each other in the natural, because both have to do with utterance and intellect, The Holy Ghost can give man what he needs supernaturally.

  • Wisdom is connected more to the spirit of man.
  • Knowledge is connected more to the mind of man.
  • Faith connects more to the heart and soul of man.

Psalm 119:66 Teach me good judgment and knowledge: for I have believed thy commandments.

 

Isaiah 40:14 With whom took he counsel, and who instructed him, and taught him in the path of judgment, and taught him knowledge, and shewed to him the way of understanding?

Dan. 2:20 Daniel answered and said, Blessed be the name of God forever and ever: for wisdom and might are his:

Romans 11:33 O the depth of the riches both of the wisdom and knowledge of God! how unsearchable are his judgments, and his ways past finding out!

Col 2:3 In whom are hid all the treasures of wisdom and knowledge.

 

I Cor. 2:7 But we speak the wisdom of God in a mystery, even the hidden wisdom, which God ordained before the world unto our glory:

 

II Cor. 8:7 Therefore, as ye abound in everything, in faith, and utterance, and knowledge, and in all diligence, and in your love to us, see that ye abound in this grace also.

II Peter 1:5-8  And beside this, giving all diligence, add to your faith virtue; and to virtue knowledge…charity.

 

Gal. 5:5 For we through the Spirit wait for the hope of righteousness by faith.

 6 For in Jesus Christ neither circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision; but faith which worketh by love.

  • Gifts enable us to do.
  • Grace enables us to be.

Grace is God’s favor of love and mercy put to our account that we may draw from by faith.

 

James 3:17 But the wisdom that is from above is first pure, then peaceable, gentle, and easy to be intreated, full of mercy and good fruits, without partiality, and without hypocrisy.

 

Psalm 51:6 Behold, thou desirest truth in the inward parts: and in the hidden part thou shalt make me to know wisdom.

Prov. 23:23 Buy the truth, and sell it not; also wisdom, and instruction, and understanding.

Luke 21:15 For I will give you a mouth and wisdom, which all your adversaries shall not be able to gainsay nor resist.

 

Acts 6:9 Then there arose certain of the synagogue, which is called the synagogue of the Libertines, and Cyrenians, and Alexandrians, and of them of Cilicia and of Asia, disputing with Stephen.

 10 And they were not able to resist the wisdom and the spirit by which he spake.

 

  1. The Word of Wisdom is the Truth of God’s Will revealed that you might know for yourself and also to help another to better understand God’s Will and Eternal purpose.
  2. The Word of Knowledge is the Knowledge given by the Spirit to help us appropriate the promises.
  3. The gift of Faith is for something specific that needs immediate attention.
