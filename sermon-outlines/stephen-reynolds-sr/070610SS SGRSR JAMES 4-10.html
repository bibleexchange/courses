TITLE: Humbled & Lifted Up

TEXT: James 4:10

 

If you humble yourself, God will use you!

 

The Promise requires faith & the Promise requires humility.

 

I can humble myself today by the Lord’s help.

 

JAMES 4:10

Humble yourselves in the sight of the Lord, and he shall lift you up.

 

Let God choose what He is going to do with me. “HE SHALL LIFT YOU UP.” It is a guarantee—if we do our part He will do his.But before there is a lifting up, I must humble myself.

 

PROVERBS 15:33

The fear of the LORD is the instruction of wisdom; and before honour is humility.

 

“I realy want God to do something with me, to lift me up.”ßthe key is simple obedience out of a humble heart.

 

Our being honored by God is because we have HONORED HIM.

 

“instruction of wisdom” ß we don’t even know how, we must be taught

 

This begins with a GODLY REVERANCE, FEARING GOD (James 4:7 “Submit”)

 

Repentance is one of the first steps. Turn away from sin and turn toward God.

 

Soulwinning is more than just passing out tracts. My life must eminent with the truth and light inside I have received every day before the lost.

 

2 Timothy 3:16

All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness:

 

ALL INSPIRED RECORD but not all inspired utterances.

 

If I have a humble heart of reverence, He will instruct me. “instruction in righteousness.”

 

Isaiah 66:2

For all those things hath mine hand made, and all those things have been, saith the LORD: but to this man will I look, even to him that is poor and of a contrite spirit, and trembleth at my word.

 

PROVERBS 1:7

he fear of the LORD is the beginning of knowledge: but fools despise wisdom and instruction.
