Rev. Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

Deliverance Center, Adult Sunday School

 

TITLE: Overcoming Temptation

TEXT: James 4:7-10

 

God is…

Good, Faithful, almighty, true, salvation, etc.

 

I can never make myself like God. That is the devil’s lie from the Beginning Genesis 3. There is no possible way to becomelike God on your own. He has to do it. He has designed a plan and it workds and it is good. How many times have I felt miserable when I did wrong? Thank God He did not let me be happy in my sin. We are thinking about What is the Lord Like? Who is the Lord?

 

Satan is the tempter, what is the Lord?

  • The one Who makes a way of escape
  • Our Help
  • He makes us Free
  • God cannot be moved, set, established, always knows what He is going to do

 

The best way to deal with temptation is before it ever starts.

 

I Thessalonians 3:5 Who is the tempter? Does the Lord ever tempt me?

 

The Lord is not a tempter, the devil is the tempter.

 

I Thessalonians 2:4 àGod trieth our hearts. There is a big difference between tempting you and trying your heart.

 

  1. The First Key to Overcoming Temptation is Submitting Yourself to God, Ja. 4:7

This submission is not just when temptation is upon you. That would be like walking on the edge of a cliff. We must know God and that He is going to test us: Get away from the edge.

Matthew 4:1 The devil is the tempter, God tries the Heart.

Deuteronomy 8:2

Mt. 4& Lk. 4 The tempter comes by to tempt Christ to satisfy the flesh in response to his words. That is the key, there is noting wrong with eating. Especially when you have gone 40 days without food. The key is the fact that the devil is behind it.

I don’t like silk on a man. I was taught when you go to church you wear a dress shirt and tie. My tie is probably made of silk. But, I am not going to strain at gnats. I am not going to touch what the devil says. Instead of arguing is this right or wrong or is that person right or wrong, but find out if the design of devil is involved. Luke 22:31

Jesus is being asked three questions: 1)If you be the son of God, make bread of these stones. The devil is always looking for opportunity to best tempt. Jesus had been fasting for 40 days. The stones were no problem for Christ, but He would not doing anything to satisfy the devil. There is nothing wrong with making bread out of stones. What is wrong is the suggestion of the devil.

You can know the difference between the devil the tempter and God the tester. God tests us with His Word.

What is it in my heart that the devil is using to tempt me? Get it out! I don’t have to live a life of constantly perpetually being tempted.

James 1:2 Temptations will be MANY not few. But not forever, not all day long seven days a week.

 

  1. The Second Key is Walking in the Path of Duty, (IN HIS WILL< GOD IS MY PROTECTOR) You will never fail trusting God.

 

In the midst of the temptation is not the time to start learning.

 

James 1:12-16
