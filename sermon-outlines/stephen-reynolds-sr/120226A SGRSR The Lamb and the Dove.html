II.           The nature of the dove Harmless as Doves

Doves are gentle–Doves don’t get rough, hiss, jeer, taunt, sneer, ridicule, disdain, scorn –Doves are Harmless–Doves don’t strike at each other.

A.  We should have the harmless, gentle and peaceful nature of Christ.
