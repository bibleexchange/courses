Title: Prayer is a Privilege and Powerful

Text: Luke 11:1

THY KINGDOM COME…submission, God reigning in our lives, grace reigns through righteousness.

Colossians 3:22

 

THY WILL BE DONE...servants, Ephesians 6:6 “willing and obedient in all things”

 

GIVE US THIS DAY OUR DAILY BREAD…substance, Hebrews 11:1, we need daily bread physically, but even more important, spiritually. It takes the word of God to brings substance to your soul. It is not merely a simple surface reading. He will share with a friend some things, he would not share with a servant. Relationship is very important. John 15:14-17. In a position of a friend, knowledge is imparted in greater levels.

 

FORGIVE US—forgiveness is necessary, and we are to forgive everyone.
