Profiting from God’s Ultimate Purpose

 

Isaiah 48:17 Thus saith the LORD, thy Redeemer, the Holy One of Israel; I am the LORD thy God which teacheth thee to profit, which leadeth thee by the way that thou shouldest go.

 

“profit” 03276.  ley  ya`al,  yaw-al'

To ascend; to be valuable (useful, benefited) set forward, can do good, (be, have) profitable.

 

Ascend above being in captivity, discouragement and unbelief.

 

  1. Removed from the captives but also remove the things from captivity.
  2. Removing discouragements
    1. Unworthiness discourages people from even trying.
    2. Unbelief discourages those that try.
  3. Removing unbelief
    1. Unbelief hardens the heart.
    2. Unbelief feeds off from the deceitfulness of sin.
