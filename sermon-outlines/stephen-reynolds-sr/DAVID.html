“Behold, the days come”---

Jer 23:5 Behold, the days come, saith the LORD, that I will raise unto David a righteous Branch, and a King shall reign and prosper, and shall execute judgment and justice in the earth.

  1. The Coming of the Lord is revealed in this prophecy in a plural of “days”
    1. He came the first time (33yrs) as a suffering Saviour and hung on the cross in our place
    2. He will come the second time (one day) secretly in the air for those found watching.
    3. He will come and reveal Himself to the whole world and reign for 1000 years
