TITLE: A Cry of Repentance in the Land

TEXT: Jeremiah 14:7-22

 

INTRODUCTION

The cry is for water and rain because their joy and fruit is gone and not for REPENTANCE. 

The cry of repentance needs to be in the land. Cry this Cry! Call out to repentance. More than just words, but BELIEVING God to bring it about. Rebellion on the streets is hard and difficult. God help us.

A wayfaring man to tarry for a night—

Matthew 24:7 False prophets prophesy there will be no famine
