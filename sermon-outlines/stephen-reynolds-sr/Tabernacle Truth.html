Tabernacle Truth’s

Seeing CHRIST in the TABERNACLE

 

Some of the greatest spiritual truths to be found in the Bible are found in the

study of the tabernacle. We must have a firm grasp of these “tabernacle truths” if we are to understand some portions of the New Testament, such as the epistle to the Romans and the epistle of Hebrews.

 

The key to understanding the tabernacle and it’s purpose is Jesus Christ.

 

To understand the purpose and nature of the tabernacle, we can go back to the opening chapters of the book of Genesis.

 

1. God’s Perfect Provision: Genesis 2

A) A perfect God, a perfect human race, a perfect environment. Gen. 2:7/ Gen. 1:31

B) Fellowship with God was His Divine Purpose and the high point of human activity.

C) With the creation of man, God pronounced His creation “very good” Gen. 1:31

 

II. God’s Perfect Provision Rejected: Genesis 3

 

A) With all the perfection it did not keep man from sinning. Genesis 3:6

B) Sin bought disastrous consequences. Genesis 3: 23,24

C) Man was driven out of the garden. Genesis 3:24

No longer could man live in the place where God dwelled.

D) The separation of man and God had taken place. Genesis 3:23

 

SEPARATION from God is the point TO THE STUDY OF THE TABERNACLE!

Restored fellowship through Jesus Christ is the message of the Tabernacle for man.

 

III.God’s Purpose Of The Tabernacle: Exodus 25:8 “Three Reasons”

 

A) Fellowship. Through the tabernacle, God would provide a way in which man

could have personal fellowship with the God who created him.

B) Protection. The tabernacle protected sinful man from the glory of God’s holy

and righteous presence.

C) Revelation.

 

1. God revealed Himself—Exodus 33: 7-10

2. God revealed man apart from God—Lev. 1:1-4

Approach to God is through a burnt sacrifice

A totally consumed blood sacrifice

3. God revealed the means by which man may be reconciled to God.

Atonement through a blood Sacrifice. Lev. 1:4
