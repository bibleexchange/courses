Adult Sunday School

August 7, 2011

 

The fruit of Holiness

 

Rom. 6:22 But now being made free from sin, and become servants to God, ye have your fruit unto holiness, and the end everlasting life.

 

Ps. 86:2 Preserve my soul; for I am holy: O thou my God, save thy servant that trusteth in thee.

17 Shew me a token for good; that they which hate me may see it, and be ashamed: because thou, LORD, hast holpen me, and comforted me.

 

Rom. 6:22 “become servants to God”

Ps 86:5 For thou, Lord, art good, and ready to forgive; and plenteous in mercy unto all them that call upon thee.

Ps. 86:17 “Shew me a token for good”

 

  • Make all your goodness to be in me.
  • Mark me as your servant

 

  • Own me as your own
  • Make with me a sign.
  • Fix the honorable mark of thy name upon me, that I may be known to be thy servant.

 

The slave was marked, to show whose property he was.

 

Give grace that fruit will appear in my countenance,

 

 "that they which hate me may see"

 

Such, that they will be confounded; for thou, O Lord, hast helped me in struggle, consoled me in sorrow, and turned my sadness into joy and gladness.

 

  • Help me, in struggle and comfort me, in sorrow.

 

  • How can you hurt a person, assisted by the Lord?

Ps 86:2 Preserve my soul; for I am holy: O thou my God, save thy servant that trusteth in thee.

 

“for I am holy”

 

We don’t belong to our self.

 

Humility is best defined as coming to the end of yourself.

 

HUMILTY – Come to the end of yourself

 

GET RID OF YOUR TERMS

 

Are you ready to give your all to Him?

 

Are you ready to give your all for the Gospel?
