Being Redeemed For A Purpose

Gal 4:5

“Redeem them that were under the law”

 

We were all held under the Law and in bondage to sin and could not get free. A continual cycle that could not be broken no matter how much we tried. We were slaves to sin and Satan was our master. Then Jesus came and died on the cross and shed His own blood to purchase us back to God. He paid the full price for full Redemption. In this redemption process we are being purchased or bought back. The more we realize or come to understand that we are not our own but we have been bought with a price we will surrender ourselves to Gods Will and ultimate purpose. As babes in Christ we are saved and born again but we have yet to be baptized into the Body of Christ. Once we have been filled with the Holy Ghost we become children in the family of God baptized into the Body of Christ. But still as children not fully developed. It’s only as we grow in grace and understand truth through faith that we shall become the sons of God, which is the positioning of sons. The placing of sons. Fully mature and fully developed. To the fullness and stature of Christ.
