The Challenge

Job 22:2 Can a man be profitable unto God, as he that is wise may be profitable unto himself?

A. The profit to God is by His own power to create Praise, Honor and Glory unto His own Name through the believer.

Can a man be thus profitable to God? No, for such is the perfection of God that he cannot receive any benefit or advantage by men; what can be added to that which is infinite?

Can the light of a candle be profitable to the sun or the bucket of water to the ocean?

But God can glorify Himself in man.

2-4 Eliphaz is accusing Job of being proud.

5 Self deception and self confidence making us serve only our own purposes.

B. No pleasure to the Lord for man to seek His own perfection.
