The Lord is My Source

Ps 119:73—Thy hands have made me and fashioned me: give me understanding, that I may learn thy commandments.

 

  1. Every Believer must recognize and accept the Lord as their source
    1. Our source is revealed  through David’s prayer
      1. Our Creator Father, Son and Holy Ghost
      2. Foundation-(Word) 9,11,17,25,28,38,42-43,49-50,57-58,65,67,74
      3. Our Potter “Thy Hands made me and fashioned me”
      4. Our Teacher ”give me understanding, that I may learn”
      5. Our Law giver “that I may learn thy commandments.

 

  1. Every believer must receive and learn from the Lord
    1. Thy very open “Hands” of fellowship oh God that “made me and fashioned me, give me understanding, that I may learn thy commandments”

“Thy Hands”

Thy creation, born again, saved, delivered, made alive, made new, made whole

 

Thy “understanding”     119:34,73,125,144,169

“Understanding” that I may know you

“Understanding” that others would see you knowing me

  • By  “Thy hands have made me and fashioned me”
  • By answered prayer and You “give me understanding”
  • By Me learning” thy commandments”
  • By me understanding that the Lord is my source for living

 

We must as fellowshipping believers have this “understanding” that they may know you as their source, beginning with our example in all things no matter what.

 

“Thy commandments”

Every Believer, made and fashioned by God must desire more and more of the Lord

  • Made Me and Fashioned Me
  • Give Me
  • Learn Me

Every Believer must seek to be taught

Every Believer must seek to be given understanding

Every Believer must seek to pray and teach by example

 

“Give me understanding”—Perfect Understanding in all things

  • The Lord's great care in Creation.
  • The Lord's perfection in New Creation within us.
  • The Method of this Perfecting
    • Perfection through teaching
    • Perfection through understanding
    • Perfection through Learning
    • Perfection through prayer and daily living

 

Thy Open hands of fellowship have made, fashioned and given me understanding, That I may learn thy commandments and teach others through prayer and daily living.

 

(They that fear God will be glad when they see me; because I have hoped in thy word).
