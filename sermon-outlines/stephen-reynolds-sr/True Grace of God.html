1Pe 5:12 By Silvanus, a faithful brother unto you, as I suppose, I have written briefly, exhorting, and testifying that this is the true grace of God wherein ye stand.

 

Continuance and perseverance in the True doctrine of GRACE is the only ground and foundation of Christian strength: Now the sum of the apostles doctrine, is salvation freely given by God to those who repent, believer and continue in the word

John 8:31 ¶ Then said Jesus to those Jews which believed on him, If ye continue in my word, then are ye my disciples indeed;

32 And ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free.



We know that when a man preaches to a congregation, he is dealing with
a mixed audience.  He is speaking to those who are stubborn and
rebellious and also to those who are tender hearted and sincere toward
God wanting to please Him.  Among this number are children who have a
limited understanding of truth.

I believe when a man receives a Word from God and preaches it as the
Spirit of God directs, the Spirit of God is preaching that Word
through Him.

Do you think that that Word could fall like the gentle rain on the
tender, but ignorant heart, but that same Word preached to the same
congregation could fall on the hard and rebellious heart like the
hammer that breaks the rock in pieces?

 

YES   Mat. 13:3-9

Do you think that a spirit of darkness such as a spirit of fear could
intercept the Word preached through a man by the Holy Ghost and use it
to crush the spirit of a child, for instance, who is tender toward the
Lord, but ignorant and vulnerable?
