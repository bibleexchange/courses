The difference between preaching at a congregation and to a congregation

 

Preaching at someone is scolding, reprimanding or trying to convict them, which is the Work of the Holy Ghost or correct them which is the Pastor’s Job.

 

Preaching at: Give them a sermon

Preaching to: Send them a message of life, love, hope and victory.

 

Preaching to them is proving, painting and persuading them to repent, which is the Pastor’s responsibility as well.

 

Seeing sin is something we need to do but how we handle it will be through the Pastor.

 

I Know that a lot of people do not take everything to God in prayer and they make a matter,  their concern then they find themselves talking about it then effected by it.

 

We need to pray and keep a close communication with the Pastor or we are going to drive people away rather than help them.

 

Evangelists sometime get into areas where they shouldn’t be; by their motive, not necessarily their actions or words.

 

Conviction is by the Holy Ghost and the best testimony is the life you live and the motive you have to encourage by using the Word of God not just thoughts of the Word.
