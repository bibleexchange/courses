Spiritual Warfare

“Fight the good fight of faith”

 

1Timothy 6:12 Fight the good fight of faith, lay hold on eternal life, whereunto thou art also called, and hast professed a good profession before many witnesses.

 

“FIGHT” - “Fight the good fight” - “Fight the good fight of faith”

 

We must fight all our battles of faith in the realm of the Spirit.

We must remain alive in God through Jesus Christ God’s Son.

We must really lay hold on Eternal Life in His fullness to continue to fight the good fight of faith.

 

There is nothing worth fighting for outside Eternal Life.

 

Why fight so hard for the devil?

Why fight so hard to die?

Why fight a fight of unbelief?

Why fight alone?

Why fight to lose?
