  1. A Watchman

12 The watchman said, The morning cometh, and also the night: if ye will enquire, enquire ye: return, come.

  • Enquire
    • He describes the unquietness of the people of Dumah, who were night and day in fear of their enemies, and ever ran to and fro to enquire news.
    • Enquire of the Lord, ask counsel of God in the spirit of obedience and faith, he will, in the end, return an answer of peace

Ps 27:4 One thing have I desired of the LORD, that will I seek after; that I may dwell in the house of the LORD all the days of my life, to behold the beauty of the LORD, and to enquire in his temple.

  • Return
    • I see your danger is not past, and there will be occasion for farther enquiries.  Therefore return,
  • Come
    • Come to me the next morning, and so from morning to morning.
      • Every minister of God is a watchman.
      • Watchmen are often called to denounce heavy judgments.
      • Watchman are to be stedfast.
