v. Paul a servant

when you begn to humble yourself and exalt Jesus becoming a servant—dedicated wholly to his will he has completely bought and possessed this life. This the gospel for everyman.

 

v.6

Ro 1:7 To all that be in Rome, beloved of God, called to be saints: Grace to you and peace from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.

 

Prepare for this weekend alone. If you think too far off you will miss God.

 

As a bondservant. Have no personal right. Exist only for the master’s service. There are going to be thoughts in your mind.

 

Obedience is very import. The revelation of God coming to you of you who is and what he wants there will be thoughts that come to puff you up.

 

 

2Co 10:5

Casting down imaginations, and every high thing that exalteth itself against the knowledge of God, and bringing into captivity every thought to the obedience of Christ;

 

As the servant of Jesus Christ he is going to reveal to you everything of the Gospel. But not all at once.

 

Romans 1:17

For therein is the righteousness of God revealed from faith to faith: as it is written, The just shall live by faith.

 

Walking in step with Jesus. There are thoughts that will come into your life to exalt above the word of God. When you are trying your hardest, the devil will find a weakness.

 

Ro 1:1 ¶ Paul,

  1. a servant of Jesus Christ,
  2. called to be an apostle, separated unto the gospel of God,

 

separated completely unto what God will reveal to me.
