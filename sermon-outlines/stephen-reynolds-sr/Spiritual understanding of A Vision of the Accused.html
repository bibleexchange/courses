I. The Accused - Zech. 3:1

"And He shewed me Joshua the high priest

standing before the Angel of the Lord."

1. His Position -

(1.) "High Priest"

(2.) "Standing before the Lord"

2. His Purpose -

(1.) To intercede for the people -

(2.) To offer sacrifices to God -

3. His Condition -

(1.) "A brand plucked out of the fire" vs. 2

  • He knows what is like to be a cast away by man, circumstances and sin
  • He knows what it is like to be delivered from sin

 

(2.) "Clothed with filthy garments" vs. 3

Symbolic of his moral character.

Scorched, got burnt, burned by sin, by wrong choices – burning in the flames, but "Plucked"

2Co 1:10 Who delivered us from so great a death, and doth deliver: in whom we trust that he will yet deliver us;

 

II. The Accuser - vs. 1

"And Satan standing at his right hand to resist

him."

1. Satan is the Accuser - Satan means Adversary.

2. "Stood at Joshua's right hand"

The Accuser in ancient courts stood at the right hand.

3. To resist him.  Resist his ministry.

4. To accuse him.  Disloyalty - inconsistency - mixture of motive.

5. He discovers their weaknesses -

6. He attacks these weaknesses -

 

When we serve God faithfully the accuser suggests that we do it for a selfish motive.

"Doth Job fear God for nought?"

Job 1:9 Then Satan answered the LORD, and said, Doth Job fear God for nought?

Job was accused (He fears you not for your own sake, but for the blessing that he received from you)

 

God Himself is "the reward" of His people

Ge 15:1 After these things the word of the LORD came unto Abram in a vision, saying, Fear not, Abram: I am thy shield, and thy exceeding great reward.

 

Mal 1:10 Who is there even among you that would shut the doors for nought? neither do ye kindle fire on mine altar for nought. I have no pleasure in you, saith the LORD of hosts, neither will I accept an offering at your hand.

(There was not one sincere, honest priest among them. They were selfish and worldly; and so basely so, that not one of them would even kindle a fire on the hearth of the altar unless paid for it)   Kindle the fire on the altar with fervent Prayer- like Job, Elijah, Daniel,John the Baptist, JESUS, Peter, James , John, Paul,  Epaphras - (Col. 4:12)

III. The Advocate - vs. 2

"And the LORD said unto Satan"

"Jehovah" ---  The Covenant keeping God.

1. As Advocate He rebukes the devil -

vs. 2 "The Lord rebuke thee, O Satan"

2. As Advocate He vindicates him.

vs. 2  "Is not this a brand plucked out of the fire?"

3. As Advocate He intercedes for him.

 

vs. 4 "Take away the filthy garments from him."

 

"Behold, I have caused thine iniquity to pass from thee."

 

4. As Advocate He redeemed him.

 

"I will clothe thee with change of raiment."
