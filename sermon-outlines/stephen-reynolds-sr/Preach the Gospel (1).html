Sinister

Menacing, ominous, threatening, evil, disturbing, creepy, baleful, malevolent

(Malicious, spiteful, wicked, nasty, mean)

Benevolent

Kind, caring, compassionate, generous, giving, benign, gentle, munificent

(Generous, bountiful, openhanded, freehanded, charitable, unstinting, unsparing, magnanimous)
