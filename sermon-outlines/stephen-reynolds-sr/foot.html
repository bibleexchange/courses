Making Every Step Sure in God—(God’s Divine Help for every Step of faith)—

Psalm 17:5—Hold up my goings in thy paths, that my footsteps slip not. {slip...: Heb. be not moved}

  1. We must walk in God's paths
    1. God’s path’s are Revealed
      1. The path of Deliverance
      2. The path of Life
      3. The path of the Just
      4. The path of Wisdom
      5. The path of Righteousness
      6. The path of Holiness
      7. The path of Judgment
    2. God’s paths are revealed from faith to faith.

Rom. 1:17—For therein is the righteousness of God revealed from faith to faith: as it is written, The just shall live by faith.                                                                                                                                                                                                                     18—For the wrath of God is revealed from heaven against all ungodliness and unrighteousness of men, who hold the truth in unrighteousness;

  1. We must spend our life in obedience to God’s Word
    1. God’s Word is Revealed
    2. God’s Word is His Will.
    3. God’s Word is Truth
    4. God’s Word is the Old Paths              
      1. Walk in the Old Paths
