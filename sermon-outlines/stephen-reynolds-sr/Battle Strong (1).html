  • Time to battle
  • Time to face the Hottest Battle

 

  1. Facing the hottest battle in life and you retire from them and leave them to be smitten and die.

 

    1. Careless—unwise in battle       NEED       Be Wise in battle but Be Wiser in Battle

 

    1. Heartless—weak hearted         NEED       Be strong in battle but be stronger in battle.

 

    1. Selfish—self-interested            NEED    Be in Battle but be more into the battle yourself.

 

      1. Be in battle for His name sake not your own.
      2. Be in battle for His name sake while yours is not known.
      3. Be in battle for His name sake but not alone.

 

  • It is not wise to be in battle alone
  • It is not safe to be in battle alone
  • It is not battle strong to be in battle alone or to be alone without battle.
