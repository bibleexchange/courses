July 1, 2007, Sunday PM

 

TITLE: Running with the Message of God’s Mercy

TEXT: Zechariah 2:

 

God give us a clear vision of the mercy of God that we may run with this message.

 

John 8:32, 36 If you want mercy forsake your sin

 

Sin=even those things in our spirit where we neglect what we ought to be doing. There are no boundaries to God’s grace, which abounds more and more and more.

 

The devil likes to put stipulations saying you can only have this much or that much. God has set a righteous judgment that we might have unlimited and the devil comes by trying to pull up the stakes and landmarks. Don’t allow the devil to pull up your stake but drive it in deeper. What God has said is the secret to us making it. Fasten to the Word of God and not to how we feel for how we feel can change and even get worse. It is good to feel good but we need to have something that if all our feelings turn to rottenness we can yet receive of the mercy of God. God is merciful.

 

God is not ridiculing us and picking on us to put us down, but come to purify and cleanse us.

 

Mercy doesn’t work without truth.
