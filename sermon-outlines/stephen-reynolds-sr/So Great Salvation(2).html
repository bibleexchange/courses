  1. The Meaning of “So Great Salvation”

 

  1. Must be experienced before it can be greatly expressed.

 

    1. Our experience must agree with the Word that Salvation is so Great.

 

  • Why is salvation so great? It’s magnitude is due to two things:

 

  • The GREATNESS of the One who proclaimed it, for He is ETERNAL!
  • The great fact that God in the person of His Son suffered for our sins!

 

    • He took place in the penalty of our sin that we might take place, in Righteousness

 

      • Full Salvation is only for the believer that is not neglectful.

 

  • The Holy Ghost is sent to take the things of Christ and show the believer.
  • The Overcoming believer is granted rights to the Throne in Christ.

 

      1. Great in its Foundation

 

          1. Jesus Christ is the Chief Cornerstone.
            1. Jesus Christ is the only Saviour.
            2. Jesus Christ is Lord, to the glory of God the Father.

 

                1. The Lord will neglect us if we neglect Him.
                2. The Lord will not neglect us if we don’t neglect Him.
          1.   Keep drawing nigh to Christ.
            1. We need Christ in every part of our life.
              1.  Neglect Him and there is no escape.
  • The slightest neglect is always noticed and must be corrected.
          1.   Keep the knee bowed in prayer, fellowship and submission.
          2.   Keep confessing through the Power of the Holy Ghost to        God, that Jesus Christ is Lord.
