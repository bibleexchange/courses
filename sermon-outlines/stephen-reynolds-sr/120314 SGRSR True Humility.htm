Psalm 115

God is Glorified When Man is unselfishly dependent on Him

Man must depend on God for his all

                                            Man must stay dependent on Him

 

God is calling you. Even though we feel we don’t deserve it. We cannot defeat the plan of God, but defeating ourselves. We have a responsibility to know the truth and walk in the truth.

 

The door of God’s grace is wide open. By grace through faith, everyone has access (Romans 5:2)

a total obedient life total submitted life

 

God wants to give of his grace, wisdom, glory more than we want to receive.

  1Pe 1:3 ¶ Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, which according to his abundant mercy hath begotten us again unto a lively hope by the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead,
