April 8, 2007

Rev. Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

Deliverance Center – Sunday PM

 

TITLE: The Word of Life

TEXT: Philippians 2:13-16

 

The anointing is for me. Not just for preaching, but to help me live an overcoming life.

There are simple things that give you direction to help people. God gives you the right words and right understanding to reach people so that they sense you are preaching to them and not at them.

The Word of Life. The Bible must be looked at only as words upon a page. The letter killeth, but the spirit giveth life.

The word makes us to shine brighter and brighter, it is a living Word.

Let this Word be alive in our soul.

 

—PRAYER—

 

The Word of God is more powerful than death and the devil.

It is as the resurrection took place, something about Jesus was more than a man living, more than just the physical, giving good counsel, HE IS THE WORD OF LIFE.

I John 1:1 How do you I know He is real? I have HANDLED THE WORD OF LIFE.

 

It is our responsibility to give God the glory and the praise.
