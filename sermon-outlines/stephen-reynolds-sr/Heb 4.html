Heb 4:12 For the word of God is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any twoedged sword, piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit, and of the joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart.

 

piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit,

 

  1. Let us make sure of our salvation
  2. Let us avail ourselves of all God has for us in Christ.

 

It is imperative that we make sure of our salvation in our soul and in our spirit, lest we be found to be mere professors of Christianity.

 

A man could profess Christianity in his soul and his spirit be dead

A man of faith in Christ who possesses Christ in his soul his spirit is made alive.

 

1Co 15:22 For as in Adam all die, even so in Christ shall all be made alive.

 

We must take to heart the example of the Israelites who, although saved and separated from Egypt but never entered into Canaan and died in the wilderness and thus missed God's rest.

 

God expects His people to avail themselves of all He has for them in Christ.

 

How can a person be sure of his salvation?

 

By exposing his heart to the piercing sword of God's Word, for sooner or later, that sword will find him out if he is a mere pretender.
