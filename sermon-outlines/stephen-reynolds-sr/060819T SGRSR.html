TITLE: BUT NOAH

TEXT: Genesis 6:1-8

 

Man in general in all the world were not pleasing to God. God judges man that refuses His plan. He is faithful giving us His promises. We must be faithful to seek after them and know them.

As it was in the days of Noah so shall it be in the days of the comingn of the Son of man. NOAH found grace in the eyes of the Lord.

God is longsuffering and gives to us His Word. And after a long time of waiting patiently 120 years of Noah’s preaching. The world rejected and laughed at it. After such a long time of pleading for them, men turned from God’s plan and provision and God brought judgment to them. As God turned and brough judgment, God will judge this generation. BUT NOAH FOUND GRACE IN THE EYES OF THE LORD.

2 Peter 1:1-3

The knowledge of God’s Word is more important than anything in the world. Wickedness is on the increase.
