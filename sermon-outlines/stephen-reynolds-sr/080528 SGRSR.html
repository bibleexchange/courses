TITLE: Having Compassion on Lost Souls

TEXT: Psalm 69:16

 

Having the heart of God and reaching out to lost souls.

 

Hear me, O LORD; for thy lovingkindness is good: turn unto me according to the multitude of thy tender mercies.

 

Intense compassion for the lost. Not a practice or form. If you don’t care like you should they will never come to birth. The care of a mother over the unborn child in her womb.

 

v. 32 The humble shall see this, and be glad: and your heart shall live that seek God.
