A Spirit of Wisdom

A heart of Wisdom

 

Christ’s conduct as well as His preaching is highly edifying because everything He did and said was wise.

His manner of teaching made everything he spoke interesting, impressive with authority.

He had many prejudices to remove, and he did what was necessary to take them out of the way.

 

There is as much to be observed in the manner of speaking the truth, as in the truth itself, in order to make it effectual to the salvation of those who hear it.

Eph 4:15 But speaking the truth in love, may grow up into him in all things, which is the head, even Christ:

Pr 8:7 For my mouth shall speak truth; and wickedness is an abomination to my lips.

Ac 26:25 But he said, I am not mad, most noble Festus; but speak forth the words of truth and soberness.

1Ti 2:7 Whereunto I am ordained a preacher, and an apostle, (I speak the truth in Christ, and lie not;) a teacher of the Gentiles in faith and verity.

 

A harsh, method of preaching the promises of the Gospel, and a smiling manner of preaching the terrors of the Lord, are equally unacceptable.

 

Some preachers are always severe and overbearing: others are always mild and clever: neither of these can do God's work; and it would take severe and mild and leave off the overbearing and cleverness to make a good PREACHER.

Col 4:2 Continue in prayer, and watch in the same with thanksgiving;

I.            Continue in prayer

a.  Prayer should be a joyful habit

b.  We are to be prayerful in character  as well as on our knees

II.          Watch in the same with thanksgiving

a.  Be thankful for a desire to pray

b.  Be thankful for the privilege to pray beyond the rent veil

c.   We must pray  ---  Guard your life through Prayer

We must fight drowsiness, distractions, and discouragement when we pray.

The wife is to submit properly to her husband,

The husband is to love his wife as Christ loved the church,

The young person is to submit to his parents,

Parents are to keep from discouraging their children,

Men are to serve their human masters as rendering service to the Lord Christ,

Masters are to care for the well being of their employees as those accountable to God, then they must pray.
