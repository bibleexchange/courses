TITLE: The Way of True Greatness

TEXT: Isaiah 54:16-17

 

The sword of the Spirit quickens and makes alive that which has been destroyed by sin.

“thou shalt condemn that tongue that riseth up against you.” UNDERSTAND there is an inheritance, a heritage, and a place to receive from God by FAITH. Faith working in our heart will produce Christ’s righteousness in us.

 

Romans 1:1 RECOGNIZE THAT YOU ARE A SERVANT OF THE LORD.
