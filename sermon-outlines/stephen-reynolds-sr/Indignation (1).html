  • Why didst thou give me a being, when thou didst foresee I should be exposed to such incredible hardships?

 

If you are really praying to pray through the chastening will not only be accepted but understood and  cause you to overcome.

 

  1. That is, diversity of diseases and afflictions in great abundance; showing that God has infinite means to try the heart of man.              I Thess. 2:4
  2. I am attacked by successive troops; one company being wearied, another attacks, so that I am harassed by continual warfare.      II Cor. 4:3-10
  3. Without constant, strong, and loving confidence in God, trials may become so great, that even good men may wish to die, or find fault with God for bringing them into being, and continuing them amidst their accumulated sufferings.  But let them in patience wait upon God, and they will find that He is very pitiful and of tender mercy; that not a trial has been suffered that has not aided in working out for them an exceeding and eternal weight of glory.                           II Corinthians 4:17-18               I Cor. 4:20-21; 11:32
