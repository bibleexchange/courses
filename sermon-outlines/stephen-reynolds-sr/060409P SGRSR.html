Rev. Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

April 9, 2006

Sunday Evening

Title:

Text: Haggai 2:4

 

There is a lot of opposition to the work of God. In Haggai the temple work had been stalled for 16 years. There is a job to be done. There is a ministry that is needed to reach out to the lost, dying, distressed, and oppressed. Do what I know I can do.

IF you are faithful to God there will be raised up treasures in heaven. Let God’s name be exalted in all we do and say. LOVE HIM. Our faith must be fruit of our love for God. I Corinthians 13:2

When He shakes everything that can be shaken will be shaken.

 

How is it that ye are without faith?
