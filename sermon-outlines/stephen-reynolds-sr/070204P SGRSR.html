Deliverance Center – Sunday PM

Stephen G. Reynolds, Sr.

February 4, 2007

TITLE:

TEXT: Colossians 1:23,24

 

INTRODUCTION

Rejoice in sufferings.

Treading the winepress alone.

I encourage myself in the Lord. --David

I alone encourage my disciples. –Jesus

I want to be like Jesus. The flesh has to do in order to be like Jesus. You have to be humble. Put aside wishes, dreams, desires, goals, and opinions. Affection for this world must die.

Psalm 89:15 There is a sound of rejoicing that the Lord has called us to know and not just to hear. There is a sound of rejoicing that involves suffering. If you look to be rich and successful in this world you will never be like Jesus, if that is what you spend your life seeking to. The only place for riches when It is God’s blessing.

Blessed is the people that know the joyful sound. REJOICING IN SUFFERING. KNOWING THE JOYFUL SOUND> We want to be able to be the vessel He can use to make a difference in this city. This walk of faith is going to be with suffering. To be like Jesus we will suffer the same causes he suffered.

To be like Jesus we will have to learn to tread the winepress alone. Isa. 63:3

There is victory in knowing that God knows what I can handle.
