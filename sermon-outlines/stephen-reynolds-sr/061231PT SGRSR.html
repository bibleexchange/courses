TITLE: God’s Choice Men

TEXT:  Acts 15:7

 

We need men that have been chosen by the Lord, for the jobs that need to be done. Though they were many they were in one accord. V.22.

Men worthy of double honor that would do what they were sent to do. Hazarding even their lives. Giving themselves for the gospel’s sake. They gave themselves completely. Seeking not their own way or their own advantage. But hazarding or lives for the gospel’s sake.
