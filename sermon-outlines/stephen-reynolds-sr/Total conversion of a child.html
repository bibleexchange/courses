 5 And whoso shall receive one such little child in my name receiveth me.

"Make Me A Child, Like Son"

Last night my little boy

Confessed to me Some childish wrong; And kneeling at my knee

He prayed with tears—

"Dear God, make me a man like daddy—Wise and strong,

I know You can."

Then while he slept I knelt beside his bed,

Confessed my sins, And prayed with low-bowed head,

"O God, make me like a child, Like my son here—Pure and gladness,

Trusting Thee with faith sincere."
