The Grace of God Teaches

Titus 2:11 For the grace of God that bringeth salvation hath appeared to all men,                                                                       12 Teaching us that, denying ungodliness and worldly lusts, we should live soberly, righteously, and godly, in this present world;                                                                                                                                                                                                                13 Looking for that blessed hope, and the glorious appearing of the great God and our Saviour Jesus Christ;                                                                                                                                               14 Who gave himself for us, that he might redeem us from all iniquity, and purify unto himself a peculiar people, zealous of good works.

  1. The Grace of God brings Salvation to all men that believe.
    1. Mr 1:15 And saying, The time is fulfilled, and the kingdom of God is at hand: repent ye, and believe the gospel.
    2. Ac 15:11 But we believe that through the grace of the Lord Jesus Christ we shall be saved, even as they.
    3.  Ac 16:31 And they said, Believe on the Lord Jesus Christ, and thou shalt be saved, and thy house.
    4. 1Co 1:21 For after that in the wisdom of God the world by wisdom knew not God, it pleased God by the foolishness of preaching to save them that believe.
    5. 1Ti 1:16 Howbeit for this cause I obtained mercy, that in me first Jesus Christ might shew forth all longsuffering, for a pattern to them which should hereafter believe on him to life everlasting.
  2. The Grace of God teaches all men that are saved, the full benefits of Redemption.
    1. The Grace of God teaches us to deny ungodliness.
    2. The Grace of God teaches us to deny worldly lusts.
    3. The Grace of God teaches us to live Soberly.
    4. The Grace of God teaches us to live Righteously.
    5. The Grace of God teaches us to live Godly.
    6. The Grace of God teaches us to oppose all the corruption that is in this present world.
  3. The Grace of God Purifies by chastening.
    1. 1Co 11:32 But when we are judged, we are chastened of the Lord, that we should not be condemned with the world.
    2. 2Co 6:9 As unknown, and yet well known; as dying, and, behold, we live; as chastened, and not killed;
    3. 2Ti 2:25 In meekness instructing those that oppose themselves; if God peradventure will give them repentance to the acknowledging of the truth;
    4. Heb 12:6 For whom the Lord loveth he chasteneth, and scourgeth every son whom he receiveth.

 7 If ye endure chastening, God dealeth with you as with sons; for what son is he whom the father chasteneth not?  

    1.  Re 3:19 As many as I love, I rebuke and chasten: be zealous therefore, and repent.
