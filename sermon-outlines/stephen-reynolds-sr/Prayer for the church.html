Pr 8:30 Then I was by him, as one brought up with him: and I was daily his delight, rejoicing always before him;

 

This Divine wisdom, delighted in the creation of man,

so it continues to delight in his instruction.

He offers lessons of instruction continually,

He uses every means and opportunity to call men from folly and vice to sound knowledge, holiness, and happiness.

It is to man that God especially gives wisdom; and he has it in the form of reason beyond all other creatures; therefore it is said,

Pr 8:31 Rejoicing in the habitable part of his earth; and my delights were with the sons of men.

They alone are capable of intelligence, and reason.
