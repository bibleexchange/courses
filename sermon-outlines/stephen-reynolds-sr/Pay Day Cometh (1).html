Vow

 

05088.  rdn  neder,  neh'-der

A voluntary promise (to God); To perform some service or do something pleasing to Him, in return for some hoped for benefits or abstain from certain things.

 

In the O.T. vows were never regarded as a religious duty, but once they were made they were considered sacred and binding.
