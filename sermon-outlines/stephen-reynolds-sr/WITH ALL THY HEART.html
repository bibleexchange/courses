SEEK THE LORD

SEEKING—in search of, in quest of, on the lookout for MORE.

LOOKING—

 

“WITH ALL THY HEART”…

 

Deut 4:29 But if from thence thou shalt seek the LORD thy God, thou shalt find him, if thou seek him with all thy heart and with all thy soul.

 

To seek God, means to look to Him diligently, earnestly and sincerely, striving to live and be like Him.

 

This brings us to where we really know Him.

 

Jesus said to know Him is to have eternal life.

 

We see that the pursuit of God is a lifelong quest.

 

It is a major change in the life of the believer to have his eyes fully on the Lord, because the only way for that to be possible is to have your whole heart and your whole soul seeking the Lord.

  1. Seek the Lord thy God
  1. Seek Him that you may find your whole heart and soul seeking Him.
  2. Seek Him that you may turn back to Him.


“if from thence” “seek the LORD thy God”

 

  1. From right where you are at.
  2. From any point of view.
  3. From any place of trouble.
    1. God often uses trouble to get our attention.
    2. You can’t change your heart but God can as you seek Him.

 

  1. Seeking the Lord is a very simple process that many people complicate by acting on how they feel instead of giving how they feel to Jesus.

 

  1.  Give Him how you feel and watch what he does with it.
  2. The Lord can do better with you than you can do with yourself.
  3. The Lord will show Himself to you and through you as He has your whole heart and soul.

 

This world has yet to see what God would do with a man fully yielded to the Lord.
