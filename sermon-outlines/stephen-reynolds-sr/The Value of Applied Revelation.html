Its one thing to receive Revelation and another thiong to apply Revelation

Jer 15:16 Thy words were found, and I did eat them;

 

We must Receive – we must apply – applying the truth to our lives is absolutely necessary.

The Value is when the truth is applied.

A slow consistent strong process must take place.

  1. Truth must be sought after
  2. Truth is nourishment for your soul
  3. Truth is creates an appetite for that which is divine, supernatural
  4. Truth produces an awakening

Eph 5:14 Wherefore he saith, Awake thou that sleepest, and arise from the dead, and Christ shall give thee light. {he: or, it}
