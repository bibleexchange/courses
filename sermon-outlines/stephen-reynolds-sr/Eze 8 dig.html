Eze 8:8 Then said he unto me, Son of man, dig now in the wall: and when I had digged in the wall, behold a door.

  1. A discovery  of a door that led to idolatry (Egyptian idolatries) into which the covenant-people had backslid, practicing them in secret places where they shrank from the light of day
    1. A door-A private door, by which the priests entered into the chamber of their imagery,                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          to perform idolatrous worship to their images.                                                                                                                                                        Eze 8:7 ¶ And he brought me to the door of the court; and when I looked, behold a hole in the wall.
  1. Dig
  2. behold a hole in the wall---
    1. for vision
    2. for discerning
    3. for understanding
    4. not for entering but gazing,  many gaze and have no intentions of entering
    5. The hypocrites inside had no intentions of others finding out
  3. God wants His prophet to more than gaze, He wants him to enter
    1. A hole, dig, behold a door
    2. Make the hole bigger
  4. behold the wicked abominations that they do here
    1. Eze 8:10 So I went in and saw; and behold every form of creeping things, and abominable beasts, and all the idols of the house of Israel, pourtrayed upon the wall round about.
    2. Eze 8:12 Then said he unto me, Son of man, hast thou seen what the ancients of the house of Israel do in the dark, every man in the chambers of his imagery? for they say, The LORD seeth us not; the LORD hath forsaken the earth.
      1. Had they chambers of imagery, and shall not we have chambers of devotion?
      2. They think themselves out of God's care
    3. More and greater abominations discovered to the prophet. He thought that what he had seen was bad enough and yet
      1. Eze 8:13 ¶ He said also unto me, Turn thee yet again, and thou shalt see greater abominations that they do.
      2. Eze 8:14 Then he brought me to the door of the gate of the LORD'S house which was toward the north; and, behold, there sat women weeping for Tammuz.
    4. Even greater abominations
      1. Eze 8:15 Then said he unto me, Hast thou seen this, O son of man? turn thee yet again, and thou shalt see greater abominations than these.
      2. Eze 8:16 And he brought me into the inner court of the LORD'S house, and, behold, at the door of the temple of the LORD, between the porch and the altar, were about five and twenty men, with their backs toward the temple of the LORD, and their faces toward the east; and they worshipped the sun toward the east.

      1. A front
      2. A fortress astronghold
      3. A hole sign of weakness
        1. Of pride
        2. Of anger
        3. Of  lust
        4. Of feelings
        5. Of thoughts
        6. Of my own stubborn will
        7. of misrepresentation
        8. of deception

    1. “behold a door”
      1. The door of opportunity is ruined by sin, waiting

  3. They were practicing their sin in secret

        3. Many hide from right judgment
        4. It is work to dig, to reveal and expose all the secret things within the heart
