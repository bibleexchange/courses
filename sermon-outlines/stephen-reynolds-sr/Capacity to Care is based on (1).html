This man; Coniah had children, 1Ch 3:17-18, one named Mt 1:12,

 Childless; in the sense, "No man of his seed shall prosper."  None of his seed should occupy the throne of David in Jerusalem.  Zedekiah, who reigned after him, was his brother.  The prophet here refers to the earthly throne of David which began with righteous rulers, and gained strength and glory by obedience to God's laws; it was destroyed by wicked and corrupt rulers, leading a wicked and corrupt people in iniquity-rebellion against God.  Herein is it set forth as an example to all nations.
