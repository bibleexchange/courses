THE KING’S HIGHWAY

 

And an highway shall be there, and a way, and it shall be called The way of holiness; the unclean shall not pass over it; but it shall be for those: the wayfaring men, though fools, shall not err therein.  (Isa 35:8)

 

No lion shall be there, nor any ravenous beast shall go up thereon, it shall not be found there; but the redeemed shall walk there:  (Isa 35:9)

 

Remove not the ancient landmark, which thy fathers have set.  (Pro 22:28)

 

 

God has provided the HIGHWAY for man.  Man must stay on the highway to reach heaven.  Jesus is the only way.

 

I. A HOLY WAY

 

A. The unclean SHALL NOT pass over it

 

1. This is God’s Word

2. Not for the unsaved

 

B. A separated way

 

Follow peace with all men, and holiness, without which no man shall see the Lord:  (Heb 12:14)

 

1. God is Holy

2. Separate from the world

 

C. A straight way

 

D. Jesus makes us Holy

 

1. Washed in the blood of the lamb

2. Justified by faith

3. Jesus is the way

 

 

 

II. A PROTECTED WAY

 

A. No lions on the way

 

B. No ravenous beast

1. Satan is the devourer

2. Scavengers eat the dead and wounded

 

a. A way of life

b. The life of Jesus

 

C. Under the Blood of Jesus

 

III. A VICTORIOUS WAY

 

A. God’s way brings victory

 

B. Leads unto life

 

C. More than conquerors

 

D. A powerful way
