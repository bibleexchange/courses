ONE MORE NIGHT WITH THE FROGS

 

Ex. 8:1-10

 

Moses asked Pharaoh, when do you want the frogs to go?  Pharaoh answered, tomorrow.  Why did he want to spend one more night with the frogs?  Many people tarry and wait in the plague of sin instead of receiving

immediate deliverance.
