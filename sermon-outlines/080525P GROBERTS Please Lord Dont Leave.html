TITLE: Please, Lord, Don’t Leave

TEXT: Ezekiel 9:1-4

 

We are in very serious times. I really wonder how close we are to serious trouble.

Ezekiel 9 gives us the product of backsliding churches. This is a vision showing the very problem and the very help for the righteous.
