Title: The Promise of God’s Presence

Text: Joshua 1:6-9

 

We take too many things for granted, yet God is so faithful to his people. “I will not fail you.” But God gives us a condition, “You must OBEY Me.” We must not be just hearers but doers of the Word.

 

Authority is placed by God to keep us in a protected place. You are not too safe without counsel. There is a danger in no submission to God-given authority.

 

We should not be neglectful of God’s great salvation. The power of God is not seen today in our lives as it should be seen!

 

A man that really loves, will always tell the truth! A true shephered loves his sheep.

 

We are living in a Laodicean church age where many are lethargic in their walk with God. Without the presence of God and His power, the Church will cease to exist.

 

The confidence Joshua needed was in the Word that God gave to Joshua! When God speaks a Word, He can bring it to pass.

 

James 4:17 Know to do good and do it not ⇒ the sin plaguing many of us is not the sin we ARE doing, but is the sin of what we are NOT doing. We are too comfortable in where we are at. We need a revival and a move of God.

 

Daniel 11:32 And such as do wickedly against the covenant shall he corrupt by flatteries: but the people that do know their God shall be strong, and do exploits.
