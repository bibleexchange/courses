Everywhere He Went He Was Doing Good

VERSE 1

Well I've been reading, and I've been feeding, upon the precious Word of God.

Marvelous story, so full of glory; It is the path that Jesus trod.

He was man's deliverer, and sin forgiver, every problem He understood.

Everywhere He went, my Lord was doing good…doing good.

 

CHORUS

Everywhere He went…He was doing good.

Gave beatitudes, helped the multitudes, everywhere He could.

When the cripples saw Him, they started walking; the dumb were talking like they should.

Everywhere He went, my Lord was doing good…doing good.

 

VERSE 2

Jesus opened blind eyes; He heard the lame cry; He raised the dead and calmed the sea.

He's a mighty helper; He cleansed the leper, and He set the captive free.

Though the world denied Him, and crucified Him, He abides with us today.

And His mighty power is just the same today.
