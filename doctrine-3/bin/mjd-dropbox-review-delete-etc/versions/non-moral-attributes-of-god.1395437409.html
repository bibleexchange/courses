Explanation of "Non-Moral Attributes"SWAHILI

AttributesSWAHILI

The qualities that belong to the substance and constitute a description of it. A description of the nature or qualities of a personSWAHILI

"For example, God says of Himself, 'I am holy'; therefore we can say, God is holy. Holiness, then is an attribute of God, because holiness is a quality that we may attribute or apply to Him. -- Doctrine Book "SWAHILI

Non-moralSWAHILI

Non-moral attributes are those truths of the Divine essence that do not involve moral qualities. It is an inherent quality without regard to right or wrong. Non-moral is not the same as "un-moral" or "immoral".SWAHILI
