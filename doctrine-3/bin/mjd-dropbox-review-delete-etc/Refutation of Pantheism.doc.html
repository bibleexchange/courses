Why do we seek to refute pantheism?   It is necessary because of the profound religious significance associated with pantheism.

 

We reject all pantheistic theories because…

 

They are ___________________________ —everything exists and acts of necessity.

 

We have a consciousness that we are  ____________  __________________  

________________  and that we are accountable for our conduct.

Lk. 23:39-41; Rev. 20:12,13; Rom. 14:11,12; 1 Cor. 3:12-15

 

 

AND, nothing shall be hidden or kept back in that day of terrible judgment.—

Ec. 3:16-18; 12:14; Mt. 12:35-37; Mk. 4:22; Lk. 12:2,3; 1 Tim. 5:24,25; Heb.

10:31; Rev. 6:15-17

 

 

 

2.  They destroy the ___________________________________________.

 

“The evil of materialism [or, pantheism] is seen in the fact that it destroys the foundation of morality.  For if man be only a machine, he is not responsible for his actions.  Consequently we cannot call the hero noble and the villain bad, for they cannot help acting as they do.”—Myer Pearlman, Knowing the Doctrines of the Bible, 56.  (Brackets and clarification added by instructor.)

 

If all things are  ______________,  then error and sin are also  _____________!

Then there is _______________________ by which to distinguish right and wrong.

 

Then _________________________ is sinful.

(supernatural) union of the human and the divine.  When  _____________________

between persons who retain their distinct individualities.  True religion is  ________

_________________  and service offered by  ______________________ to another
