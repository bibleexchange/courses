Study Questions

 

 

1. What is essence?

2. What is an attribute?

3. What is the difference between the essence and the attributes of God?

4. What are anthropomorphic representations?

5. Give John 4:24 verbatim.

6. What does the Scripture mean when it says:  “God created man in His

Own image,” (Genesis 1:27)?

7. Can the essence of God be three persons and one person at the same time?

8. Explain the infinitude of God.

9. Give Acts 17:28 verbatim.

10. What is meant by the expression necessary existence?

11. Does God eat and sleep?

12. Who said, “[God] is the first Cause, Himself uncaused.”?

13. What did he mean?

14. What is meant by the non-moral attributes of God?

15-18. List the non-moral attributes of God and briefly (2-3 sentences) explain each in

your own words.

19. Why doesn’t God change?

20. How might one explain the scriptures that say God does not repent with those

that represent Him as repenting?

EXTRA CREDIT: How might one reconcile Psalm 78:41 and God’s unlimited/limitless power?  (75-100 words)
