THE HOLY SPIRIT I. THE PERSON OF THE HOLY SPIRIT A. The Holy Spirit is referred to as third person of the Trinity. 1. The Holy Spirit is not third in power or position. 2. The Holy Spirit is equal with The Father and The Son. B. The Holy Spirit possesses the attributes of personality. 1. A mind “And he that searcheth the hearts knoweth what is the mind of the Spirit, because he maketh intercession for the saints according to the will of God.” – Rom. 8:27 2. A will “But all these worketh that one and the selfsame Spirit, dividing to every man severally as he will.” – I Cor. 12:11 3. Feeling “And grieve not the Holy Spirit of God, whereby ye are sealed unto the day of redemption.” – Eph. 4:30 C. The Holy Spirit has personal activities ascribed to him. 1. He reveals “For the prophecy came not in old time by the will of man: but holy men of God spake as they were moved by the Holy Ghost.” – II Peter 1:21 2. He teaches “But the Comforter, which is the Holy Ghost, whom the Father will send in my name, he shall teach you all things, and bring all things to your remembrance, whatsoever I have said unto you.” - John 14:26 3. He bears witness “The Spirit itself beareth witness with our spirit, that we are the children of God:” – Rom. 8:16 4. He intercedes “Likewise the Spirit also helpeth our infirmities: for we know not what we should pray for as we ought: but the Spirit itself maketh intercession for us with groanings which cannot be uttered.” – Rom. 8:26 5. He speaks “He that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith unto the churches; To him that overcometh will I give to eat of the tree of life, which is in the midst of the paradise of God.” – Rev. 2:7 6. He commands “Now when they had gone throughout Phrygia and the region of Galatia, and were forbidden of the Holy Ghost to preach the word in Asia,” Acts 16:6 7. He testifies “But when the Comforter is come, whom I will send unto you from the Father, even the Spirit of truth, which proceedeth from the Father, he shall testify of me:” – John 15:26 II. THE MINISTRY OF THE HOLY SPIRIT A. The Holy Spirit will always exalt Jesus. Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide you into all truth: for he shall not speak of himself; but whatsoever he shall hear, that shall he speak: and he will shew you things to come. He shall glorify me: for he shall receive of mine, and shall shew it unto you. - John 16:13-14 1. He shall not speak of Himself. The Holy Spirit does not exalt Himself. He will always exalt Jesus. The error of the false charismatic churches is their exaltation of the spirit. The spirit that they operate in is not the true Holy Spirit. – John 15:26 2. He will not glorify man. The Holy Spirit does not exalt man. The Holy Spirit will use a man to glorify Jesus. B. The Holy Spirit will show you things to come. 1. Through prophecy. 2. The Holy Spirit will reveal future events. C. The Holy Spirit will give power to minister. – Acts 1:8 1. He will anoint a man to preach the Gospel. For our gospel came not unto you in word only, but also in power, and in the Holy Ghost, and in much assurance; as ye know what manner of men we were among you for your sake. - I Thessalonians 1:5 And my speech and my preaching was not with enticing words of man's wisdom, but in demonstration of the Spirit and of power: - I Corinthians 2:4 2. He will minister to the congregation during the preaching. D. The Holy Spirit will lead men to the truth. 1. He will help you to understand the Word. 2. He will draw men to the truth. E. The Holy Spirit is the comforter. But the Comforter, which is the Holy Ghost, whom the Father will send in my name, he shall teach you all things, and bring all things to your remembrance, whatsoever I have said unto you. - John 14:26 F. The Holy Spirit will help you to pray. 1. Praying in the Holy Ghost But ye, beloved, building up yourselves on your most holy faith, praying in the Holy Ghost, - Jude 1:20 2. Praying through the saints. – Rom. 8:36-27 G. The Holy Spirit will lead you into the will of God. – Rom. 8:14, Mt. 4:1 1. He will give you direction for ministry. – Acts 8:29, Acts 10:19-20 2. He will keep you from doing your own will. – Acts 16:6-7 H. The Gifts of the Holy Spirit. III. THE WORK OF THE HOLY SPIRIT IN MAN A. The Holy Spirit will convict men of their sin. Nevertheless I tell you the truth; It is expedient for you that I go away: for if I go not away, the Comforter will not come unto you; but if I depart, I will send him unto you. And when he is come, he will reprove the world of sin, and of righteousness, and of judgment: - John 16:7-8 B. The Holy Spirit is active in the new birth. – John 3:5-8 C.
