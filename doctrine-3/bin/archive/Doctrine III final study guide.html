DOCTRINE III-FINAL STUDY GUIDE

 

1. Define Theology.

 

  Theology is the study of God.

 

2. If the Scriptures offer no reasoned demonstration of God’s

existence, why do we make the attempt?

 

To convince genuine seekers after God

 

To strengthen the faith of those who already believe

 

To enrich our knowledge of the nature of God

 

3. Give five areas of evidence or arguments of God’s existence.

 

Argument from creation Argument from History

 

Argument from design Argument from universal belief

 

Argument from man’s nature

 

4. Give verbatim Hebrews 11:6.

 

But without faith it is impossible to please him: for he that cometh to God must believe that he is, and that he is a rewarder of them that diligently seek him.

 

5. Give four Scriptural descriptions of God.

God is Spirit God is love

 

God is light God is a consuming fire

 

6. List the three primary names of God.

 

Elohim – God

 

Jehovah – LORD

 

Adonai – Lord

 

7. What name of God does LORD signify, and what is the Hebrew root

with its meaning of this name?

 

Jehovah comes from havah the verb to be

8. List the seven covenant names of God with the meaning of each name.

 

Jehovah-jireh – (the LORD will provide)

Jehovah-rapha – (the LORD that healeth)

 

Jehovah-nissi – (the LORD our banner)

 

Jehovah-shalom – (the LORD our peace)

Jehovah-ra’ah – (the LORD my shepherd)

 

Jehovah-tsidkenu – (the LORD our righteousness)

Jehovah- shammah – (the LORD is there)

 

9. What is the meaning of El Shaddai?

 

Almighty God

 

10. Give Scripture verbatim with reference to show the essence of God.

 

“God is a Spirit: and they that worship Him must worship Him in spirit and in truth.”- John 4:24

 

11. List four non-moral attributes of God with an explanation of each one.

 

Omnipresence – God is everywhere present.

Omniscience - God has perfect and eternal knowledge of all things.

Omnipotence - God is all powerful.

Immutable – God is unchangeable.

 

12. List six moral attributes of God.

 

God is Holy. God is Merciful.

God is Righteous. God is Love.

God is Faithful. God is Good.

 

13. List three Old Testament appearances of Christ.

 

Abraham saw Jehovah

The Angel of the Lord

The fourth man in the fire

 

 

 

14. What is the literal meaning of the word Christ?

 

The anointed one

 

15. List the Biblical names of Jesus.

 

Son of God Son of man

The Word Son of David

Lord Christ

 

16. Give a definition for the word Jesus.

 

The transliteration of the Hebrew word Joshua which means Jehovah is salvation.

 

17. List three personality attributes of the Holy Spirit.

 

A mind

will

feeling

 

18. What personal activities are ascribed to the Holy Spirit?

 

He reveals He intercedes

He teaches He speaks

He bears witness He commands

He testifies

19. Who will the Holy Spirit always exalt?

 

The Holy Spirit will always exalt Jesus.

20. What are three works of the Holy Spirit within a man?

 

The Holy Spirit will convict men of their sin.

The Holy Spirit is active in the new birth.

The Holy Spirit will help in the process of sanctification.
