Doctrine Ill — Study Questions

 

INSTRUCTIONS: Answer the following questions to the best of your ability using whatever means possible: Bible, notes from class, textbook, reference books and other resource materials.

 

1. Does God exist? Give Scripture.

2. Where shall we find evidences of God’s existence?

3. What can be deduced from these areas of reality?

4. If the Scriptures offer no reasoned demonstration of God’s existence, why do we make the attempt?

5. Give a fact(s) in regards to Creation that proves there is a Creator.

6. Give an illustration(s) in regards to design and beauty in the universe that proves there is a Designer.

7. Give a thought(s) in regards to human nature that testifies to the fact that God does exist.

8. Give an event(s) in regards to history that declares that God deals in the affairs of men, thus it is actually His Story.

9. Give datum or data in regards to the universal belief of God, or even a god, which reveals that God exists.

10. How can we best explain the Laws of the Universe that men have discovered?

11. Who might attempt to deny the existence of God?

12. What will a little proof do in respect to God’s existence?

13. What does the Bible say about one who denies God’s existence?

14. In theory, who alone is able to support the non-existence of God?

15. Who and what is God? Give the best definition probably ever given apart from Scripture.  

16. Where can we find the best definition?   

17. How can the best definition be formulated?   

18. Are God’s names in Scripture a mere combination of sounds?   

19. List some of the most common Scriptural names of God with their meaning. (Observe the next question before making your list.)  

20. List the seven covenant names of Jehovah, with an interpretation for each.   

21. List five un-Scriptural or erroneous views of God. Briefly summarize each.   

22. Can man comprehend God?   

23. Name the sin of polytheism and three things that sin does.   

24. What are the various types of pantheism and what does each hold to be true?  

25. What is the difference between a monist and a monotheist?   

26. Give a logical refutation of agnosticism.   

27. Give a logical refutation of polytheism.   

28. Give a logical refutation of pantheism.   

29. Give a logical refutation of dualism.   

30. Give a logical refutation of deism.

BONUS: Who made the Maker?
