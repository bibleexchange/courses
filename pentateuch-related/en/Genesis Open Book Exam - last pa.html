(46-50)

 

46.     Q: Was it the will of God for Israel (Jacob) to go down into Egypt?

          A: Yes it was, God had spoken to him for him to go (Genesis 46:3)

 

47.     Q: What was Israel’s request at his time of death?

          A: That he be not buried in the land of Egypt but with his fathers, in their burying  

               place (Genesis 47:30, 31)

 

48:     Q: What peculiar blessing did Jacob give to the sons of Joseph?

          A: He placed his right hand on the younger instead of the elder and blessed as the

               greater (Genesis 48:17-19)

 

49.     Q: What was the special blessing of Judah?

          A: That out of his family the Messiah should come (Genesis 49:10)

 

50.     Q: Where was Jacob buried?

          A: In the cave of Machpelsh (Genesis 50:13)
