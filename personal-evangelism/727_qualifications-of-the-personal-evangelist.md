#Qualifications of the Soul Winner

<h2>He must be a Christian</h2>
<p>Must be born again.&#9;&#9;I Peter 1:23; John 3:3, 5, 7</p>
<p>Must have truth.&#9;&#9;John 8:32; II Peter 1:4-10 (produces faith & love)</p>
<p>Must follow Christ.&#9;&#9;Matt. 4:19</p>

<h2>He must be led by the Spirit.</h2>
<p>Micah 3:8; Zech. 4:6; Mat. 4:1; Rom. 8:14; I Cor. 2:10; Acts 20:22; I Peter 1:22</p>

<h3>To help the worker give the Word.</h3>

<h3>To convict the sinner.</h3>
<p>Acts 8:29 The Spirit said and Philip ran &#8211; swift obedience. Father, Mother&#8230;Has the Spirit told you to go join yourself to your children and teach them the way of Salvation? Young men, Young women&#8230;Where are your friends? Your brothers? Your sisters? Roll call is soon to be taken in Heaven.</p>
<p>Rev. 20:15&#8212;&#8220;And whosoever was not found written in the Book of Life was cast into the lake of fire.&#8221;</p>

<h2>He must be a man of prayer.</h2>
<p>Acts 3:1&#8212;Peter and John went up together into the temple at the hour of prayer&#8230;</p>
<p>Acts 11:5&#8212;I was in the city of Joppa praying&#8230;</p>
<p>Acts 11:12&#8212; And the Spirit bade me go with them, nothing doubting&#8230;</p>

<h3>Pray that God will lead you to the right person.</h3>

<h3>Pray that God will help you give the right words.</h3>

<h3>Pray that God will continue the work. Eph. 11:16-20; Phil. 1:4, 5; Col. 1:3,4</h3>

<h2>He must be concerned for souls.</h2>
<p>&#9; </p>
<p>Have you ever wept over souls? (Romans 9:2) Scotsman John Knox prayed, &#8220;Give me Scotland, or I die!&#8221; &#8220;Souls, Souls, Souls!&#8221; is the Savior&#8217;s cry, Satan&#8217;s cry and the Soul-winner&#8217;s cry. Matt. 23:37; Luke 19:41, 42</p>

<h2>He must have confidence in the power of God&#8217;s Word.</h2>
<p>Isaiah 55:10, 11&#8212; My word&#8230;shall not return unto Me void&#8230; (Ps. 107:20)</p>
<p>Psalm 138:2&#8212;&#8230;Thou hast magnified Thy Word above all Thy name.</p>
<p>Hebrews 4:12&#8212; For the word of God is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any two-edged sword, piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit, and of the joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart.</p>
