<h2>The Preparation of the Personal Worker</h2>
<p>Matthew 4:19&#8212;&#8220;And He saith unto them, follow me and I will make you fishers of men.&#8221;</p>

<h3>Dedication</h3>

<h4> We must be dedicated to the Lord. </h4>
<p>We need complete and absolute devotion to the Lord. Samuel was devoted to every word of God and became a prophet. His cry was &#8220;Speak Lord, thy servant heareth.&#8221;</p>
<p>Samuel woke up to learn the voice of God</p>
<p>Samuel grew up to obey the voice of God</p>
<p>Saul went from a sinner to a servant of God. Saul of Tarsus became PAUL THE GREAT APOSTLE. He asked the question in Acts 9:6, &#8220;Lord, what wilt THOU have me to do.&#8221; Saul made his decision to serve God and God made him a choice vessel to bear His name. An instant&#8217;s decision was turned into a lifetime dedication. I Cor. 6:19, 20 tells us that we are the Lord&#8217;s. Because He bought us with His own blood, It is up to us to surrender and devote our lives to Him.</p>

<h4>Dedicated to the task</h4>
<p>Complete dedication to the work and service of the Lord is the second requirement. Our task and service is outlined in Matt. 28:19, 20: Go ye&#8230;Teach all&#8230;Baptizing&#8230;Teaching them to observe all things that I have&#8230;Lo, I am with you&#8230;</p>
<p>&#9;&#9;&#9;&#9;</p>
<p>Acts 20:19-21&#8212;Serving the Lord with all humility of mind, and with many tears, and temptations, which befell me by the lying in wait of the Jews:20 And how I kept back nothing that was profitable unto you, but have shewed you, and have taught you publickly, and from house to house,21 Testifying both to the Jews, and also to the Greeks, repentance toward God, and faith toward our Lord Jesus Christ.</p>
<p>Acts 8:29 &#8220;Go near and join thyself to this chariot.&#8221; This is a special call to a specific place to meet a specific need in a particular person.</p>
<p>The Apostle Paul&#8217;s lifetime dedication is summed up in 10 charges from II Timothy 4:1-8:</p><br clear=all style='page-break-before:auto;mso-break-type:section-break'><div class="Section1" style="layout-grid:18.0000pt; " >

<h4>Dedicated to the person</h4>
<p>Dedication is an unselfish love and a spiritual desire to help other people. Don&#8217;t be like a salesman, like you&#8217;re adding another soul to the collection. Don&#8217;t be counterfeit, be real. Remember the sad accusation &#8211;&#8220;Your actions speak so loud, I can&#8217;t hear what you are saying.&#8221; </p>
<p>Our demeanor and spirit must be &#8211; Pure &#8211; Genuine &#8211; Righteous. If you want to win them to God, love them. Do not be selfish, but righteous. We must be motivated by genuine empathy. We must be moved by compassion. Matt. 9:36</p>

<h4>The Personal Worker&#8217;s purpose is to present Christ.</h4>
<p>&#8220;Ye are my witnesses saith the Lord.&#8221; Isa. 42:10; Acts 1:8</p>
<p>Remember you are presenting Christ to a sinner so build up in their mind the truth that Christ is the ONLY Savior.</p>

<h5>Two important things in presenting Christ &#8211;</h5>

<h6>The Situation</h6>
<p>First, let&#8217;s look at the Situation (preferably one-on-one). It decreases their boldness when you get them alone. Remember to be very careful in dealing with people, they are all different. When a person you&#8217;re dealing with has friends, or an audience with him, and it is disturbing. Get them alone. Situation is very important!</p>
<p>John 1:41, 42, 43, 45; 4:7; 7:10; 8:10; Luke 19:5; Matt. 9:25; Mark 5:40; Luke 8:54</p>

<h6>The Person</h6>
<p>Second, let&#8217;s look at the Person. It is necessary to know where they are&#8212;spiritually and mentally. Are they are sinner? Have they always been sinner? Are they a backslider?</p>
<p>If they are a backslider, help them to see why they backslid. You&#8217;ll never backslide over one experience, it is a process. If you neglect your Christian experience and things continues to happen, and you don&#8217;t pray enough to pray through, and get rid of those stumbling blocks by using them for stepping stones to lift you higher, you will backslide. Not just one incident or thing will cause anyone to backslide; it is a process of the enemy to destroy you. The devil secretly sets up these stumbling blocks to destroy but God can use them for good.</p>
<p>Strongholds - II Cor 10:4</p>
<p>Snares &#8211; Prov. 13:14; 14:27</p>
<p> Sieve &#8211; Luke 22:31; Amos 9:9</p>
<p>Isaiah 8:13&#8212;Sanctify the LORD of hosts himself; and let him be your fear, and let him be your dread. 14&#8212;And he shall be for a sanctuary; but for a stone of stumbling and for a rock of offence to both the houses of Israel, for a gin and for a snare to the inhabitants of Jerusalem.</p>

<h4>The Personal Worker&#8217;s purpose is to produce a decision for Christ</h4>

<h5>You are after the decision, and not the winning of an argument. Win a soul, not an argument!</h5>

<h5>Three things a successful worker will produce:</h5>
<p>Impression &#8211; must make an impression to gain their interest.</p>
<p>Expression &#8211; must give expression.</p>
<p>Two absolutes in a personal workers life &#8211;</p>
<p>(a)&#9;Word must be &#8211; vitally experienced in the heart</p>
<p>(b)&#9;Word must be &#8211; vitally expressed from the heart</p>
<p>Possession &#8211; get them to possess Christ</p>
<p>Must leave them with something &#8211; Leave them with a Possession:</p>
<p>The Word & Love of God&#224;Truth &#8211; Faith &#8211; Love</p>
<p>Remember &#8211; Impression without expression leaves NO Possession!!!</p>
<p>&#9;Who should do personal work?</p>
<p>&#9;&#9;Witnessing is the main work&#9;&#9;&#9;&#9;</p>
<p>Of the whole Church &#8211;&#9;&#9;&#9;&#9;&#9;</p>
<p>In the whole world &#8211;&#9;&#9;&#9;&#9;&#9;&#9;&#9;For the whole age &#8211;&#9;</p>
<p>To the whole man</p>
<p>Matt. 4:19; 16:24; Acts 1:8</p>
