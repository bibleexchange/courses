The pricing information below is for the A.C.E. core curriculum including math, English, Literature/Creative Writing (Grades 2-5), social studies (history), science, and Word Building (spelling and etymology). Additional literature/resource books are required beginning at level three and are available from A.C.E. These resource sets are termed noncurriculum items and range in price from $15 to $75 per level depending on subjects taken. For planning purposes, please expect to add as much as $75 for resource books, plus your local sales tax and shipping charges. Shipping charges range from 7% to 11% of the order total for US destinations, and a much higher rate for International shipments depending on location.

Individuals purchasing complete courses with answer keys or complete grade levels qualify for a discount of 10 percent or 25 percent respectively on all curriculum items in their orders.

Deeper discount purchase-agreements are available for organizations (schools and institutions) that typically purchase in large volumes.
