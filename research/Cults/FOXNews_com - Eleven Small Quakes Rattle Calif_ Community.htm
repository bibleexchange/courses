Eleven Small Quakes Rattle Calif. Community

Thursday, September 22, 2005

A series of earthquakes (search) rattled a rural community near Bakersfield, Calif. Thursday.

A total of 11 quakes rumbled through Wheeler Ridge (search), Calif., beginning at 4:24 p.m. EDT. Only one was felt in Bakersfield, police said.

No damage has been reported.
