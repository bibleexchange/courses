Charismatic Imitation: Catholic At Heart

Satan’s master-stroke has always been to join and dilute what he cannot stop. The Catholic churches and the Charismatic churches are perfect bedfellows and have been so joined from their beginning. Here is a list of their similarities. We will discuss each item separately.

  • They both fit the model of the Laodicean church in Revelation 3:14-22.
  • Catholics and Charismatics both promote marriage to the world and hate holiness of lifestyle.
  • Adherence to these religious systems is promoted instead of consecration and devotion to the person of Jesus Christ.
  • The emotions manifest in the churches of these two movements are deeply soulical, an almost perfect imitation of the real Spirit-filled life, but lacking in one area, “the love of truth” as represented in a “Sola Scriptura” mindset.
  • Both movements have developed a similar “end times view” of Revelation and the coming judgments. They see themselves as the source of the future kingdom on earth.

What a picture this represents.
