BEWARE OF FALSE DOCTRINE

 

Matthew 16: 6-12

 

Leaven – What we refer to as yeast today.   Causes bread to rise.  Leaven represents influence.  Jesus said in Mt. 13:33 The Kingdom of Heaven is like unto leaven.  In that passage it is a good influence.

 

Christians need to beware of the subtle and destructive influence of false doctrine.
