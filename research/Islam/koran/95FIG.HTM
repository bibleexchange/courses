Book 95


The Fig

In the name of Allah,
the Beneficent,
the Merciful.

[95.1]

I swear by the fig and the olive,

[95.2]

And mount Sinai,

[95.3]

And this city made secure,

[95.4]

Certainly We created man in the best make.

[95.5]

Then We render him the lowest of the low.

[95.6]

Except those who believe and do good,
so they shall have a reward never to be cut off.

[95.7]

Then who can give you the lie after (this) about the judgment?

[95.8]

Is not Allah the best of the Judges?
