Book 112


The Unity

In the name of Allah,
the Beneficent,
the Merciful.

[112.1]

Say:
He,
Allah,
is One.

[112.2]

Allah is He on Whom all depend.

[112.3]

He begets not,
nor is He begotten.

[112.4]

And none is like Him.
