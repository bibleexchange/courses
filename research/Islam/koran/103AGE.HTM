Book 103


The Age

In the name of Allah,
the Beneficent,
the Merciful.

[103.1]

I swear by the time,

[103.2]

Most surely man is in loss,

[103.3]

Except those who believe and do good,
and enjoin on each other truth,
and enjoin on each other patience.
