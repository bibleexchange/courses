Book 106


The Qureaish

In the name of Allah,
the Beneficent,
the Merciful.

[106.1]
For the protection of the Qureaish--

[106.2]

Their protection during their trading caravans in the winter and the summer-- 3 So let them serve the Lord of this House 4 Who feeds them against hunger and gives them security against fear.
