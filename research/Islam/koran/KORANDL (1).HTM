About the Koran

This is an electronic version of The Holy Qur'an, translated by M.H. Shakir and published by Tahrike Tarsile Qur'an, Inc., in 1983. The text was provided by the Online Book Initiative and subsequently marked up at the HTI in SGML. Like all the versions of this text derived from the Online Book Initiative, it is not free from errors. We will strive to correct any errors pointed out to us.

This is the working directory for the Online Book Initiative's "Online Book Repository". As you can see it's still quite raw, but we're working on it.
The OBI is a project to make a large collection of freely redistributable text available in a common format for others to do with as they like.

We deal only with freely redistributable texts. If you have anything to donate to the collection please get in touch with bzs@world.std.com and tell us! If you ever find anything in here that you suspect is not freely redistributable please tell us, that is not our intent!
