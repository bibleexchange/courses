Book 86


The Nightly Visitant

In the name of Allah,
the Beneficent,
the Merciful.

[86.1]

I swear by the heaven and the comer by night;

[86.2]

And what will make you know what the comer by night is?

[86.3]

The star of piercing brightness;

[86.4]

There is not a soul but over it is a keeper.

[86.5]

So let man consider of what he is created:

[86.6]

He is created of water pouring forth,

[86.7]

Coming from between the back and the ribs.

[86.8]

Most surely He is able to return him (to life).

[86.9]

On the day when hidden things shall be made manifest,

[86.10]

He shall have neither strength nor helper.

[86.11]

I swear by the raingiving heavens,

[86.12]

And the earth splitting (with plants);

[86.13]

Most surely it is a decisive word,

[86.14]

And it is no joke.

[86.15]

Surely they will make a scheme,

[86.16]

And I (too) will make a scheme.

[86.17]

So grant the unbelievers a respite:
let them alone for a while.
