Dear Sister Wicker,

Greetings from good old Maine.  My husband said you called last night.  I was in Biddeford.  There was a motor home that Elaine wanted to look at. It is a small one, but all equipped so she could go with the tent etc.

The last thing in the world that I would want to do is cause you any grief.  If only I could get an understanding of what I have sent to your husband through to you.  I know, you probably don't even want to know, but before God, if you only knew.  All it is, Sister Wicker, is an opportunity to get some easy income.  You could do it and earn as much as you do in a part time job, or even a full time one.  The business is so simple.  You simply have brochures, tapes or whatever and share them with people.  People that you don't even know will take a wrapped tape and agree to let you call them to ask what they thought about it.  Of course, not everyone will want any one thing.  But this is something that everyone needs.  Simply stated: a lawyer, prepaid to work for you when you need him.  It covers five areas in life. If anyone needs a lawyer, they don't have to come up with the money up front to get one.  Most people have not even made out a will.  That is part of the plan.  They want anyone to immediately get one.  This is true even if you only have a vehicle.  The seller doesn't have to know much at all.  The "tools" do it for you.  Of course, one has to familiarize himself with the content, however.  They have 3-way calling to answer any questions that a prospect might have.  We are taught not to answer questions, but rather to have a call made, or a tool played for the person or to have a person come and talk to them that has been in it for longer.  It is so easy.  You might not believe it because of words, but in reality, it is.  Just passing out a tool and receiving a phone number so one can follow up is almost the extent of it.  If there are meetings that one can go to in order to have input and encouragement, it is highly recommended.  

Do you realize that if you did this and people understood what they had in front of them, you would get money for them wanting the protection?  If you would allow me to send you the video tape, would you watch it?  It could help you so much in your finances.  It has already brought in hundreds to James and John and over a hundred to me.  Yes, there are "tool" to invest in.  They are so cheap.  It only costs one dollar for the cassettes.  That is so good!   There are things to know about the business and you don't get that all said in a letter if this kind. This is NOT a typical letter.  You are a friend and I know this scares you, and I do understand.  I want to try to help suppress those fears and give it a chance.  What if this worked?  What if this really did change things for your family?  It has to many.  It really has changed things for many!!  I needed extra cash.  I like, as you do, to be able to help my children.  I enjoy that, and I know you do too.  This is going to make me able to do so.  

My husband went to a salesman last night.  He had known him a little bit from a car lot where he was working.  They spent a very minimal amount of time with him and he could immediately see the potential.  He asked for a dozen applications.  He is going to call us and we will sign him and them up.  That will be worth big bucks. I will get $30. prepaid commission on each person that he signs up plus him.  That would mean $390.00 for me, because I don't have 3 managers under me.  If and when I do, some of that would go to them.  That is a part time job paycheck because of one conversation.  That isn't bad.  Of course, that doesn't happen every time.  But the potential is there.  We are excited with it.  We both do it and it is beginning to grow.  I am going to try to find out how far a person is from where you live that is a professional is.  The 3-way calling will do it all for you.  They have special times that thy set aside to explain to any interested prospect.  There are different ways of doing it.  Some are just working with video tapes and they sit in their homes and let it play and then sign the members up.  

I'm going to close.  I hope I haven't said anything to you to make you unhappy.  I only want to help.  This is not a quit your job and go work for Pre-paid.  This is a side job until you build your business big enough to be getting more than you are at your present job.  We went to a meeting to hear a man that got in 4 years ago and he gets 1/4 million dollars each year. It has already made him a millionaire.  Not everyone will get to that status, for sure.  Many are getting to where this brings in more than their jobs.  

I love you, Sister Wicker.  I trust this turns out to be more than you ever dreamed of.  I hope this will help you in ways that you have always wanted the stability of financial status. This would take you off your feet forever.  This could free you up in many ways.  I know my husband said you shared with him about the "anger."  He told me he was going to be praying.  I know you don't want this. This is the enemy and we all know it.  I once experienced it.  God set me free.  Keep believing Him.  He is going to work in your behalf.  I know He is.  I love you.  If you can squeeze in time, drop me a note.

 

 

In Christian love,

 

 

Sister Reynolds
