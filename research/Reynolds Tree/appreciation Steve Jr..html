September 15, 2003

 

 

 

 

 

Dear Stephen Jr.

 

I just wanted to take a moment to let you know that you are appreciated.  The tapes are being done so rapidly.  I appreciate this a lot.  There is a lot that you have to do that no one even knows about.  You are a busy guy.  It is so pleasant to work with you on the platform.  You don't question when I make a judgment call and ask you to open the service up and get it started.  There are things on that platform that I have to have in order to do my part properly.  He puts responsibility in me to do whatever will benefit.  Some have no idea of "working together."  It is so important.  We have to watch your Dad.  Sometimes I am getting instruction from Grandpa to speed it up or whatever, that others may not even hear.   I can't have anyone playing while your Dad is leading cause I am changing and trying to find out what the next step is.  Playing in the background is so different from a bass or drums or guitar.  The whole object, as you know, is to hear what is being said and to encourage worship.  Some just want to play to play.  Thank you for working with me.  It is so hard to fight for what I want.  Just thought maybe you needed to hear this.

 

As for ideas that someone might give you on CAP, if you do it in grayscale and let it be cleared through Sister Bernier before doing it in color, it might save some ink.  Our ideas and hers may be different.  We go with her ideas as she is the principal.  Many times she may have others doing the same thing in order to get an idea of which she would approve of and have for the final thing.  Also, tell me what number ink you take and what you need.  You never have to pay for it out of your family income.  The church will pay for it.  I have an answering service now that I think will work out better than my past.  Put anything on it as you think of it.  I will get it.  (like the number on the box for your ink)   Save the old one and the box.  Some of my contacts call for both.  Elaine is a good contact person to physically give that to.  Tell her to get it to me.  We are together a lot.

Keep up the good work.  I know it is terribly stressful at times.  You are a good example to Joe.  I would like to see him get interested in many of the things that he now lags behind in.  You have more influence on him than even me.  ex. Nursing home ministry, street work, singing with the rest…. Anything you can do would always be appreciated.  He really wants to, I think.  He watches you.  He respects you.  He repeats some of your advice.  He thinks about the remarks you offer.  Thanks!

Grammy
