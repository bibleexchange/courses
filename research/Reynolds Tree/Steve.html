The chore of the front hall was clearly not done Thursday night.  I came in today, Friday, and picked up the small round punch hole paper that someone dropped on the stairs.  The students could be instructed not to carry that around unless it is emptied out each time so as not to leave a trail of these hole-sized papers along their way.  The floor was very untidy as it had dirt etc. and showed that it was badly in need of vacuuming.  I know that someone is on that chore.  Maybe you would want to remind them that if anyone came into the place, it would set a very unpleasant 1st impression, plus we just want God's house to be kept looking really nice.  

 

Also, downstairs there was fruit and veg. on the long tables.  They were in the large garbage bags (that are expensive) that I buy for the big trash can.  I don't know if this is a one time thing or if it is weekly etc.  I don't know where it came from, but it should be dealt with the day it comes in.  The fruit flies were many in number.  I took some home with me.  There was much that could have been made into a fruit salad, if tended to the day it came. Perhaps the cooks weren't even aware of it being there.  I didn't get any call.  If I had I would have called Shannon to see when she was coming in and maybe asked Glenda to cut it up and prepare it for a salad.  I would only have had to buy one thing… Cool Whip ….. and we would have had a FREE nice fruit salad.  Maybe Glenda could be  put in charge of letting Tammy know each time stuff comes in …… or something.  Tammy can let me know if it is something that she can't do.  She just told me yesterday that she had time and wanted me to let her know if she could do something.  The veggie still could be dealt with and cooked up for a stir-fry meal if they do it today.  The rest is gone by, I am sure.  As you know, every free meal is dollars saved.

 

Please don't get the mental impression that you hate to see these papers or listen to me.  I am only trying to keep things running smoothly and I know you have a HUGE amount of responsibility on your shoulders.  I don't know how you can continue to do all that is required of you, never mind try to find time to do physical work.  I will be praying for you, Steve.  We do appreciate all you are doing.  You won't hear it much, but know that your Dad and I see all you are carrying for a load.  The Center would not be running if it were not for your part.  Be CAREFUL, however, to meet the needs of your wife and your body.  You have to get rest!!  You have to have a healthy body.  If I can help in any way, please do not hesitate to let me know.  As I speak, Elaine is teaching Joe how to use the machine in the learning center.  The man that gave us the white tote gave Dad a machine that he was no longer using.  We have it here and it can and will be a great help to the ministry.  Joe needs to get involved and this can be done right in his room.  So, if we can be of help typing or something, please let me know.
