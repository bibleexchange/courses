Given to Jessica by her Dad during a time when she was moving far away from her father and family.  It was sent to her to give her strength and comfort to help in this hard time, to let her know that he was there for her and praying for her.

 

 

May God be with you today.

May the joy of God's goodness be with you today.

May His love be a light that will brighten your way.

May his strength bring you comfort and freedom from care.

And may you always find peace

Just in knowing He's there.

 

Psa. 32:7   Thou art my hiding place, Thou shalt preserve me from trouble: Thou shalt compass me about with songs of deliverance.
