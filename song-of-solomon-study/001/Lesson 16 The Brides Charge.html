-The Bride’s Charge-


I charge you, O ye daughters of Jerusalem, by the roes, and by the hinds of the field, that ye stir not up, nor awake my love, till he please.

Song of Solomon 2:7


The little bride is charging the daughters of Jerusalem


She has been drawn on into some deep experiences with God, We have found her in different chambers, we have found her with a holy desire to know where He is feeding His flock and she wants to be amongst those that are being fed, knowing that as she receives what He is giving—it will strengthen her to run even closer to Him.


Now as she moves on in this banqueting house this is a very close communion and fellowship that she has entered into. And now because she has tasted of the sweetness and the grace and the goodness of her Beloved She does not one thing to disturb that.


So she charges those that are around her not to disturb Him in any way.


  • Not to disturb Him around her


  • Not to disturb within her


Hang on to what God has given you!!!
