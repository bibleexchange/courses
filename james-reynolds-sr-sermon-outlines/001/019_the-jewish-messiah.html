  1. God is One –


When Scripture says “one” it is speaking of a compound unity. It cannot be anything less.


Genesis 1:1 “In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth.”

“God” Elohiym (a plural noun) Gods

“Create” Bara (a singular verb)


Genesis 1:26 “And God said, Let Us make man in Our image, after Our likeness…” (The Chumash)


Deuteronomy 6:4 “Hear, O Israel: Hashem is our God, Hashem is the One and Only.” (The Chumash)


“Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God is one LORD.” (KJV)


“LORD” Yehovah self-existent - eternal

“God” Elohiym (a plural noun)

“Yehovah” is God’s covenant name.


God is one, but Three in One – God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit.
