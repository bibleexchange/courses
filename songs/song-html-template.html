<h1>Song Title</h1>

 

<h2>Verse 1</h2>

<p>

<span class='song-line'>I've been traveling for Jesus so much of my life

</span>

<span class='song-line'>Been traveling o'er land and on sea

</span>

<span class='song-line'>But I'm planning on taking a trip to the sky

</span>

<span class='song-line'>That will be the last move for me

</span>

</p>

 

<h2>Verse 2</h2>

<p>

<span class='song-line'>I've seen wonderful sights as I travelled afar

</span>

<span class='song-line'>How little, how empty they seem

</span>

<span class='song-line'>When I make my last move to that city of gold

</span>

<span class='song-line'>That will be the last move for me

</span>

</p>

 

<h2>Verse 3</h2>

<p>

<span class='song-line'>Here I'm bothered with packing each time that I move

</span>

<span class='song-line'>And carry a load in each hand

</span>

<span class='song-line'>But I'll not need one thing that I've used in this world

</span>

<span class='song-line'>When I move to that heavenly land

</span>

</p>

 

<h2>Verse 4</h2>

<p>

<span class='song-line'>Everything that I'll need will be furnished up there

</span>

<span class='song-line'>Not even my song books I'll bring

</span>

<span class='song-line'>Or the precious old Bible that showed me the way

</span>

<span class='song-line'>I'll not need when I stand by my King

</span>

</p>

 

<h2>Chorus</h2>

<p>

<span class='song-line'>When I move to the sky up in heaven so high

</span>

<span class='song-line'>What a wonderful time that will be

</span>

<span class='song-line'>I'm ready to go washed in Calvary's flow

</span>

<span class='song-line'>That will be the last move for me

</span>

</p>
