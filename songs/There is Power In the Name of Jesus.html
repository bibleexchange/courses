There is Power In the Name of Jesus

 

Verse 1:

 

Mother barely twenty out there on her own,

A teenage boy in prison before he’s even grown,

The illness of a loved one, a widow no one calls,

But there is one solution, there’s an answer for it all.

 

Verse 2:

 

A nation needing mercy, we’re just fightin’ for our lives.

We’re a church needs revival; maybe a broken man and wife.

But in the name of Jesus all those chains of bondage fall,

You see, your prayers are heard and they’re answered, when God’s children call.

 

Chorus:

 

There is power in the name of Jesus;

There is hope, there is strength, and victory to claim.

There is healing in His holy presence;

There is power in His name.
