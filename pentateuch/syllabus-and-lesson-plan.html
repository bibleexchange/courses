Syllabus Silabasi

What is the Pentateuch? Pentatuki ni nini?

The first five books of God's Holy Word which contain the record of the beginning and origin of all things and shadows of all subsequentBiblical revelation. Vitabu vitano vya kwanza vya Neno takatifula Mungu ambalo limeingizwa katika habari ya mwanzo na kwa asili ya vitu vyote na vivuli vya yote yaliyo fuata ufunuo wa kiBiblia.

One of the most diverse and powerful studies of the Bible. Moja ya tofauti sana na masomo yenye nguvu ya Biblia.

Essentially, the Pentateuch is the foundation of all spiritual truth and social principle! Kimsingi,Pentatuki ni msingi wa kweli yote ya kiroho na kanuni ya kijamii!

What does this course involve? Kozi hii inahusisha nini?

You will be reading the textbook and responding to the essays by answering questions. Utakuwa ukisoma kitabu na kuitikia kwa imlailikujibu maswali.

Each section will conclude with Study Questions that are answered"open book" which prepares you for the test that follows. The test is not "openbook" but is taken by memory. Kila sehemu itaishia na kujifunza maswali ambayo ya mejibiwa kwenye Ckitabu kilicho funguliwa Dambacho kinakuandaa kwa ajili ya zoezi litakalo fuata.zoezi sio"kufungua kitabu" balini kuchukua kwa kukariri.

You will be consecutively reading each of the five books of thePentateuch at the same time producing a question and answer from each chapter. Utakuwa kwa mfululizo umesoma kila kitabu cha vitabu vitano vya Pentantuki kwa wakati huo huo na kutoa swali na jibu kutoka kila sura.

You will be studying on your own various topics relating to the Pentateuch; such as: The Feasts of the Lord, and Murmurings in theWilderness. Utakuwa umejifunza kwa mada zako mwenyewe tofauti kuhusiana na Pentatuki;kama vile:sikukuu za Bwana,na manung 9niko jangwani.

You will be memorizing Scripture. Utakuwa ukikariri maandiko.

You will complete an open book Final Exam at the conclusion of this course that will test your understanding of the Pentateuch. Utakamilisha mtihani wa mwisho wa kitabu kilicho funguliwa kwenye hitimisho la kozi hii ambayo itajaribu ufahamu wako wa Pentatuki.

How is this Course Divided? Kozi hii imegawanywa kwa jinsi gani?

This course is divided into five (5) main sections. Each section will conclude with: 1) a special assignment 2) study questions and 3) a test. Kozi hii imegawanywa katikasehemu kuu (5). Kila sehemu itaishia na : 1) kazi maalumu 2) maswali ya kujifunza na 3) zoezi.

Section 1:Genesis Sehemu ya 1:Mwanzo

Section 2:Exodus Sehemu ya 2:kutoka

Section 3:Leviticus Sehemu ya 3:Walawi

Section 4:Numbers Sehemu ya 4:Hesabu

Section 5:Deuteronomy Sehemu ya 5:Kumbukumbu la torati

All assignments in this course are either: Kazi zote katika kozi hii ni mojawapo:

OpenBook kitabu kilicho funguliwa

Resources may be used to answer questions; such as, this textbook, the Bible, commentaries, and etc. OR, Vianzo vyaweza kutumika kujibu maswali; kama vile hiki kitabu, Biblia, vitabu vya maoni, na kadhalika. AU,

ClosedBook Kitabu kufungwa

No resources are to be used. Closed book assignments are by memory only and are designed to test your memory as well as comprehension. Hakuna vianzo vya kutumika.kazi za kitabu kilicho fungwa ni kwa kukariri tu na zibuniwa kupima kumbukumbu yako kama ilivyo katika uwezo wa kufahamu.
