Abraham was named Abram at birth (meaning: father of a multitude.) He was the son of Terah and the founding patriarch of the great Hebrew nation. He lived approximately from1996-1822 B.C. Terah had two other sons, Nahor and Haran. Haran died in Ur of the Chaldees, leaving a son, Lot.
