CANOPYKANOPI/KITAMBA

One possible difference in the world before the flood was water above the earth. During the second creative day the Bible says God separated the waters above the earth from the waters below the earth.Then God said to let there be an expanse in the midst of the waters and let it divide the waters from the waters, and God called the expanse Heaven. This has been interpreted various ways. Some have seen this as referring to a water vapor canopy that enveloped the earth. This would allow for rich vegetation as well as long life for the inhabitants of the earth. Before the Flood, the people lived much longer. The longlives of these Patriarchs definitely increase the probability of an accuraterecord of the beginning being preserved through the ages.
