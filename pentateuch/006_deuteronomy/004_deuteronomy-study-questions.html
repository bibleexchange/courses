Section 5: Deuteronomy Study Questions Sehemu 5: Kumbukumbu la torati maswali ya kujifunza

Explain the title of this book. Elezea jina la kitabu hiki.

The name of this book reveals its purpose: "deuter" meaning'second" and "nomos" meaning "law." It is a repetition of the laws of Sinai.(17:18) The title of this book is rendered "copy" in 17:18, and means"duplicate." Jina la kitabu hiki hufunua kusudi lake: "kumbukumbu" maaana yake"ya pili" na "torati" maaana yake "sheria." Ni marudioya sheria ya mlima Sinai.(17:18) Jina la kitabu hiki ni Cnakala Diliorodishwa katika 17:18, na kumaanisha"kunakili."

Why would we say that Deuteronomy is the most integral part of thePentateuch? Kwa nini tunasema kumbukumbu la torati sehemu muhimu sana ya Pentatuki?

Deuteronomy is a most integral part of the Pentateuch seeing that it condenses and continues the great truths of the first four books of theBible Kumbukumbu ni sehemu muhimu sana ya Pentatuki kuonekana kwamba kinatia uzito na kuendeleza kweli kubwa za vitabu viine vya kwanza vya Biblia
