2. The Preeminence of Melchizedek toAaron, 7:4-19

Melchizedek is both king and priestof an eternal order – Aaron was priest of a temporal order.

a. Abram paid tithes of all to Melchizedek. Gen. 14:20

b. Levi was yet in the loins of Abram when he gave a tenth of all. Heb.7:9,10

c. Levi received tithes of their "brethren." 7:5

d. Melchizedek receivedtithes of Abraham, thus declaring Himself to

be greater.7:6,7

d. Melchizedek blessed Abram 7:7 "the less is blessed of the better."

f. Melchizedek"King of Righteousness" lives (7:3,8,16,17,21,24,25,28)

g. Aaron died Num. 20:29

h. Aaron was succeeded by his son Num. 20:25-28

i. Melchizedek'spriesthood is fulfilled in Christ as a man (Heb. 7:11-19,23,24)
