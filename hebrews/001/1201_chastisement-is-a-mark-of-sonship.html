Chastisement, A Mark of Sonship -12:4-11

Disciplines with convictions in your life show evidence of the Lord's love working in you.

  • You must come face to face with sin and overcome sin.
  • You must come face to face with death and obey the Lord.
  • You must come face to face with the Rebuke of the Lord and submit.
  • You must come face to face with chastening and scourging.

    • Chastening and Scourging must be governed by the Lord

    Chastening and Scourging must be understood and received.

  • Soberly
  • Sensibly
  • Spiritually

    • God brings us out of sin and works to keep us from sin.
