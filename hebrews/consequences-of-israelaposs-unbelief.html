Consequences of Israel's Unbelief (3:7-11)

A whole generation of people perished in the wilderness except Joshua and Caleb. This is a warning to everygeneration that rejects the Gospel.

3:8 The wilderness period defined: "Provocation"

10-11 The people provoked God to anger.

3:11 "They shall not enter into My rest."

Luke 13:3 "Except ye repent ye shall all likewise perish."

John 3:5 "Except a man be born again."
