# What the book of Proverbs says about money
# Kitabu cha mithali kinasema nini kuhusu fedha

Money is a very important subject.Fedha ni somo muhimu sana. It may not seem like a spiritual subject, but it can affect our lives in spiritually negative ways if we do not manage it correctly.Inaweza isionekane kama somo la kiroho,lakini linaweza kuathiri maisha yetu ya kiroho katika njia hasi kamahatutazitumia kwa usahihi. Paul told Timothy that an unhealthy love for money is a source of evil (1 Tim. 6:10).Paulo alimuambia Timotheo kwamba shina moja la mabaya ya kila namna ni kupenda fedha (1Tim.6:10). Many marriage problems are because of money.Matatizo mengi ya ndoa yanasababishwa na pesa. Modern advice says not to do business with friends or family because it can cause fighting. Ushauri wa kisasa unasema usifanye biashara na ndugu au rafiki kwasababu inaweza kuleta ugomvi.These problems are not new, and there is some great advice in the book of Proverbs that can help shape our attitudes about wealth and how we spend it.Matatizo haya si mapya,na kuna baadhi ya mashauri katika kitabu cha mithali inayosaidia kutengeneza tabia zetu kwa habari ya utajiri na namna ya kutumia utajiri wetu.

The Proverbs talk about money and other kinds of wealth, such as fields, plentiful harvests, and other possessions. Mithali inazungumza kuhusu pesa na utajiri mwingine wowote kama vile mashamba,mavuno mengi na vingine vyote tunavyovimiliki.This is natural for an agrarian culture where some people traded goods for other goods.Hii ni asili kwaajili ya utamaduni wa kilimo ambapo baadhi ya watu walifanya biashara pamoja.

## Where does money come from?
## Fedha zinatoka wapi?

By reading Proverbs, we can compile a list of what brings prosperity to people:
Kwa kusoma mithali,tunaweza kukusanya orodha ya vitu vinavyoleta mafanikio kwa watu

* Wisdom (8:18, 21)
*Hekima (8:18,21)
* Hard work (10:4; 12:11; 12:27)
* Bidii (10:4;12:11;12:27)
* God (10:22)
* Mungu (10:22)
* Righteousness (13:21–22, 25; 15:6)
* Haki (13:21-22,25;15:6)
* Obedience (13:18)
Utii (13:18)

Reading the above verses, you might draw the conclusion that the only people that are rich are those that love God, are wise, and work hard.Ukisoma mistari hiyo,unaweza kufikia hitimisho kwamba watu watu wenye utajiri ni wale wanaompenda Mungu,wenye hekima na wenye bidii. But then why are many rich people wicked (28:6), foolish (30:22), and lazy? Lakini kwanini watu wengi wenye utajiri ni waovu(28:6),ujinga (30:22) na uvivu?

### God gives some blessings to all men
### Mungu hutoa baadhi ya Baraka kwa watu wote

Both rich and poor are created by God (22:2).Wote walio matajiri na masikini wameumbwa na Mungu(22:2). All people are His creation, and so He loves them all and cares for them all.Watu wote wanatokana na uumbaji wake na hivyo anawapenda wote na anawajali wote. He even cares for the wicked.Pia anawajali hata waovu. Jesus gave an example of this by saying that God sends rain to both the just and the unjust (Matthew 5:45).Yesu alitoa mfano wa msemo huu kwamba Mungu huwanyeshea mvua wenye haki na wasio haki(matayo5:45) .Someone may be blessed physically, but that does not mean that God is pleased with what they are doing.Mtu anaweza kubarikiwa kimwili lakini haimaanishi kuwa Mungu anapendezwa nay ale wanayofanya.

### The benefits of hard work is a universal principle
### Faida za kufanya kazi kwa bidii ni kanuni ya wote

We have to remember that Proverbs are not absolute promises, but principles.Tunapaswa kukumbuka kwamba mithali siyo ahadi kabisa, lakini ni kanuni. It's not an automatic guarantee that if you get up early every day and work hard that you will be rich, but the chances of you getting rich while lying in bed are very slim.Siyo dhamana ya moja kwa moja kwamba ukiamka mapema kila siku na kufanya kazi kwa bidii kwamba utakuwa tajiri,lakini nafasi za wewe kuwa tajiri wakati ukiwa umelala kitandani ni chache sana. God gives blessings to the righteous, but anyone, including a sinner, can benefit from the advice to work hard.Mungu hutoa utajiri kwa wenye haki,lakini yeyote hata mwenye dhambi anaweza kufaidika katika ushauri wa kufanya kwa bidii. Following are some basic principles about work that apply to everyone:Zifuatazo ni kanuni za msingi kuhusu kazi zinazomhusu kila mmoja.

* If you do not plant seeds, then you will not have a harvest (12:11; 28:19).
*Usipootesha mbegu basi hutavuna(12:11;28:19)
* Some people only talk about what they want to do, but talk or ideas alone wont make you rich. You have to put actions to those words (14:23).
*Baadhi ya watu huzungumzia tu kuhusu kile wanachotaka kufanya,lakini mazungumzo au mawazo pekee havitakupa utajiri.Unatakiwa kuwa na vitendo katika maneno hayo(14:23)
* If you stay in bed all day, your fields won't be plowed, and neither will anyone pay you to sleep. If you want to get paid, you have to get out of bed (20:13).
*Kama utakaa kitandani siku nzima,shamba lako halitalimwa,hakuna yeyote atakayekulipa.Kama unataka kulipwa,unatakiwa utoke kitandani(20:13).
* You can start working hard on a project, but unless you continue working on it, it will not produce results (24:30–34).
*Unaweza kuanza kwa kufanya kazi katika mradi Fulani,kama hutaendelea kuufanyia kazi mradi huo hautaleta matokeo(24:30-34)

### Not all blessings are physical
### Sio Baraka zote ni za kimwili

If you are righteous, God will bless you, but it will not always be with money or physical possessions.Kama wewe ni mwenye haki,Mungu atakubariki,lakini si lazima wakati wote Baraka hizo ziwe kwa njia ya pesa au mali zingine. As humans, we often think that money is the most important thing for life, but God knows that there are other blessings that we need more than money.Kama wanadamu,tunafikiria kwamba pesa ndio kitu muhimu katika maisha,lakini Mungu anajua kwamba kuna Baraka zingine ambazo tunahitaji zaidi ya pesa.

In chapter 3, we see a description of Woman Wisdom.Katika sura ya tatu tunaona maelezo ya mwanamke mwenye hekima. Verse 16 says that she holds life in her right hand and riches and honor in her left hand.Mstari wa 16unasema kwamba ana wingi wa siku katika mkono wake wa kuume,utajiri na heshima katika mkono wake wa kushoto. In ancient Hebrew culture, the right hand was more honored than the left hand was honored (Gen. 48:14; Ecc. 10:12; Matt. 25:33)[1].Katika utamaduni wa waebrania wa kale mkono wa kulia uliheshimika kuliko mkono wa kushoto(Gen.48:14;Ecc.10:12;Matt.25:33)[1]. This shows us that wisdom may give riches, but riches are not as important as life is.Hii inatuonyesha kwamba hekima inaweza kutupa utajiri,lakini utajiri sio muhimu zaidi ya maisha. Also, the two verses which immediately precede it (3:15–15) talk about how wisdom is better than gold and silver (See also 8:19 and 16:16). Wealth is not bad, but wisdom is better.Pia,mistari miwili ambayo inafuata (3:15-15)inazungumza kuhusu namna hekima ilivyo muhimu kuliko dhahabu na fedha (Angalia pia 8:19 na 16:16). God gave Solomon wealth, but only because he first asked for wisdom.Mungu alimpa sulemani utajiri,lakini kwasababu tu alimuomba kwanza hekima. Wisdom is not the only thing that is better than wealth.iHekima siyo kwamba ndiyo kituy muhimu kuliko utajiri. Bruce Waltke says, "Wealth is a desirable state and the reward of wisdom (see 3:9–10), not the aim of one’s life"[2]. Bruce Walker anasema,”Utajiri ni hali inayohitajika na malipo ya hekima(Angalia 3:9-10),siyo lengo la maisha ya mtu”[2]. Proverbs gives many things that are better than having money:Mithali  inaonyesha vitu vingi muhimu kuliko kuwa na pesa:

* The lips of knowledge (20:15)
*Midomo ya maarifa(20:15)
* A good name (22:1)
*Jina jema(22:1)

* Being an honest person (19:22)
*Kuwa mtu mwaminifu (19:22)
* A good wife is better (31:10)
*Mwanamke mwema ni bora (31:10)
* Walking in integrity (19:1)
*Kutembea kwa uadilifu (19:1)
* Fear of God (15:16-17; 16:8; 16:19)
*Hofu ya Mungu(15:16-17;16:8;16:19)

## Money does not solve all problems
## Pesa hazitatui matatizo

Why does God say that so many things are more valuable than money?Kwanini Mungu anasema kwamba kuna vitu vingi vya thamani kuliko pesa? It is because there are many things that money cannot buy.Ni kwasababu kuna vitu vingi ambavyo pesa haziwezi kununua. Money gives you a sense of security (10:15), but money will fail you (11:28).Pesa zinakupa maana ya ulinzi , Money cannot save a failing marriage.Pesa haziwezi kuokoa ndoa inayoangamia. Money cannot save you from a cure-less disease. Pesa  haziwezi kukuokoa na ugonjwa usiotibika.And money cannot help you in the afterlife (11:4; 11:28).Na pesa hazitakusaidia katika maisha yajayo(11:4)

### Money cannot buy true friends
###Pesa haziwezi kukupatia marafiki  wa kweli

When you have money, many people will want to be your friend (14:20; 19:4; 19:7), but are these people true friends? Will these same people want to be your friend when the money is gone?Ukiwa na pesa,watu wengi watapenda kujenga urafiki nawewe(14:20;19:4;19:7),Lakini je hawa ni marafiki wa kweli?Je watu hao watahitaji kuwa marafiki zako hata ikiwa huna pesa tena?

### Money does not last forever
### Pesa haziwezi kudumu milele

Proverbs 23:4–5 says that wealth grows wings and flies away (see also 27:23–24). 
Mithali 23:4-5 inasema kwamba utajiri huota mabawa na kupeperuka(Angalia pia 27:23-24).

### Money can bring problems to your life
### Pesa zinaweza kukuletea matatizo katika maisha yako

Proverbs 17:1 suggests that wealth can be the cause of strife in a home. It can also make you proud (28:11) and demanding (18:23).Mithali 17:inasema kwamba utajiri unaweza kuwa chanzo cha ugomvi nyumbani.Pia inaweza kukufanya ujivune(28:11)

## God gives riches so that we might share with others
##Mungu anatupa utajiri ili tushiriki pamoja na wengine

We are commanded to give back to God (3:9-10), but we are also commanded to help the poor.Tumeamriwa kutoa kwa Mungu(3:9-10),Lakini pia tumeamriwa kusaidia masikini. God will provide for the poor (10:3; 15:25; **22:22-23**), and He may use us to do it.Mungu atawapatia masikini (10:3;15:25;**22:22-23**),Na anaweza kututumia sisi kufanya hivyo. If we have something, and we have a neighbor that needs it, we should give it to them (3:27-28). Tukiwa na kitu,na tuna jirani mwenye uhitaji,tunatakiwa tuwasaidie(3:27-28).Jesus repeated this command during the Sermon on the Mount (Matthew 5:42).Yesu alirudia amri hii wakati wa mahubiri mlimani(Matayo 5:42). God gives wealth to certain people so that they would redistribute it to others.Mungu huwapa baadhi ya watu utajiri ili kwamba wagawanye kwa wengine. Those people who are virtuous will share what they have with the poor (29:7; 31:20).Watu walio wemawatagawana vile walivyonavyo na masikini(29:7;31:20). Part of the reason that there is poverty in the world is because of social and economic injustice (13:23).Sehemu ya sababu kwamba kuna umasikini katika dunia ni kwasababu ya udhalimu wa kijamii na kiuvhumi(13:23). Those that give to the poor will be blessed (11:24; 14:21; **19:17**; 22:9; 28:27).Wale wanaowapa masikini watabarikiwa(11:24;14:21;**19:17**;22:9;28:27).

### God will not listen to those who do not help the poor
###Mungu hawasikii wale wasiosaidia masikini

All men are created in the image of God, and when we oppress any person, we insult God (14:31; 17:5).Watu wote wameumbwa kwa sura na mfano wa Mungu,Na kama tukimuonea mtu yeyote ,tunamtukana Mungu(14:31;17:5). In Matthew 25, Jesus tells the story about people who will be punished because they neglected the poor.Katika matayo 25,Yesu anaelezea hadithi ya watu ambao wataadhibiwa kwasababu waliwatelekeza masikini. He said that when people neglect the poor, they are really neglecting Him (Matthew 25:41–46).Anasema kwamba watu wakiwatelekeza masikini,hakika wanamtelekeza yeye(matayo25:41-46). See also 21:13, 28:27, and 29:14.Angalia pia 21:13,28:27 na 29:14. Those that take advantage of the poor will be punished (22:16; 23:10-11).Wale wanaowaonea masikini wataadhibiwa(22:16;23:10-11).

## Some advice about using money
Mashauri machache kuhusu namna ya kutumia pesa

### Save for the difficult times
###Weka akiba kwaajili ya kutumia nyakati ngumu

We need to make plans about how we will spend our finances (21:5).Tunatakiwa tuweke mipango kuhusu namna ya kutumia fedha (21:5). The ant is used as an example of saving for difficult times (6:6–11).Chungu anatumiwa kama mfano wa kuweka akiba kwaajili ya nyakati ngumu(6:6-11) No one has to tell it what to do.hakuna wa kumweleza cha kufanya. It knows that winter is coming, and so it must gather food.Anajua wakati wa baridi unakuna,kwa hiyo anatakiwa kukusanya chakula. The hard-worker does not need a leader, and in fact, he will likely become a leader (12:24).Mfanyakazi bora hahitaji kiongozi,na kwa ukweli,anaweza kuwa kiongozi(12:24). This hard work can keep you from becoming the slave of another.Bidii hii inaweza kukusaidia usiwe mtumwa wa mwingine. Jesus gave several stories of people who were given more authority because of their hard work.Yesu alitoa hadithi kadhaa za watu waliopewa mamlaka kwasababu ya bii zao. See also 10:5.Angalia pia 10:5.

### Getting rich quickly is usually not good
###Kuwa tajiri kwa haraka siku zote siyo kuzuri

Working in something consistent that will bring a moderate reward is better than looking for new ways to not have to work hard (12:11).Kufanya bidii kwaajili ya kitu thabiti cha kuleta malipo au faida ya wastani ni bora kulikokutafuta njia mpya za kufanya usifanye kazi au usitumie nguvu kufanikiwa(12:11). For example, there is a popular business book named _The 4-Hour Work Week_, which tells people that they can do little work and make a lot of money.Kwa mfano,Kuna kitabu maarufu kinachoitwa-Masaa 4 ya kazi kwa wiki_,kinachowaambia watu kwamba wanaweza kufanya kazi ndogo na kupata pesa nyingi. Many times work is difficult, but trying to find an easier way may not be profitable (14:4). See also 13:11, 20:21, 21:5, and 28:20-22.Kazi ya masaa mengi ni ngumu,lakini kujitahidi kutsfuts njis rshisi inaweza isiwe na faida(14:4).Angalia pia 13:11,20:21,21:5 na 28:20-22.
