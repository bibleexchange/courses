### A true friend never abandons you
###Rafiki wa kweli hatakuacha

Proverbs 17:17 says that "A friend loveth at all times, And a brother is born for adversity." There are two ways of interpreting this verse:Mithali17:17 inasema kwamba”Rafiki hupenda siku zote;na ndugu amezaliwa kwa siku ya taabu.Kuna njia mbili za kutafsiri mstari huu:


1. One way says that the friend is better than the brother. Njia ya kwanza inasema rafiki ni bora kuliko ndugu.Bruce Waltke says that in this verse that "The friend is represented as always present, in good times and bad; the relative only in adversity. Bruce Waltke anasema kwamba katika mstari huu”Rafiki anaweza kuoneshwa kama mara zote yupo,katika nyakati nzuri na mbaya;Ndugu ni katika taabu tu."[4]
2. The other way is that the two lines show how both the friend and the brother show their love.Njia nyingine mistari miwili inaonyesha umuhimu wa wote rafiki na ndugu kuonyesha upendo wao. Tremper Longman, III says "The relationship between the cola seems to be one of intensifying and particularizing, but not contrasting.uhusiano kati ya njia zote inaonyesha ni moja ya kuimarisha na kwenda pamoja lakini siyo kutofautiana.That a friend loves all the time would include moments of adversity.Kwamba rafiki anapenda muda wote hata nyakati za tabu."[5]

Either way of interpreting it, the verse talks about how a friend is always there—no matter what.
Ama njia nyingine ya kuitafsiri,mstari unazungumzia kuhusu namna rafiki anapokuwa nawe bila kujali chochote.

Talk is cheap. ## Conclusion
## Hitimisho

We need to work hard at keeping our relationships with our friends strong, because if we offend them, it may be impossible to restore that relationship (**18:19**). The stronger the friendship, the greater the divide when offended. Tunatakiwa kutia bidii katika mahusiano yetu na marafiki zetu,kwasababu kama tukiwakosoa,itakuwa ngumu kudumisha uhusiano huo(**18:19**).This is why some family feuds are so strong, and also why people who were once madly in love are now clamoring for a divorce.Hii ndiyo sababu familia zingine zinakuwa na migogoro mikubwa,na pia kwanini watu ambao mwanzo walikuwa wanapendana sana sasa wanakimbilia talaka. So we should take great care to fix problems with our closest friends when they are small.Kwahiyo tunatakiwa kujitahidi sana kusuluhisha matatizo na rafiki zetu wa karibu matatizo hayo yakiwa bado madogo.
